#### Disasters and accidents

* [2026 Mindanao earthquake](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Mindanao_earthquake "2026 Mindanao earthquake")
  * A magnitude 7.8 earthquake, whose epicenter is located off [Maasim, Sarangani](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maasim%2C_Sarangani "Maasim, Sarangani"), [Philippines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippines "Philippines"), strikes [Mindanao](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mindanao "Mindanao"), hitting as far as [Leyte](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leyte "Leyte"); the provinces of [North Maluku](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Maluku "North Maluku") and [North Sulawesi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Sulawesi "North Sulawesi"), [Indonesia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indonesia "Indonesia"); and [Sabah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sabah "Sabah"), [Malaysia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malaysia "Malaysia"). At least 32 people are killed while 134 others are injured and seven are missing. [(*The Straits Times*)](https://www.straitstimes.com/live-earthquake-hits-southern-philippines-june8) [(*The Guardian*)](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2026/jun/08/philippines-earthquake-mindanao-tsunami-warnings) [(*Malay Mail*)](https://www.malaymail.com/news/malaysia/2026/06/08/tsunami-advisory-issued-for-sabahs-east-coast-after-powerful-mindanao-quake-rattles-region/222903) [(*The Philippine Star*)](https://www.philstar.com/nation/2026/06/08/2533701/general-santos-mindanao-quake-upgraded-magnitude-78)
  * [Tsunami](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tsunami "Tsunami") warning issued by the [Pacific Tsunami Warning Center](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Tsunami_Warning_Center "Pacific Tsunami Warning Center") (PTWC) for parts of the [Pacific](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Islands "Pacific Islands") remains in effect. [(RNZ)](https://www.rnz.co.nz/news/pacific/597554/tsunami-advisories-issued-for-parts-of-pacific-after-7-point-8-earthquake-in-philippines)
  * Agencies in Indonesia, [Japan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan "Japan"), Malaysia, and the Philippines lift their respective warnings after observing small waves. The PTWC cancels its advisory issued for [Guam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guam "Guam") and the [Northern Mariana Islands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Northern_Mariana_Islands "Northern Mariana Islands"); while [Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia "Australia") and [New Zealand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Zealand "New Zealand") cancel their warnings shortly after the issuances. Tsunamis are also recorded in [Palau](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Palau "Palau"). [(*The Straits Times*)](https://www.straitstimes.com/live-earthquake-hits-southern-philippines-june8) [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/6/8/tsunami-warnings-issued-after-8-2-magnitude-earthquake-off-philippines) [(BBC News)](https://www.bbc.com/news/live/c20y4xp43ywt)
  * [Philippine president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_the_Philippines "President of the Philippines") [Bongbong Marcos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bongbong_Marcos "Bongbong Marcos") orders the suspension of classes in affected areas across Mindanao, coinciding with the country's first day of the new school year. #### Politics and elections

* [2026 Armenian parliamentary election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Armenian_parliamentary_election "2026 Armenian parliamentary election")
  * Preliminary results from yesterday's election reveal that the governing [Civil Contract](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Civil_Contract_%28Armenia%29 "Civil Contract (Armenia)") party won 49.8% of votes, translating to a [parliamentary majority](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Parliamentary_majority "Parliamentary majority"), but falling short of the two-thirds majority required to call a [constitutional referendum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constitutional_referendum "Constitutional referendum") for a peace deal with [Azerbaijan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Azerbaijan "Azerbaijan") and the restart of border trade with [Turkey](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turkey "Turkey"). Opposition alliances [Strong Armenia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strong_Armenia "Strong Armenia") and [Armenia Alliance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Armenia_Alliance "Armenia Alliance") entered parliament after winning 23.2% and 9.9% of the vote ​respectively. A fourth party, [Prosperous Armenia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prosperous_Armenia "Prosperous Armenia"), narrowly missed entering parliament after a later tally of [electronic votes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electronic_vote "Electronic vote"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/armenias-ruling-party-leads-parliamentary-vote-with-57-early-results-2026-06-07/)
