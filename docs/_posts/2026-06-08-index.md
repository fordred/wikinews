#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [2026 Iran war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Iran_war "2026 Iran war")
  * [2026 Iranian strikes on Israel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Iranian_strikes_on_Israel "2026 Iranian strikes on Israel")
    * The [Israeli military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel_Defense_Forces "Israel Defense Forces") confirms that the [Israeli Air Force](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli_Air_Force "Israeli Air Force") launched retaliatory strikes against [military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_Republic_of_Iran_Armed_Forces "Islamic Republic of Iran Armed Forces") sites in [western](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Western_Iran "Western Iran") and [central](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Central_Iran "Central Iran") [Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran "Iran"), making it the first time [Israel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel "Israel") launched strikes against Iran since the [April 7](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Iran_war_ceasefire "2026 Iran war ceasefire") [ceasefire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ceasefire "Ceasefire"). The strikes come as Iranian state media reported explosions were heard in [Isfahan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Isfahan "Isfahan"), [Kermanshah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kermanshah "Kermanshah"), [Tabriz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tabriz "Tabriz"), and [Tehran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tehran "Tehran"). [(*The Jerusalem Post*)](https://www.jpost.com/israel-news/defense-news/article-898666) [(Israel National News)](https://www.israelnationalnews.com/news/428279)
  * [2026 Iranian strikes on the Kurdistan Region](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Iranian_strikes_on_the_Kurdistan_Region "2026 Iranian strikes on the Kurdistan Region")
    * The [Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_Revolutionary_Guard_Corps "Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps") says that it has launched strikes in the [Sulaymaniyah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sulaymaniyah_Governorate "Sulaymaniyah Governorate") [province](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Governorates_of_Iraq "Governorates of Iraq") against the Kurdish opposition group headquarters in the [Kurdistan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kurdistan "Kurdistan") [region](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kurdistan_region "Kurdistan region") of [Iraq](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iraq "Iraq"). #### Disasters and accidents

* [2026 Mindanao earthquake](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Mindanao_earthquake "2026 Mindanao earthquake")
  * A magnitude 7.8 earthquake, whose epicenter is located off [Maasim, Sarangani](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maasim%2C_Sarangani "Maasim, Sarangani"), [Philippines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippines "Philippines"), strikes [Mindanao](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mindanao "Mindanao") with damages reported; hitting as far as [Leyte](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leyte "Leyte") and the provinces of [North Maluku](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Maluku "North Maluku") and [North Sulawesi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Sulawesi "North Sulawesi"), [Indonesia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indonesia "Indonesia"). At least one person is killed and four others are injured. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/philippines-earthquake-mindanao-6e489739402863eaf40cbfd30a1b1cc7) [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/business/environment/earthquake-magnitude-73-strikes-mindanao-philippines-gfz-says-2026-06-07/) [(*The Philippine Star*)](https://www.philstar.com/nation/2026/06/08/2533701/general-santos-mindanao-quake-upgraded-magnitude-78)
  * [Tsunami](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tsunami "Tsunami") warnings are issued by the [Pacific Tsunami Warning Center](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Tsunami_Warning_Center "Pacific Tsunami Warning Center") and agencies in the Philippines, Indonesia, and [Japan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan "Japan"); with [Guam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guam "Guam"), [Malaysia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malaysia "Malaysia"), [Papua New Guinea](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Papua_New_Guinea "Papua New Guinea") and [Taiwan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taiwan "Taiwan") also being warned of possible waves. The [Philippine Institute of Volcanology and Seismology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippine_Institute_of_Volcanology_and_Seismology "Philippine Institute of Volcanology and Seismology") orders evacuations in coastal areas of nine provinces; while officials in Indonesia orders evacuations in northern [Gorontalo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gorontalo "Gorontalo"), [Manado](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manado "Manado") in North Sulawesi, and the [Sangihe Islands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sangihe_Islands "Sangihe Islands").
