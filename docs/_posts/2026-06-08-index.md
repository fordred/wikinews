#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [2026 Iran war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Iran_war "2026 Iran war")
  * [2026 Iranian strikes on Israel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Iranian_strikes_on_Israel "2026 Iranian strikes on Israel")
    * The [Israeli military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel_Defense_Forces "Israel Defense Forces") confirms that the [Israeli Air Force](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli_Air_Force "Israeli Air Force") launched retaliatory strikes against [military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_Republic_of_Iran_Armed_Forces "Islamic Republic of Iran Armed Forces") sites in [western](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Western_Iran "Western Iran") and [central](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Central_Iran "Central Iran") [Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran "Iran"), making it the first time [Israel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel "Israel") launched strikes against Iran since the [April 7](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Iran_war_ceasefire "2026 Iran war ceasefire") [ceasefire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ceasefire "Ceasefire"). The strikes come as Iranian state media reported explosions were heard in [Isfahan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Isfahan "Isfahan"), [Kermanshah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kermanshah "Kermanshah"), [Tabriz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tabriz "Tabriz"), and [Tehran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tehran "Tehran"). [(*The Jerusalem Post*)](https://www.jpost.com/israel-news/defense-news/article-898666) [(Israel National News)](https://www.israelnationalnews.com/news/428279)
  * [2026 Iranian strikes on the Kurdistan Region](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Iranian_strikes_on_the_Kurdistan_Region "2026 Iranian strikes on the Kurdistan Region")
    * The [Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_Revolutionary_Guard_Corps "Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps") says that it has launched strikes in the [Sulaymaniyah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sulaymaniyah_Governorate "Sulaymaniyah Governorate") [province](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Governorates_of_Iraq "Governorates of Iraq") against the Kurdish opposition group headquarters in the [Kurdistan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kurdistan "Kurdistan") [region](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kurdistan_region "Kurdistan region") of [Iraq](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iraq "Iraq"). [(AA)](https://www.aa.com.tr/en/middle-east/iran-strikes-headquarters-of-kurdish-opposition-groups-in-northern-iraq-amid-tensions-with-israel/3959671)
  * [2026 Strait of Hormuz crisis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Strait_of_Hormuz_crisis "2026 Strait of Hormuz crisis")
    * [Iran Ambassador](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_current_ambassadors_from_Iran "List of current ambassadors from Iran") to [Russia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia "Russia") [Kazem Jalali](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kazem_Jalali "Kazem Jalali") tells the [Russian newspaper](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_newspapers_in_Russia "List of newspapers in Russia") *[Izvestia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Izvestia "Izvestia")* that the [Strait of Hormuz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strait_of_Hormuz "Strait of Hormuz") will reopen, but with new conditions set by [Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran "Iran") and [Oman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oman "Oman"), including transit fees. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/business/energy/hormuz-strait-will-be-open-with-transit-fees-iran-envoy-moscow-quoted-2026-06-08/)

#### Disasters and accidents

* [2026 Mindanao earthquake](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Mindanao_earthquake "2026 Mindanao earthquake")
  * A magnitude 7.8 earthquake, whose epicenter is located off [Maasim, Sarangani](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maasim%2C_Sarangani "Maasim, Sarangani"), [Philippines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippines "Philippines"), strikes [Mindanao](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mindanao "Mindanao") with damages reported; hitting as far as [Leyte](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leyte "Leyte") and the provinces of [North Maluku](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Maluku "North Maluku") and [North Sulawesi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Sulawesi "North Sulawesi"), [Indonesia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indonesia "Indonesia"). At least 19 people are killed while 134 others and injured. [(BBC News)](https://www.bbc.com/news/live/c20y4xp43ywt) [(AP via ABC News)](https://abcnews.com/International/wireStory/78-magnitude-earthquake-shakes-part-southern-philippines-tsunami-133670776) [(*The Philippine Star*)](https://www.philstar.com/nation/2026/06/08/2533701/general-santos-mindanao-quake-upgraded-magnitude-78)
  * [Tsunami](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tsunami "Tsunami") warnings issued by the [Pacific Tsunami Warning Center](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Tsunami_Warning_Center "Pacific Tsunami Warning Center") (PTWC) and an agency in [Japan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan "Japan") remain in effect; with [Papua New Guinea](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Papua_New_Guinea "Papua New Guinea") and [Taiwan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taiwan "Taiwan") also being warned of possible waves. [(Sky News)](https://news.sky.com/story/tsunami-alert-after-8-2-magnitude-quake-hits-the-philippines-13551811) [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/6/8/tsunami-warnings-issued-after-8-2-magnitude-earthquake-off-philippines)
  * Agencies in Indonesia, [Malaysia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malaysia "Malaysia"), and the Philippines lift their respective warnings after observing small waves. The PTWC cancels its advisory issued for [Guam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guam "Guam") and the [Northern Mariana Islands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Northern_Mariana_Islands "Northern Mariana Islands"); while [Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia "Australia") and [New Zealand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Zealand "New Zealand") cancel their warnings shortly after the issuances. Tsunamis are also recorded in [Palau](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Palau "Palau"). [(*The Straits Times*)](https://www.straitstimes.com/live-earthquake-hits-southern-philippines-june8) [(KUAM News)](https://www.kuam.com/story/298070744/tsunami-advisory-canceled-for-guam-cnmi) [(BBC News)](https://www.bbc.com/news/live/c20y4xp43ywt)
  * Philippine President [Bongbong Marcos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bongbong_Marcos "Bongbong Marcos") orders the suspension of classes in affected areas across Mindanao, coinciding with the country's first day of the new school year.
