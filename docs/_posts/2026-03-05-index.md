#### Politics and elections

* [2026 Kosovan presidential election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Kosovan_presidential_election "2026 Kosovan presidential election")
  * In [Kosovo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kosovo "Kosovo"), 120 members of the [Kuvendi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Assembly_of_the_Republic_of_Kosovo "Assembly of the Republic of Kosovo") are due to [indirectly elect](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indirect_election "Indirect election") the [President](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Kosovo "President of Kosovo"). If no President is elected, the Kuvendi dissolves and new parliamentary elections are set within 45 days.
