#### Disasters and accidents

* [2025 Pacific typhoon season](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Pacific_typhoon_season "2025 Pacific typhoon season")
  * At least two people are killed as [Typhoon Fung-wong](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Typhoon_Fung-wong_%282025%29 "Typhoon Fung-wong (2025)") makes landfall over [Luzon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Luzon "Luzon"), [Philippines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippines "Philippines"). Over 900,000 people are evacuated and rescue operations for victims of the previous [Typhoon Kalmaegi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Typhoon_Kalmaegi "Typhoon Kalmaegi") are suspended. [(BBC News)](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cq509yj82y8o) [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/business/environment/philippines-evacuates-100000-people-fung-wong-intensifies-into-super-typhoon-2025-11-09/)
* At least seven people are killed, 13 rescued, and 280 reported missing, when a boat carrying hundreds of migrants sinks off the [Malaysia–Thailand border](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malaysia%E2%80%93Thailand_border "Malaysia–Thailand border"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/migrant-vessel-sinks-off-malaysia-thailand-border-hundreds-missing-2025-11-09/)

#### Law and crime

* [Ecuadorian security crisis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ecuadorian_security_crisis "Ecuadorian security crisis")
  * Four inmates are killed and 30 others are injured during a [prison riot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prison_riot "Prison riot") in [Machala](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Machala "Machala"), [El Oro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/El_Oro_Province "El Oro Province"), [Ecuador](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ecuador "Ecuador"). [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/ecuador-prison-riot-violence-machala-drug-trafficking-e5eba46aabaad6bd49ad8f6d189c9fce)
  * Following the riot, 27 inmates at the Machala prison are found dead by [asphyxiation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asphyxiation "Asphyxiation"), with investigations ongoing to determine the circumstances of the deaths. [(AFP via Al Arabiya)](https://english.alarabiya.net/News/world/2025/11/10/authorities-say-31-died-in-ecuador-prison-many-asphyxiated)
* [Maldives–Sri Lanka relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maldives%E2%80%93Sri_Lanka_relations "Maldives–Sri Lanka relations")
  * [Sri Lanka](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sri_Lanka "Sri Lanka") [foreign minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ministry_of_Foreign_Affairs_%28Sri_Lanka%29 "Ministry of Foreign Affairs (Sri Lanka)") [Ananda Wijepala](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ananda_Wijepala "Ananda Wijepala") says that [police](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Law_enforcement_in_Sri_Lanka "Law enforcement in Sri Lanka") and [navy officers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sri_Lanka_Navy "Sri Lanka Navy") have been sent to the [Maldives](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maldives "Maldives") to cooperate with local authorities after the [Maldivian military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maldives_National_Defence_Force "Maldives National Defence Force") seized a Sri Lankan ship with over 300 kilograms of [heroin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heroin "Heroin") on Friday. [(Xinhua News)](https://english.news.cn/20251109/f8f4c3e4e4964a4d87187fef6e84ed86/c.html) [(Hiru News)](https://hirunews.lk/goldfmnews/429738/sri-lanka-to-bring-back-suspected-drug-traffickers-and-vessel-from-maldives)
