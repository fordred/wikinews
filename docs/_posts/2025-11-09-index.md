#### Business and economy

* [China–United States trade war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China%E2%80%93United_States_trade_war "China–United States trade war")
  * [China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China "China")'s [Commerce Ministry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ministry_of_Commerce_%28China%29 "Ministry of Commerce (China)") suspends a ban on the export of [gallium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gallium "Gallium"), [germanium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germanium "Germanium"), [antimony](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antimony "Antimony") and other super-hard materials used in the [semiconductor industry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Semiconductor_industry "Semiconductor industry") to the [United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States"). [(CNBC)](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/09/china-suspends-ban-on-exports-of-gallium-germanium-antimony-to-us.html)

#### Disasters and accidents

* [2025 Pacific typhoon season](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Pacific_typhoon_season "2025 Pacific typhoon season")
  * At least two people are killed as [Typhoon Fung-wong](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Typhoon_Fung-wong_%282025%29 "Typhoon Fung-wong (2025)") makes landfall over [Luzon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Luzon "Luzon") in the [Philippines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippines "Philippines"). Over 900,000 people are evacuated and rescue operations for victims of the previous [Typhoon Kalmaegi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Typhoon_Kalmaegi "Typhoon Kalmaegi") are suspended. [(BBC News)](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cq509yj82y8o) [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/business/environment/philippines-evacuates-100000-people-fung-wong-intensifies-into-super-typhoon-2025-11-09/)
* Three people are killed and 15 are injured during [storm surges](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Storm_surge "Storm surge") at various beaches on [Tenerife](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tenerife "Tenerife"), [Canary Islands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canary_Islands "Canary Islands"), [Spain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spain "Spain"). [(The Guardian)](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2025/nov/09/rough-seas-pull-people-into-the-ocean-in-tenerife)
