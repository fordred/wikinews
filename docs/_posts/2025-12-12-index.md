#### Law and crime

* Civil society groups and church representatives file complaints against [Philippine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippine "Philippine") [Vice President](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vice_President_of_the_Philippines "Vice President of the Philippines") [Sara Duterte](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sara_Duterte "Sara Duterte") at the [Office of the Ombudsman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Office_of_the_Ombudsman_%28Philippines%29 "Office of the Ombudsman (Philippines)"), accusing her of [plunder](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plunder "Plunder"), violations of Anti-Graft and Corrupt Practices Act, [bribery](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bribery "Bribery"), and [betrayal of public trust](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Betrayal_of_public_trust_%281987_Philippine_Constitution%29 "Betrayal of public trust (1987 Philippine Constitution)").
