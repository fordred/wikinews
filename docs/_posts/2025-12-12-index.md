#### Disasters and accidents

* [Gaza humanitarian crisis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_humanitarian_crisis "Gaza humanitarian crisis"), [Gaza genocide](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_genocide "Gaza genocide")
  * Fourteen people are killed, including three children who died of [hypothermia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hypothermia "Hypothermia"), amid a severe storm in the [Gaza Strip](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_Strip "Gaza Strip"). At least 13 homes previously damaged by [Israel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel "Israel") have also collapsed. [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2025/12/12/live-at-least-10-people-die-in-gaza-in-past-24-hours-amid-storm)
* At least eight people are killed and multiple others are injured when a [tank truck](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tank_truck "Tank truck") crashes and explodes in [Tiko](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tiko "Tiko"), [Southwest Region](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southwest_Region_%28Cameroon%29 "Southwest Region (Cameroon)"), [Cameroon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cameroon "Cameroon"). [(Africanews)](https://www.africanews.com/2025/12/12/fuel-tanker-explosion-kills-at-least-eight-people-in-cameroon//)
* Four people are killed and eleven others are rescued when an under-construction building on top of a [Hindu temple](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hindu_temple "Hindu temple") collapses in [Verulam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Verulam%2C_KwaZulu-Natal "Verulam, KwaZulu-Natal"), [KwaZulu-Natal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KwaZulu-Natal "KwaZulu-Natal"), [South Africa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Africa "South Africa"). [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/building-collapse-south-africa-temple-verulam-ec650d93dca9d1233363acf888bad859) [(SABC)](https://www.sabcnews.com/sabcnews/1090053-2/)

#### Law and crime

* [Corruption in Bolivia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Corruption_in_Bolivia "Corruption in Bolivia")
  * A [Bolivian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bolivia "Bolivia") judge orders former [president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Bolivia "President of Bolivia") [Luis Arce](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Luis_Arce "Luis Arce") into five months of [pre-trial detention](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pre-trial_detention "Pre-trial detention") in [La Paz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/La_Paz "La Paz") while authorities investigate alleged [embezzlement](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Embezzlement "Embezzlement") linked to the diversion of public funds for projects in [Indigenous communities](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indigenous_peoples_in_Bolivia "Indigenous peoples in Bolivia") during his tenure as [economy minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ministry_of_Economy_%28Bolivia%29 "Ministry of Economy (Bolivia)") [under Evo Morales](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Presidency_of_Evo_Morales "Presidency of Evo Morales"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/americas/bolivia-judge-rules-ex-president-arce-be-held-pre-trial-detention-five-months-2025-12-12/)
* [Corruption in the Philippines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Corruption_in_the_Philippines "Corruption in the Philippines")
  * Civil society groups and church representatives file complaints against [Philippine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippine "Philippine") [vice president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vice_President_of_the_Philippines "Vice President of the Philippines") [Sara Duterte](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sara_Duterte "Sara Duterte") at the [Office of the Ombudsman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Office_of_the_Ombudsman_%28Philippines%29 "Office of the Ombudsman (Philippines)"), accusing her of [plunder](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plunder "Plunder"), violations of Anti-Graft and Corrupt Practices Act, [bribery](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bribery "Bribery"), and [betrayal of public trust](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Betrayal_of_public_trust_%281987_Philippine_Constitution%29 "Betrayal of public trust (1987 Philippine Constitution)"). [(Bloomberg)](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-12-12/philippines-vice-president-duterte-faces-fresh-graft-complaint) [(Rappler)](https://www.rappler.com/philippines/plunder-complaint-sara-duterte-confidential-funds-december-2025/)
* [Police](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Police_Command_of_the_Islamic_Republic_of_Iran "Police Command of the Islamic Republic of Iran") in [Mashhad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mashhad "Mashhad"), [Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran "Iran"), arrest [Nobel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nobel_Prize "Nobel Prize") laureate [Narges Mohammadi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Narges_Mohammadi "Narges Mohammadi") during a ceremony honoring deceased lawyer and human rights activist [Khosrow Alikordi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Khosrow_Alikordi "Khosrow Alikordi").
