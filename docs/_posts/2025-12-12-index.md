#### Disasters and accidents

* Fourteen people are killed, including three children who died of [hypothermia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hypothermia "Hypothermia"), amid a severe storm in the [Gaza Strip](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_Strip "Gaza Strip"). At least 13 homes previously damaged by [Israel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel "Israel") during the [genocide](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_genocide "Gaza genocide") have also collapsed. [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2025/12/12/live-at-least-10-people-die-in-gaza-in-past-24-hours-amid-storm)

#### Law and crime

* Civil society groups and church representatives file a complaints of [plunder](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plunder "Plunder"), violations of Anti-Graft and Corrupt Practices Act, [bribery](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bribery "Bribery"), and [betrayal of public trust](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Betrayal_of_public_trust_%281987_Philippine_Constitution%29 "Betrayal of public trust (1987 Philippine Constitution)") against [Philippine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippine "Philippine") [Vice President](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vice_President_of_the_Philippines "Vice President of the Philippines") [Sara Duterte](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sara_Duterte "Sara Duterte") at the [Office of the Ombudsman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Office_of_the_Ombudsman_%28Philippines%29 "Office of the Ombudsman (Philippines)").
