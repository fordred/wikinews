#### Disasters and accidents

* [Drought in Somalia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Drought_in_Somalia "Drought in Somalia")
  * All [UN agencies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/UN_agencies "UN agencies") warn that the [hunger](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hunger "Hunger") and [malnutrition](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malnutrition "Malnutrition") crisis is rapidly worsening, with a growing risk of [famine in Somalia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Famine_in_Somalia "Famine in Somalia"). More than 6 million people of the 31 per cent of the population facing critical levels of [food insecurity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Food_insecurity "Food insecurity"), while nearly 1.9 million children are suffering from [acute malnutrition](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Acute_malnutrition "Acute malnutrition"). Of those, approximately 493,000 children are facing severe acute malnutrition. [(Relief Web)](https://reliefweb.int/node/4212109) [(UN News)](https://news.un.org/en/story/2026/05/1167516) [(AA)](https://v.aa.com.tr/3938671)
