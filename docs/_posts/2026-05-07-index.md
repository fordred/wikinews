#### Politics and elections

* [Politics of Solomon Islands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Politics_of_Solomon_Islands "Politics of Solomon Islands")
  * The [Parliament](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Parliament_of_Solomon_Islands "National Parliament of Solomon Islands") of the [Solomon Islands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solomon_Islands "Solomon Islands") debates a [no-confidence motion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/No-confidence_motion "No-confidence motion") against incumbent [prime minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_Solomon_Islands "Prime Minister of Solomon Islands") [Jeremiah Manele](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeremiah_Manele "Jeremiah Manele"). The debate is a culmination of a [political crisis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Political_crisis "Political crisis") that started with mass resignations of the cabinet on 16 March, recently escalated by a ruling on 1 May by the [Court of Appeals](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Judiciary_of_Solomon_Islands "Judiciary of Solomon Islands") ordering the reconvening of Parliament.
