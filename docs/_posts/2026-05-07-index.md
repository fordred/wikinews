[(*Times of Israel*)](https://www.timesofisrael.com/liveblog_entry/us-confirms-striking-iran-in-self-defense-says-it-does-not-seek-escalation/) [(CNN)](https://edition.cnn.com/2026/05/07/politics/us-forces-strike-military-facilities-in-iran)
