  * The [United Kingdom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom "United Kingdom") [records](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom_weather_records "United Kingdom weather records") its hottest and sunniest year in 2025 with an average temperature of 10.09 °C (50.16 °F), surpassing the 2022 record of 10.03 °C (50.05 °F) and becoming the second year since 1884 in which the annual mean exceeded 10 °C (50 °F).
