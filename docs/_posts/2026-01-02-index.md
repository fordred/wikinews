#### Law and crime

* [2025–2026 Iranian protests](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025%E2%80%932026_Iranian_protests "2025–2026 Iranian protests")
  * [U.S. President](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_the_United_States "President of the United States") [Donald Trump](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Donald_Trump "Donald Trump") states that if [Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran "Iran") shoots and violently kills peaceful protesters as it has in the past, the United States will come to their rescue. Several protesters have been killed by [security forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Involvement_of_foreign_militias_in_the_suppression_of_protests_in_Iran "Involvement of foreign militias in the suppression of protests in Iran") in these protests, while hundreds were killed in the [2022–2023 protests](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mahsa_Amini_protests "Mahsa Amini protests"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/middle-east/trump-says-us-will-intervene-if-iran-violently-suppresses-peaceful-protests-2026-01-02/)
  * [Ali Shamkhani](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ali_Shamkhani "Ali Shamkhani"), a member of Iran's [Supreme National Security Council](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_National_Security_Council "Supreme National Security Council"), responds by threatening retaliation, stating, "Any intervening hand that approaches Iran’s security will be cut off with a regret-inducing response." Similarly, [Ali Larijani](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ali_Larijani "Ali Larijani"), the current Secretary of the Supreme National Security Council, warns Americans to "watch over their soldiers."
