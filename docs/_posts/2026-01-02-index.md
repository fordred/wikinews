#### Health and environment

* [Climate change in the United Kingdom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Climate_change_in_the_United_Kingdom "Climate change in the United Kingdom")
  * The [United Kingdom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom "United Kingdom") [records](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom_weather_records "United Kingdom weather records") its hottest and sunniest year in 2025 with an average temperature of 10.09 °C, surpassing the 2022 record of 10.03 °C and becoming only the second year since 1884 in which the annual mean exceeds 10 °C.
