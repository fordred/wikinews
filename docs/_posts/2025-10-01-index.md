  * At least six people are killed, including the perpetrator, and 35 others are injured in a [suicide bombing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Suicide_attack "Suicide attack") at a [mosque](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mosque "Mosque") in [Maiduguri](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maiduguri "Maiduguri"), [Borno State](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Borno_State "Borno State"), [Nigeria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nigeria "Nigeria"). No group has claimed responsibility. [(*The Punch*)](https://punchng.com/borno-gov-condemns-bomb-attack-on-mosque-mourns-victims/) [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/blast-hits-mosque-during-evening-prayers-nigerias-maiduguri-witness-2025-12-24/)
* An [explosive device](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Explosive_device "Explosive device") kills two [police officers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moscow_City_Police "Moscow City Police") and one civilian in [Moscow](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moscow "Moscow"), [Russia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia "Russia"), near where Russian lieutenant general [Fanil Sarvarov](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fanil_Sarvarov "Fanil Sarvarov") was killed in a separate bombing two days prior. [(BBC News)](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cz68z3wn1e3o)
