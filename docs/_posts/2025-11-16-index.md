#### Politics and elections

* [2025 Chilean general election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Chilean_general_election "2025 Chilean general election")
  * [Chileans](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chileans "Chileans") vote to elect [their president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Chile "President of Chile"), who will succeed the [term-limited](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Term_limit "Term limit") incumbent [Gabriel Boric](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gabriel_Boric "Gabriel Boric"). [(CNN)](https://www.cnn.com/2025/11/15/americas/chile-presidential-election-explainer-latam-intl)
  * Preliminary results indicate a second round between [Jeannette Jara](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeannette_Jara "Jeannette Jara") and [José Antonio Kast](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jos%C3%A9_Antonio_Kast "José Antonio Kast") on 13 December. [(*El País*)](https://elpais.com/chile/2025-11-16/resultados-de-las-elecciones-presidenciales-en-chile-2025-en-vivo.html)

* [Nuclear program of Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nuclear_program_of_Iran "Nuclear program of Iran")
  * [Iranian Foreign Minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minister_of_Foreign_Affairs_%28Iran%29 "Minister of Foreign Affairs (Iran)") [Abbas Araghchi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abbas_Araghchi "Abbas Araghchi") says that [Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran "Iran") is no longer [enriching uranium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Enriched_uranium "Enriched uranium") due, in part, to the damage at [nuclear facilities](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nuclear_facilities_in_Iran "Nuclear facilities in Iran") following the [Iran–Israel war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran%E2%80%93Israel_war "Iran–Israel war") and the [US strikes on nuclear sites](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_strikes_on_Iranian_nuclear_sites "United States strikes on Iranian nuclear sites") in Iran. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/iran-nuclear-enrichment-c0079c38746a896e2967a9a9cc2799d5)
