#### Politics and elections

* [2025 Chilean general election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Chilean_general_election "2025 Chilean general election")
  * [2025 Chilean presidential election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Chilean_presidential_election "2025 Chilean presidential election")
    * Chileans vote to elect their president, who will succeed the term-limited incumbent [Gabriel Boric](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gabriel_Boric "Gabriel Boric"). [(CNN)](https://www.cnn.com/2025/11/15/americas/chile-presidential-election-explainer-latam-intl)
    * Preliminary results indicate a second round between [Jeannette Jara](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeannette_Jara "Jeannette Jara") and [José Antonio Kast](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jos%C3%A9_Antonio_Kast "José Antonio Kast") on 13 December. [(*El País* in Spanish)](https://elpais.com/chile/2025-11-16/resultados-de-las-elecciones-presidenciales-en-chile-2025-en-vivo.html)
  * Also up for election are the whole [Chamber of Deputies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chamber_of_Deputies_of_Chile "Chamber of Deputies of Chile") and half of the [Senate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Senate_of_Chile "Senate of Chile"). [(Radio ADN in Spanish)](https://www.adnradio.cl/2025/11/16/resultados-elecciones-2025-en-chile-revisa-la-lista-de-todos-los-diputados-y-senadores-ganadores-y-cuando-asumen-sus-cargos/)
* 2025 Ecuadorian referendum [[es](https://es.wikipedia.org/wiki/Refer%C3%A9ndum_constitucional_y_consulta_popular_de_Ecuador_de_2025 "es:Referéndum constitucional y consulta popular de Ecuador de 2025"); [fr](https://fr.wikipedia.org/wiki/R%C3%A9f%C3%A9rendum_constitutionnel_%C3%A9quatorien_de_2025 "fr:Référendum constitutionnel équatorien de 2025")]
  * Ecuadorians vote on a [constitutional](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2008_Constitution_of_Ecuador "2008 Constitution of Ecuador") referendum, on foreign military bases, on public financing of political parties and the number of [assembly members](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Assembly_%28Ecuador%29 "National Assembly (Ecuador)"), and on a popular consultation about the convocation of an [constituent assembly](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constituent_assembly "Constituent assembly"). All 4 were rejected. [(El País in Spanish)](https://elpais.com/america/2025-11-17/el-primer-gran-reves-de-noboa-ecuador-dice-no-a-todas-sus-reformas-constitucionales.html)

* [Nuclear program of Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nuclear_program_of_Iran "Nuclear program of Iran")
  * Iranian foreign minister [Abbas Araghchi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abbas_Araghchi "Abbas Araghchi") says that Iran is no longer [enriching uranium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Enriched_uranium "Enriched uranium") due, in part, to the damage at [nuclear facilities](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nuclear_facilities_in_Iran "Nuclear facilities in Iran") following the [Iran–Israel war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran%E2%80%93Israel_war "Iran–Israel war") and the [US strikes on nuclear sites](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_strikes_on_Iranian_nuclear_sites "United States strikes on Iranian nuclear sites") in Iran. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/iran-nuclear-enrichment-c0079c38746a896e2967a9a9cc2799d5)
