#### Business and economy

* [AI boom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AI_boom "AI boom"), [Economy of South Korea](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Economy_of_South_Korea "Economy of South Korea")
  * [South Korea](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Korea "South Korea") records its highest annual [export](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_exports_of_South_Korea "List of exports of South Korea") value of more than US$700 billion in 2025 after [semiconductor shipments](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Semiconductor_industry_in_South_Korea "Semiconductor industry in South Korea") reach a record $173.4 billion driven by demand for [artificial intelligence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Artificial_intelligence "Artificial intelligence"). [(AFP via France 24)](https://www.france24.com/en/live-news/20260101-ai-chips-boom-sent-south-korea-exports-soaring-in-2025)

  * [Bulgaria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bulgaria "Bulgaria") [adopts](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bulgaria_and_the_euro "Bulgaria and the euro") the [euro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Euro "Euro"), replacing the [lev](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bulgarian_lev "Bulgarian lev"), becoming the 21st member of the [eurozone](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eurozone "Eurozone"). [(AFP via *The Sun*)](https://thesun.my/news/world-news/bulgaria-adopts-euro-as-21st-member-of-eurozone/)
