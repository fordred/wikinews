#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [Israeli–Palestinian conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli%E2%80%93Palestinian_conflict "Israeli–Palestinian conflict")
  * [Israeli settler violence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli_settler_violence "Israeli settler violence")
    * Two [Palestinian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Palestinian "Palestinian") teenagers are killed in separate attacks by [Israeli settlers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli_settlers "Israeli settlers") in [Hebron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hebron "Hebron") and [Beit Furik](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beit_Furik "Beit Furik") respectively, in the occupied [West Bank](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Bank "West Bank"). [(Jordan News)](https://www.jordannews.jo/Section-20/Middle-East/Palestinian-Killed-by-Settler-Gunfire-in-Hebron-Israeli-Forces-Detain-His-Body-46221)

  * The [Integrated Food Security Phase Classification](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Integrated_Food_Security_Phase_Classification "Integrated Food Security Phase Classification") review committee reports that [famine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Famine "Famine") is detected in two [Sudanese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudan "Sudan") regions, [Darfur](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Darfur_Region "Darfur Region") and [South Kordofan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Kordofan "South Kordofan"), amid increased fighting between the [Sudanese Armed Forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudanese_Armed_Forces "Sudanese Armed Forces") and the [Rapid Support Forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rapid_Support_Forces "Rapid Support Forces"), which blocks [humanitarian access](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Humanitarian_access "Humanitarian access"). [(NPR)](https://www.npr.org/2025/11/03/g-s1-96318/famine-spreads-to-two-more-areas-in-sudan-global-hunger-authority-says)
