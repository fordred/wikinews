#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [Israeli–Palestinian conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli%E2%80%93Palestinian_conflict "Israeli–Palestinian conflict")
  * [Israeli settler violence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli_settler_violence "Israeli settler violence")
    * Two [Palestinian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Palestinian "Palestinian") teenagers are killed in separate attacks by [Israeli settlers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli_settlers "Israeli settlers") in [Hebron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hebron "Hebron") and [Beit Furik](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beit_Furik "Beit Furik") respectively, in the occupied [West Bank](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Bank "West Bank"). [(Jordan News Agency)](https://www.jordannews.jo/Section-20/Middle-East/Palestinian-Killed-by-Settler-Gunfire-in-Hebron-Israeli-Forces-Detain-His-Body-46221)
* [Sudanese civil war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudanese_civil_war_%282023%E2%80%93present%29 "Sudanese civil war (2023–present)"), [Famine in Sudan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Famine_in_Sudan_%282024%E2%80%93present%29 "Famine in Sudan (2024–present)")
  * The [Integrated Food Security Phase Classification](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Integrated_Food_Security_Phase_Classification "Integrated Food Security Phase Classification") (IPC) review committee reports that [famine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Famine "Famine") is detected in two [Sudanese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudan "Sudan") regions, [Darfur](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Darfur_Region "Darfur Region") and [South Kordofan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Kordofan "South Kordofan"), amid increased fighting between the [Sudanese Armed Forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudanese_Armed_Forces "Sudanese Armed Forces") and the [Rapid Support Forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rapid_Support_Forces "Rapid Support Forces"), which blocks [humanitarian access](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Humanitarian_access "Humanitarian access"). [(NPR)](https://www.npr.org/2025/11/03/g-s1-96318/famine-spreads-to-two-more-areas-in-sudan-global-hunger-authority-says)

#### Disasters and accidents

* [2025 Shap derailment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Shap_derailment "2025 Shap derailment")
  * A landslide causes a [West Coast Main Line](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Coast_Main_Line "West Coast Main Line") passenger train to derail near [Shap](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shap "Shap"), [Cumbria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cumbria "Cumbria"), [United Kingdom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom "United Kingdom"), injuring four people. [(Sky News)](https://news.sky.com/story/paramedics-sent-to-train-derailment-in-cumbria-as-rail-services-affected-13463052) [(Avanti West Coast)](https://newsdesk.avantiwestcoast.co.uk/news/avanti-west-coast-statement-about-train-derailment-at-shap-cumbria)
* Seven people are killed, five are injured, and four are reported missing when an [avalanche](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Avalanche "Avalanche") strikes a [base camp](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Base_camp "Base camp") on a mountain in [Nepal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nepal "Nepal"). [(*The Free Press Journal*)](https://www.freepressjournal.in/world/7-killed-4-missing-as-avalanche-strikes-nepals-yalung-ri-base-camp) [(CTV News)](https://www.ctvnews.ca/climate-and-environment/article/avalanche-on-nepal-mountain-yalung-ri-kills-7-including-foreign-climbers/)
* A part of the [Torre dei Conti](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Torre_dei_Conti "Torre dei Conti") tower collapses in [Rome](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rome "Rome"), [Italy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italy "Italy"), injuring a worker and leaving another trapped. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/workers-injured-partial-collapse-tower-near-romes-colosseum-2025-11-03/) [(*Corriere della Sera* in Italian)](https://roma.corriere.it/notizie/cronaca/25_novembre_03/roma-crolla-una-parte-della-torre-dei-conti-a-largo-corrado-ricci-persone-bloccate-sotto-le-macerie-8cfa3f17-3f09-464b-a368-120bf4992xlk.shtml)
