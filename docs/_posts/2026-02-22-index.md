#### Politics and elections

* [2026 Laotian parliamentary election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Laotian_parliamentary_election "2026 Laotian parliamentary election")
  * [Laotians](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laotians "Laotians") vote in a parliamentary election to elect the 175 members of the [tenth National Assembly](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Assembly_of_Laos "National Assembly of Laos"). The only legal party in [Laos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laos "Laos") contesting the election is the [Lao People's Revolutionary Party](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lao_People%27s_Revolutionary_Party "Lao People's Revolutionary Party").
