#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [Gaza war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_war "Gaza war")
  * [Hamas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hamas "Hamas") Lieutenant Colonel Ahmed Zamzam is shot and killed by a gunman inside the [Maghazi refugee camp](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maghazi_refugee_camp "Maghazi refugee camp") in the [Deir al-Balah Governorate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deir_al-Balah_Governorate "Deir al-Balah Governorate"). The perpetrator is arrested. [(Palestine Chronicle)](https://www.palestinechronicle.com/gaza-interior-ministry-announces-assassination-of-internal-security-officer/)
* [Israel–Hezbollah conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel%E2%80%93Hezbollah_conflict_%282023%E2%80%93present%29 "Israel–Hezbollah conflict (2023–present)")
  * Three people are killed in three separated [Israeli](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel "Israel") [airstrikes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airstrike "Airstrike") against vehicles in southern [Lebanon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lebanon "Lebanon"). [(L'Orient Today)](https://today.lorientlejour.com/article/1488356/new-israeli-strike-in-southern-lebanon-hits-a-motorcycle-israel-destroys-several-construction-vehicles-in-southern-lebanon-live.html)
* [2025 Bondi Beach shooting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Bondi_Beach_shooting "2025 Bondi Beach shooting")
  * Two gunmen [open fire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mass_shooting "Mass shooting") during a [Hanukkah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hanukkah "Hanukkah") celebration at [Bondi Beach](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bondi_Beach "Bondi Beach") in [Sydney](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sydney "Sydney"), [Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia "Australia"), killing 11 people and leaving 29 others injured. One of the perpetrators is killed, while the other is arrested. [Bombs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Improvised_explosive_device "Improvised explosive device") are reported at the scene. [(News.au)](https://www.news.com.au/national/nsw-act/nsw-police-are-urging-people-to-avoid-bondi-beach/news-story/507c72e792348a04730a3157e69e5cec) [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2025/12/14/bondi-beach-shooting-live-two-in-custody-sydney-police-operation-ongoing)
* [2025 Cambodia–Thailand conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Cambodia%E2%80%93Thailand_conflict "2025 Cambodia–Thailand conflict")
  * [Thailand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thailand "Thailand") confirms that a rocket launched from [Cambodia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cambodia "Cambodia") killed a Thai villager, the first civilian death in Thailand as a result of the ongoing conflict. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/thailand-cambodia-border-conflict-rockets-4b3426dffcf3bf037d307038a23afd3a)
