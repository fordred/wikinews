#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [2025 Bondi Beach shooting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Bondi_Beach_shooting "2025 Bondi Beach shooting")
  * Two gunmen [open fire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mass_shooting "Mass shooting") during a [Hanukkah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hanukkah "Hanukkah") celebration at [Bondi Beach](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bondi_Beach "Bondi Beach") in [Sydney](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sydney "Sydney"), [Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia "Australia"), killing 15 people and leaving 42 others injured. One perpetrator is killed, while the other is arrested. Two [bombs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Improvised_explosive_device "Improvised explosive device") are found at the scene and detonated by police. [(News.au)](https://www.news.com.au/national/nsw-act/news/two-gunmen-open-fire-at-bondi-chanukah-event-12-confirmed-dead-in-mass-shooting/live-coverage/6832a70687161caed3eae0666fe03d86) [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2025/12/14/bondi-beach-shooting-live-two-in-custody-sydney-police-operation-ongoing?update=4173903)
* [2025 Cambodia–Thailand conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Cambodia%E2%80%93Thailand_conflict "2025 Cambodia–Thailand conflict")
  * [Thailand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thailand "Thailand") confirms that a rocket launched from [Cambodia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cambodia "Cambodia") killed a Thai villager. The first civilian death in Thailand as a result of the ongoing conflict. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/thailand-cambodia-border-conflict-rockets-4b3426dffcf3bf037d307038a23afd3a)
