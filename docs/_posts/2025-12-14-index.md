#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [2025 Bondi Beach shooting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Bondi_Beach_shooting "2025 Bondi Beach shooting")
  * Two gunmen [open fire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mass_shooting "Mass shooting") during a [Hanukkah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hanukkah "Hanukkah") celebration at [Bondi Beach](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bondi_Beach "Bondi Beach") in [Sydney](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sydney "Sydney"), [Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia "Australia"), killing 16 people and leaving 40 others injured. One of the perpetrators is killed, while the other is arrested. [Bombs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Improvised_explosive_device "Improvised explosive device") are reported at the scene. [(News.au)](https://www.news.com.au/national/nsw-act/nsw-police-are-urging-people-to-avoid-bondi-beach/news-story/507c72e792348a04730a3157e69e5cec) [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2025/12/14/bondi-beach-shooting-live-two-in-custody-sydney-police-operation-ongoing?update=4173903)
* [2025 Cambodia–Thailand conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Cambodia%E2%80%93Thailand_conflict "2025 Cambodia–Thailand conflict")
