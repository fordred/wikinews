#### Disasters and accidents

* At least six people are killed and 19 others are injured when a [landslide](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Landslide "Landslide") triggered by heavy rain strikes a bus carrying 32 passengers in [Khánh Hòa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kh%C3%A1nh_H%C3%B2a_province "Khánh Hòa province"), [Vietnam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vietnam "Vietnam"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/landslide-kills-6-bus-passengers-central-vietnam-2025-11-17/)

#### Health and environment

* At least 71 schools in [Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia "Australia") and [New Zealand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Zealand "New Zealand") close as authorities investigate [recalled](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Product_recall "Product recall") children's [play sand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sand_art_and_play "Sand art and play") products from [Kmart](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kmart_Australia "Kmart Australia") and [Target](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Target_Corporation "Target Corporation") after testing detected [asbestos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asbestos "Asbestos") in some samples. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/asbestos-contamination-forces-schools-australia-new-zealand-close-2025-11-17/)
