#### Health and environment

* At least 71 schools in [Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia "Australia") and [New Zealand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Zealand "New Zealand") close as authorities investigate [recalled](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Product_recall "Product recall") children's [play sand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hydrophobic_sand "Hydrophobic sand") products from [Kmart](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kmart_Australia "Kmart Australia") and [Target](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Target_Australia "Target Australia") after testing detected [asbestos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asbestos "Asbestos") in some samples.
