#### Disasters and accidents

* At least six people are killed and 19 others are injured when a [landslide](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Landslide "Landslide") triggered by heavy rain strikes a bus carrying 32 passengers in [Khánh Hòa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kh%C3%A1nh_H%C3%B2a_province "Khánh Hòa province"), [Vietnam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vietnam "Vietnam"). #### Health and environment

* At least 71 schools in [Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia "Australia") and [New Zealand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Zealand "New Zealand") close as authorities investigate [recalled](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Product_recall "Product recall") children's [play sand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sand_art_and_play "Sand art and play") products from [Kmart](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kmart_Australia "Kmart Australia") and [Target](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Target_Australia "Target Australia") after testing detected [asbestos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asbestos "Asbestos") in some samples. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/asbestos-contamination-forces-schools-australia-new-zealand-close-2025-11-17/)

#### 42 Indian pilgrims feared dead in bus-tanker collision near Medina in Saudi Arabia

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Portal:Current_events/2025_November_17&action=edit&section=T-1 "Edit section: 42 Indian pilgrims feared dead in bus-tanker collision near Medina in Saudi Arabia")]

At least 42 [Umrah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Umrah "Umrah") [pilgrims](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pilgrimage "Pilgrimage")—many believed to be Indian nationals, including around 16 from [Hyderabad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyderabad "Hyderabad")—were killed when their [bus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bus "Bus") collided with a diesel tanker near [Medina](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Medina "Medina") in [Saudi Arabia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saudi_Arabia "Saudi Arabia"). The group had been returning to Medina after offering prayers in [Mecca](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mecca "Mecca"). External Affairs Minister [S. Jaishankar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/S._Jaishankar "S. Jaishankar") expressed shock and said Indian missions were providing full assistance, while the Indian Embassy in [Jeddah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeddah "Jeddah") set up a 24×7 helpline for families. [Telangana](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Telangana "Telangana") officials, including Chief Minister [Revanth Reddy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Revanth_Reddy "Revanth Reddy"), initiated coordination with central agencies and Saudi authorities, as MP [Asaduddin Owaisi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asaduddin_Owaisi "Asaduddin Owaisi") urged the Centre to ensure swift repatriation of bodies and proper care for the injured.
