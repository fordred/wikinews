#### Disasters and accidents

* At least 42 [Umrah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Umrah "Umrah") [pilgrims](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pilgrimage "Pilgrimage") were killed when their [bus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bus "Bus") collided with a diesel tanker near [Medina](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Medina "Medina") in [Saudi Arabia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saudi_Arabia "Saudi Arabia"). The group had been returning to Medina after offering prayers in [Mecca](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mecca "Mecca"). [(Hindustan Times)](https://www.msn.com/en-in/news/india/42-indian-pilgrims-feared-dead-in-bus-tanker-collision-near-medina-in-saudi-arabia/ar-AA1Qz0Sv?ocid=msedgntp&pc=U531&cvid=5f440525dbad46fbb32bfdc27c50e374&ei=14)
* At least six people are killed and 19 others are injured when a [landslide](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Landslide "Landslide") triggered by heavy rain strikes a bus carrying 32 passengers in [Khánh Hòa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kh%C3%A1nh_H%C3%B2a_province "Khánh Hòa province"), [Vietnam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vietnam "Vietnam"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/landslide-kills-6-bus-passengers-central-vietnam-2025-11-17/)

#### Health and environment

* At least 71 schools in [Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia "Australia") and [New Zealand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Zealand "New Zealand") close as authorities investigate [recalled](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Product_recall "Product recall") children's [play sand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sand_art_and_play "Sand art and play") products from [Kmart](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kmart_Australia "Kmart Australia") and [Target](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Target_Australia "Target Australia") after testing detected [asbestos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asbestos "Asbestos") in some samples. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/asbestos-contamination-forces-schools-australia-new-zealand-close-2025-11-17/)
