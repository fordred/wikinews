#### Politics and elections

* [2026 Liberal Party of Australia leadership spill](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Liberal_Party_of_Australia_leadership_spill "2026 Liberal Party of Australia leadership spill")
  * [Angus Taylor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Angus_Taylor "Angus Taylor") replaces [Sussan Ley](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sussan_Ley "Sussan Ley") as [Leader of the Opposition](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leader_of_the_Opposition_%28Australia%29 "Leader of the Opposition (Australia)") following a leadership spill in the [Liberal Party](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Liberal_Party_of_Australia "Liberal Party of Australia"), symbolizing a power shift from the [Moderate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moderates_%28Liberal_Party_of_Australia%29 "Moderates (Liberal Party of Australia)") to the [National Right](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Right_%28Liberal_Party_of_Australia%29 "National Right (Liberal Party of Australia)") faction. Ley subsequently resigns from politics, triggering a [by-election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Farrer_by-election "2026 Farrer by-election") in her seat of [Farrer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Division_of_Farrer "Division of Farrer").
