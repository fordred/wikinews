#### Politics and elections

* [2026 Bangladeshi general election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Bangladeshi_general_election "2026 Bangladeshi general election")
  * The [Bangladesh Nationalist Party](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bangladesh_Nationalist_Party "Bangladesh Nationalist Party") wins a [landslide victory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Landslide_victory "Landslide victory") in the parliamentary elections, winning 209 seats in the first election since the [July Revolution](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/July_Revolution_%28Bangladesh%29 "July Revolution (Bangladesh)") that toppled former [prime minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_Bangladesh "Prime Minister of Bangladesh") [Sheikh Hasina](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sheikh_Hasina "Sheikh Hasina"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/china/bangladesh-votes-landmark-election-after-gen-z-revolution-2026-02-11/)
* [2026 Liberal Party of Australia leadership spill](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Liberal_Party_of_Australia_leadership_spill "2026 Liberal Party of Australia leadership spill")
  * [Angus Taylor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Angus_Taylor "Angus Taylor") replaces [Sussan Ley](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sussan_Ley "Sussan Ley") as [Leader of the Opposition](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leader_of_the_Opposition_%28Australia%29 "Leader of the Opposition (Australia)") following a leadership spill in the [Liberal Party](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Liberal_Party_of_Australia "Liberal Party of Australia"), symbolizing a power shift from the [Moderate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moderates_%28Liberal_Party_of_Australia%29 "Moderates (Liberal Party of Australia)") to the [National Right](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Right_%28Liberal_Party_of_Australia%29 "National Right (Liberal Party of Australia)") faction. Ley subsequently resigns from politics, triggering a [by-election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Farrer_by-election "2026 Farrer by-election") in her [Farrer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Division_of_Farrer "Division of Farrer") seat.
