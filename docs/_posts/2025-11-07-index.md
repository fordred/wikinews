[(BBC News)](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c70jnx9e414o)

#### International relations

* [Democratic Republic of the Congo–Rwanda relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo%E2%80%93Rwanda_relations "Democratic Republic of the Congo–Rwanda relations")
  * [2025 Democratic Republic of the Congo–Rwanda peace agreement](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo%E2%80%93Rwanda_peace_agreement "2025 Democratic Republic of the Congo–Rwanda peace agreement")
    * The [Democratic Republic of Congo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_Congo "Democratic Republic of Congo") and [Rwanda](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rwanda "Rwanda") state that they have agreed on a Regional Economic Integration Framework in [Washington, D.C.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington%2C_D.C. "Washington, D.C."), [United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States"), to expand economic cooperation and subject to conditions including the withdrawal of [Rwandan forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rwandan_Defence_Force "Rwandan Defence Force") from eastern Congo and operations against the [Democratic Forces for the Liberation of Rwanda](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Democratic_Forces_for_the_Liberation_of_Rwanda "Democratic Forces for the Liberation of Rwanda"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/africa/congo-rwanda-initial-economic-framework-washington-part-peace-process-2025-11-07/)
* [Australia–Nauru relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia%E2%80%93Nauru_relations "Australia–Nauru relations"), [Nauru Regional Processing Centre](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nauru_Regional_Processing_Centre "Nauru Regional Processing Centre")
  * [Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia "Australia") begins to deport hundreds of "non-citizens" to [Nauru](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nauru "Nauru"), as part of a bilateral agreement between both countries, in which Australia will pay Nauru [A$](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australian_dollar "Australian dollar")2.5 billion ([US$](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_dollar "United States dollar")1.6 billion) over 30 years in exchange for Nauru issuing [visas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Visa_policy_of_Nauru "Visa policy of Nauru") to these individuals deported from Australia. [(RNZ)](https://www.rnz.co.nz/international/pacific-news/578139/australia-begins-deporting-non-citizens-to-nauru-under-multimillion-dollar-resettlement-plan)
* [China–Japan relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China%E2%80%93Japan_relations "China–Japan relations"), [Discharge of radioactive water of the Fukushima Daiichi Nuclear Power Plant](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Discharge_of_radioactive_water_of_the_Fukushima_Daiichi_Nuclear_Power_Plant "Discharge of radioactive water of the Fukushima Daiichi Nuclear Power Plant")
  * [Japan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan "Japan") reports that [seafood exports](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Economy_of_Japan "Economy of Japan") to [China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China "China") have resumed for the first time since China imposed a ban in August 2023 over [treated wastewater releases](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Discharge_of_radioactive_water_of_the_Fukushima_Daiichi_Nuclear_Power_Plant "Discharge of radioactive water of the Fukushima Daiichi Nuclear Power Plant") from the [Fukushima Daiichi nuclear plant](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fukushima_Daiichi_Nuclear_Power_Plant "Fukushima Daiichi Nuclear Power Plant"). However, China maintains restrictions on products from [Fukushima](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fukushima_Prefecture "Fukushima Prefecture") and surrounding [prefectures](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prefectures_of_Japan "Prefectures of Japan"). [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/japan-china-seafood-export-fukushima-nuclear-9acffde0a742704428864490a1433317)
* [Hungary–United States relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungary%E2%80%93United_States_relations "Hungary–United States relations")
  * The [United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States") grants [Hungary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungary "Hungary") a one-year exemption from [sanctions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Embargo_of_Russian_oil_during_the_Russo-Ukrainian_war "Embargo of Russian oil during the Russo-Ukrainian war") on [Russian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia "Russia") [oil and gas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Petroleum_industry_in_Russia "Petroleum industry in Russia"), with Hungary agreeing to purchase [U.S. liquefied natural gas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Petroleum_in_the_United_States "Petroleum in the United States"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/trump-meet-hungarys-orban-discuss-russian-oil-economic-cooperation-2025-11-07/)
* [Indonesia–United Kingdom relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indonesia%E2%80%93United_Kingdom_relations "Indonesia–United Kingdom relations"), [Capital punishment in Indonesia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capital_punishment_in_Indonesia "Capital punishment in Indonesia")
  * [Indonesia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indonesia "Indonesia") repatriates [British](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/British_people "British people") nationals [Lindsay Sandiford](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lindsay_Sandiford "Lindsay Sandiford"), sentenced to death for [smuggling](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Drug_trafficking_in_Indonesia "Drug trafficking in Indonesia") [cocaine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cocaine "Cocaine") into [Bali](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bali "Bali") in 2013, and Shahab Shahabadi, who was serving a [life sentence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Life_imprisonment "Life imprisonment") for drug offences, following a bilateral agreement on humanitarian grounds. Both are transferred to the [United Kingdom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom "United Kingdom") to serve the remainder of their sentences under [UK law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Law_of_the_United_Kingdom "Law of the United Kingdom"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/british-woman-death-row-leaves-indonesia-after-repatriation-deal-2025-11-07/)
