#### International relations

* [Australia–Nauru relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia%E2%80%93Nauru_relations "Australia–Nauru relations"), [Nauru Regional Processing Centre](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nauru_Regional_Processing_Centre "Nauru Regional Processing Centre")
  * [Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia "Australia") begins to deport hundreds of "non-citizens" to [Nauru](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nauru "Nauru"), as part of a bilateral agreement between both countries, in which Australia will pay Nauru $2.5 billion over 30 years in exchange for Nauru issuing visas to these individuals deported from Australia.
