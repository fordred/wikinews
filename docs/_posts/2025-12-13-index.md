    * The [Israeli military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli_Defense_Forces "Israeli Defense Forces") kills [Ra'ad Sa'ad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ra%27ad_Sa%27ad "Ra'ad Sa'ad"), a senior [Hamas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hamas "Hamas") [military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Al-Qassam_Brigades "Al-Qassam Brigades") commander, in an airstrike against a vehicle in [Gaza City](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_City "Gaza City"), in [violation of the ceasefire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_peace_plan#Violations_and_deviations "Gaza peace plan"). [(*The Times of Israel*)](https://www.timesofisrael.com/liveblog_entry/top-hamas-commander-raad-saad-confirmed-killed-in-gaza-city-strike-israeli-official-says/) [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2025/12/14/hamas-confirms-killing-of-senior-commander-in-israeli-strike-in-gaza)

[(SANA via Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2025/12/13/joint-syrian-us-patrol-comes-under-attack-near-palmyra-state-media)

    * An RSF drone strike on a [United Nations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations "United Nations") base in [Kadugli](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kadugli "Kadugli"), [South Kordofan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Kordofan "South Kordofan"), Sudan, kills six [Bangladeshi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bangladesh "Bangladesh") [peacekeepers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peacekeeping "Peacekeeping") and injures eight others. * [2025 Cambodia–Thailand conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Cambodia%E2%80%93Thailand_conflict "2025 Cambodia–Thailand conflict")

  * [United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States") [president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_the_United_States "President of the United States") [Donald Trump](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Donald_Trump "Donald Trump") says a new ceasefire has been brokered. However, hours later, [Thai leaders](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Government_of_Thailand "Government of Thailand") announce an increased commitment to fighting [Cambodian forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Cambodian_Armed_Forces "Royal Cambodian Armed Forces").
