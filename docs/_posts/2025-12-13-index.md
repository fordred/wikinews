#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [Gaza war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_war "Gaza war")
  * The [Israeli military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli_Defense_Forces "Israeli Defense Forces") says that they killed [Ra'ad Sa'ad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ra%27ad_Sa%27ad "Ra'ad Sa'ad"), a senior [Hamas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hamas "Hamas") commander, in an apparent [violation of the ceasefire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_peace_plan#Violations_and_deviations "Gaza peace plan"). [(*The Times of Israel*)](https://www.timesofisrael.com/liveblog_entry/top-hamas-commander-raad-saad-confirmed-killed-in-gaza-city-strike-israeli-official-says/)
* [Sudanese civil war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudanese_civil_war_%282023%E2%80%93present%29 "Sudanese civil war (2023–present)")
  * [Kordofan Campaign](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kordofan_Campaign_%282023%E2%80%93present%29 "Kordofan Campaign (2023–present)")
    * A [Rapid Support Forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rapid_Support_Forces "Rapid Support Forces") drone strike kills at least three people in el-Obeid, [Kordofan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kordofan "Kordofan"), [Sudan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudan "Sudan"). [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2025/12/13/rsf-drone-strike-kills-at-least-three-in-central-sudan-injures-several)
* [2025 Cambodia–Thailand conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Cambodia%E2%80%93Thailand_conflict "2025 Cambodia–Thailand conflict")
  * In response to resumed clashes with [Thailand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thailand "Thailand"), [Cambodia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cambodia "Cambodia") shuts down [border](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cambodia%E2%80%93Thailand_border "Cambodia–Thailand border") crossings. [(BBC News)](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cd0kkyx3vvxo)
  * [U.S. president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_the_United_States "President of the United States") [Donald Trump](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Donald_Trump "Donald Trump") says a new ceasefire has been brokered. However, hours later, [Thai](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thailand "Thailand") leaders announce an increased commitment to fighting [Cambodian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Cambodian_Armed_Forces "Royal Cambodian Armed Forces") forces. [(France 24)](https://www.france24.com/en/video/20251213-thailand-vows-to-keep-fighting-cambodia-despite-trump-s-ceasefire-claim)
* [Israel–Hezbollah conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel%E2%80%93Hezbollah_conflict_%282023%E2%80%93present%29 "Israel–Hezbollah conflict (2023–present)")
  * [Israeli Defence Forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli_Defence_Forces "Israeli Defence Forces") issue an evacuation order in southern [Lebanese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lebanon "Lebanon") villages in preparation for planned air strikes on [Hezbollah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hezbollah "Hezbollah") targets. [(*The Jerusalem Post*)](https://www.jpost.com/middle-east/article-880150)
* [Syrian conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syrian_conflict_%282024%E2%80%93present%29 "Syrian conflict (2024–present)")
  * An attacker identified by the [United States Central Command](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Central_Command "United States Central Command") as a member of [ISIL](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_State "Islamic State") opens fire on a joint [Syrian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syrian_Army "Syrian Army") and [American](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Armed_Forces "United States Armed Forces") convoy in [Palmyra](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Palmyra "Palmyra"), killing two US service members and one American civilian interpreter and injuring three others, before being killed by return fire. [(SANA via Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2025/12/13/joint-syrian-us-patrol-comes-under-attack-near-palmyra-state-media)
