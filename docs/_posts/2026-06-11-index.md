#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [2026 Iran war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Iran_war "2026 Iran war")
  * [2026 Strait of Hormuz crisis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Strait_of_Hormuz_crisis "2026 Strait of Hormuz crisis")
    * The [headquarters](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Khatam_al-Anbiya_Central_Headquarters "Khatam al-Anbiya Central Headquarters") of the [Iranian military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_Republic_of_Iran_Armed_Forces "Islamic Republic of Iran Armed Forces") declares that the [Strait of Hormuz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strait_of_Hormuz "Strait of Hormuz") is closed to all traffic, including [oil tankers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oil_tanker "Oil tanker") and commercial ships, due to renewed tensions in [the region](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_East "Middle East") and [U.S.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States") strikes against [Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran "Iran"). [(*The Straits Times*)](https://www.straitstimes.com/world/middle-east/us-military-says-carrying-out-self-defence-strikes-against-iran)
    * The [United States Central Command](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Central_Command "United States Central Command") (CENTCOM) denies [Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran "Iran")'s claims that the Strait of Hormuz has completely closed, insisting that “commercial ships are continuing to transit in and out of" the [waterway](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Waterway "Waterway"). [(*Times of Israel*)](https://www.timesofisrael.com/liveblog_entry/us-central-command-denies-iranian-claim-that-hormuz-shuttered/) [(NBC News)](https://www.nbcnews.com/world/iran/live-blog/us-attacks-iran-rcna349305/rcrd111576?canonicalCard=true)
    * The [Ministry of Oceans and Fisheries](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ministry_of_Oceans_and_Fisheries "Ministry of Oceans and Fisheries") says that a [liquefied natural gas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Liquefied_natural_gas "Liquefied natural gas") (LNG) [carrier](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LNG_carrier "LNG carrier") operated by a Korean shipping company has crossed the Strait of Hormuz and arrived in the port city of [Ulsan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ulsan "Ulsan"), making it the second vessel from [South Korea](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Korea "South Korea") to cross the [waterway](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Waterway "Waterway"). [(Yonhap News Agency)](https://en.yna.co.kr/view/AEN20260610008200320) [(*Korea Herald*)](https://www.koreaherald.com/article/10769077)
  * [Iranian media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mass_media_in_Iran "Mass media in Iran") reports that explosions were heard in [southern Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southern_Iran "Southern Iran") amidst a new wave of U.S. strikes, including [Bandar Abbas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bandar_Abbas "Bandar Abbas") and the [Kargan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kargan%2C_Hormozgan "Kargan, Hormozgan") area of [Minab](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minab "Minab").
