#### Sports

* [Match fixing in tennis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Match_fixing_in_tennis "Match fixing in tennis")
  * The [International Tennis Integrity Agency](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Tennis_Integrity_Agency "International Tennis Integrity Agency") fines [French](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Tennis_Federation "French Tennis Federation") player Quentin Folliot US$70,000 and suspends him from play for 20 years for alleged [match-fixing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Match-fixing "Match-fixing").
