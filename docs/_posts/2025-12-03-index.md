* [Search for Malaysia Airlines Flight 370](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Search_for_Malaysia_Airlines_Flight_370 "Search for Malaysia Airlines Flight 370")
  * [Malaysia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malaysia "Malaysia") announces that the search for the wreckage of [Malaysia Airlines Flight 370](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malaysia_Airlines_Flight_370 "Malaysia Airlines Flight 370") will resume on December 30, with [Ocean Infinity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ocean_Infinity "Ocean Infinity") conducting a 55-day [underwater search](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Underwater_searches "Underwater searches") in a targeted area of the southern [Indian Ocean](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_Ocean "Indian Ocean") under its existing "[no-find, no-fee](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Contingent_fee "Contingent fee")" agreement with [the government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Government_of_Malaysia "Government of Malaysia"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/search-missing-flight-mh370-resume-december-30-malaysia-says-2025-12-03/)
