#### Politics and elections

* [2026 Kazakh constitutional referendum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Kazakh_constitutional_referendum "2026 Kazakh constitutional referendum")
  * [Kazakhs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kazakhs "Kazakhs") vote to determine whether to approve a new [constitution](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constitution_of_Kazakhstan "Constitution of Kazakhstan"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/kazakhs-vote-new-constitution-referendum-sunday-2026-03-13/)
* [2026 North Korean parliamentary election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_North_Korean_parliamentary_election "2026 North Korean parliamentary election")
  * [North Koreans](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Koreans "North Koreans") vote to elect the 687 members of the [15th Supreme People's Assembly](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_People%27s_Assembly "Supreme People's Assembly"). [(The Diplomat)](https://thediplomat.com/2026/03/a-primer-on-north-koreas-upcoming-parliamentary-election/)
* [2026 Republic of the Congo presidential election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Republic_of_the_Congo_presidential_election "2026 Republic of the Congo presidential election")
  * [Congolese people](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Demographics_of_the_Republic_of_the_Congo "Demographics of the Republic of the Congo") vote to elect a [president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_the_Republic_of_Congo "President of the Republic of Congo"), with incumbent [Denis Sassou Nguesso](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Denis_Sassou_Nguesso "Denis Sassou Nguesso") seeking a fifth consecutive term. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/africa/incumbent-sassou-favoured-congo-republic-ballot-amid-rights-concerns-2026-03-13/)
* [2026 Vietnamese legislative election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Vietnamese_legislative_election "2026 Vietnamese legislative election")
  * [Vietnamese people](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vietnamese_people "Vietnamese people") vote to elect the 500 members of the [16th National Assembly](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Assembly_of_Vietnam "National Assembly of Vietnam"). The sole legal party of [Vietnam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vietnam "Vietnam") contesting the election is the [Communist Party of Vietnam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Communist_Party_of_Vietnam "Communist Party of Vietnam"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/communist-party-fields-nearly-93-candidates-vietnam-holds-parliament-elections-2026-03-15/)
