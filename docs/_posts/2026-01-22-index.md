#### Law and crime

* A [regional court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Regional_Trial_Court "Regional Trial Court") in [Tacloban](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tacloban "Tacloban"), [Philippines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippines "Philippines"), convicts journalist [Frenchie Mae Cumpio](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frenchie_Mae_Cumpio "Frenchie Mae Cumpio") and Marielle Domequil, who were arrested in a 2020 raid, of [terrorism financing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Terrorism_financing "Terrorism financing") and sentences them to up to 18 years in prison; but acquits them on [a weapons charge](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Criminal_possession_of_a_weapon "Criminal possession of a weapon"). Press freedom and rights groups condemn the verdict.
