#### Law and crime

* [2025 Cambridgeshire train stabbing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Cambridgeshire_train_stabbing "2025 Cambridgeshire train stabbing")
  * Ten people are injured, including nine in life-threatening conditions, in a [mass stabbing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mass_stabbing "Mass stabbing") attack on a [London North Eastern Railway](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/London_North_Eastern_Railway "London North Eastern Railway") passenger train in [Huntingdon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Huntingdon "Huntingdon"), [Cambridgeshire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cambridgeshire "Cambridgeshire"), [England](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/England "England"). Two suspects are arrested. [(BBC News)](https://www.bbc.com/news/live/cm2zvjx1z14t)

[Police](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hellenic_Police "Hellenic Police") say that it was caused by a long-standing feud between two local families.
