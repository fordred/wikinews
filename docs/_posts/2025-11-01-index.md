#### Authors Award

* Nepal Authors Award 2025
  * International Authors Day
    * The Nepal Authors Award 2025 was a literary awards ceremony held on 1 November 2025 at the Hotel Yellow Pagoda in Kantipath, Kathmandu, Nepal. The event was organized by Infinity Services Pvt. Ltd. to celebrate achievements in literature and to provide a platform for both emerging and established authors from Nepal and abroad.

he 2025 ceremony recognized 40 authors across more than twenty categories, including awards such as Best Debut Author, Outstanding Fiction Author, Green Humanities Award, and Outstanding Contribution to Medical Knowledge. Works nominated spanned diverse genres and included both Nepali and international contributions.

The programme began at 09:00 AM with notable figures such as Shree Brahmanand and Prof. Jitendra Dhoj Khad as chief guests. Distinguished attendees included artists, writers, diplomats, and experts such as Madan Krishna Shrestha, Prakash Sayami, Chandni Joshi, Karna Shakya, General Gaurab Shumsher JB Rana, and Dr. Prakash Regmi.]
