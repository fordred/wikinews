#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [2025 Afghanistan–Pakistan conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Afghanistan%E2%80%93Pakistan_conflict "2025 Afghanistan–Pakistan conflict")
  * The [Pakistani](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pakistan "Pakistan") [government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Government_of_Pakistan "Government of Pakistan") partially reopens the [Torkham border crossing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Torkham_border_crossing "Torkham border crossing") with [Afghanistan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghanistan "Afghanistan") to allow [Afghan refugees](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghan_refugee "Afghan refugee") to flee home, while all remaining restrictions on cross-border movement stay banned, following an agreement to maintain a [ceasefire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ceasefire "Ceasefire"). [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/pakistan-afghanistan-torkham-crossing-81b8dad2d70062c5bf4bdcdc5eee9c00)

#### Arts and culture

* The [Grand Egyptian Museum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grand_Egyptian_Museum "Grand Egyptian Museum") in [Giza](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Giza "Giza"), [Egypt](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Egypt "Egypt"), officially opens to the public following long delays. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/egypt-antiquities-museum-pyramids-tutankhamun-1ead2ce683277e613a3aaebb9944d729)
* [Pope Leo XIV](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Leo_XIV "Pope Leo XIV") of the [Catholic Church](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Catholic_Church "Catholic Church") declares saint [John Henry Newman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Henry_Newman "John Henry Newman") a [Doctor of the Church](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Doctor_of_the_Church "Doctor of the Church"), one of 37, and a co-[patron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Patron_saint "Patron saint") of [Catholic education](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Catholic_school "Catholic school"). [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/vatican-newman-saint-doctor-pope-education-a180cfb0b494119bc5adc5418ef4bb18)

#### Disasters and accidents

* A [landslide](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Landslide "Landslide") triggered by heavy rain kills at least 13 people and leaves an unknown number of people missing in [Rift Valley Province](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rift_Valley_Province "Rift Valley Province"), [Kenya](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kenya "Kenya"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/sustainability/climate-energy/landslide-kills-least-13-western-kenya-2025-11-01/)
* A [stampede](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stampede "Stampede") at a [Hindu temple](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hindu_temple "Hindu temple") in [Srikakulam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Srikakulam "Srikakulam"), [Andhra Pradesh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andhra_Pradesh "Andhra Pradesh"), [India](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/India "India"), kills nine people. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/india/indian-temple-stampede-kills-nine-injures-several-2025-11-01/)
* Three people are killed, two are missing, and two others are injured in an [avalanche](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Avalanche "Avalanche") on the [Vertainspitze](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vertainspitze "Vertainspitze") mountain in the [Ortler Alps](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ortler_Alps "Ortler Alps") in [South Tyrol](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Tyrol "South Tyrol"), [Italy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italy "Italy").
