#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [2026 United States strikes in Venezuela](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_United_States_strikes_in_Venezuela "2026 United States strikes in Venezuela")
  * [United States President](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_the_United_States "President of the United States") [Donald Trump](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Donald_Trump "Donald Trump") reportedly orders [airstrikes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airstrike "Airstrike") on [Venezuela](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Venezuela "Venezuela"). [(The Times)](https://www.thetimes.com/world/latin-america/article/venezuela-attack-explosion-trump-maduro-us-latest-news-52wq9g8mw)

[(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/venezuela-us-explosions-caracas-ca712a67aaefc30b1831f5bf0b50665e) [(CNN)](https://www.cnn.com/2026/01/03/americas/venezuela-explosions-intl-hnk)
  * The [Federal Aviation Administration](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Federal_Aviation_Administration "Federal Aviation Administration") issues a [NOTAM](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/NOTAM "NOTAM") notice prohibiting U.S. aircraft from operating in Venezuelan [airspace](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airspace "Airspace"). [(Chosun)](https://www.chosun.com/english/world-en/2026/01/03/QO7FV6CKGJEB3LRYU7U5MWML3E/)
  * A national [state of emergency](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/State_of_emergency "State of emergency") is declared following the strikes. [(ABC News)](https://www.abc.net.au/news/2026-01-03/venezuela-explosions-low-flying-aircraft/106196656)
  * Venezuelan president [Nicolás Maduro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nicol%C3%A1s_Maduro "Nicolás Maduro") and his wife are captured and flown out of the country. [(BBC News)](https://www.bbc.com/news/live/c5yqygxe41pt)
