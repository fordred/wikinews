#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [2026 United States strikes in Venezuela](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_United_States_strikes_in_Venezuela "2026 United States strikes in Venezuela")
  * [United States President](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_the_United_States "President of the United States") [Donald Trump](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Donald_Trump "Donald Trump") orders [airstrikes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airstrike "Airstrike") on [Venezuela](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Venezuela "Venezuela"). Several explosions and low-flying aircraft are reported in [Caracas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caracas "Caracas"), Venezuela. [(The Times)](https://www.thetimes.com/world/latin-america/article/venezuela-attack-explosion-trump-maduro-us-latest-news-52wq9g8mw) [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/venezuela-us-explosions-caracas-ca712a67aaefc30b1831f5bf0b50665e) [(CNN)](https://www.cnn.com/2026/01/03/americas/venezuela-explosions-intl-hnk)
  * Footage shows [United States Marine Corps](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Marine_Corps "United States Marine Corps") [Bell AH-1Z Vipers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bell_AH-1Z_Viper "Bell AH-1Z Viper") striking [Fort Tiuna](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fort_Tiuna "Fort Tiuna") and [Generalissimo Francisco de Miranda Air Base](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Generalissimo_Francisco_de_Miranda_Air_Base "Generalissimo Francisco de Miranda Air Base") with [Hellfire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AGM-114_Hellfire "AGM-114 Hellfire") [air-to-surface missiles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Air-to-surface_missile "Air-to-surface missile") during the operation. Fort Tiuna is later evacuated. [(El Tiempo)](https://www.eltiempo.com/mundo/venezuela/que-es-el-fuerte-tiuna-el-principal-complejo-militar-de-venezuela-punto-de-ataque-y-explosiones-3521498)
  * A national [state of emergency](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/State_of_emergency "State of emergency") is declared following the strikes. [(ABC News)](https://www.abc.net.au/news/2026-01-03/venezuela-explosions-low-flying-aircraft/106196656)
  * [Venezuelan president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Venezuela "President of Venezuela") [Nicolás Maduro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nicol%C3%A1s_Maduro "Nicolás Maduro") and his wife [Cilia Flores](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cilia_Flores "Cilia Flores") are captured by [U.S. forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Armed_Forces "United States Armed Forces") and flown out of the country. [Venezuelan Vice President](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vice_President_of_Venezuela "Vice President of Venezuela") [Delcy Rodríguez](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delcy_Rodr%C3%ADguez "Delcy Rodríguez") confirms that both Maduro and his wife are missing. [(CBS News)](https://www.cbsnews.com/live-updates/venezuela-us-military-strikes-maduro-trump/) [(BBC News)](https://www.bbc.com/news/live/c5yqygxe41pt)
  * [U.S. Attorney General](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Attorney_General "United States Attorney General") [Pam Bondi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pam_Bondi "Pam Bondi") says [Nicolás Maduro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nicol%C3%A1s_Maduro "Nicolás Maduro") has been indicted in [New York](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_%28state%29 "New York (state)") on drugs and weapons charges. [(BBC News)](https://www.bbc.com/news/live/c5yqygxe41pt)
