#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [Gaza war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_war "Gaza war")
  * [Gaza peace plan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_peace_plan "Gaza peace plan")
    * Hamas announced its [government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_Strip_under_Hamas "Gaza Strip under Hamas") will step down to facilitate the transition of a [technocratic administration](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Committee_for_the_Administration_of_Gaza "National Committee for the Administration of Gaza") in Gaza, as stipulated in the [Gaza peace plan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_peace_plan "Gaza peace plan") of October 2025. In response, the [Board of Peace](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Board_of_Peace "Board of Peace") stated that it will judge the implementation of these steps rather than mere statements. [(*The Jerusalem Post*)](https://www.jpost.com/middle-east/article-901534)
* [Sudanese civil war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudanese_civil_war_%282023%E2%80%93present%29 "Sudanese civil war (2023–present)")
  * [Siege of El Obeid](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Siege_of_El_Obeid "Siege of El Obeid")
    * The [United Nations Human Rights Council](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations_Human_Rights_Council "United Nations Human Rights Council") orders an [inquiry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations_fact-finding_mission "United Nations fact-finding mission") into alleged [human rights](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Human_rights_in_Sudan "Human rights in Sudan") violations around [El-Obeid](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/El-Obeid "El-Obeid"), [North Kordofan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Kordofan "North Kordofan"), [Sudan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudan "Sudan"), warning that civilians face a risk of [mass atrocities](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mass_atrocities "Mass atrocities") as the [Rapid Support Forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rapid_Support_Forces "Rapid Support Forces") encircles the city amid Sudan's [civil war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Civil_war "Civil war"). [(AFP via *Macau Business*)](https://macaubusiness.com/un-rights-council-orders-urgent-inquiry-in-sudans-el-obeid/)
