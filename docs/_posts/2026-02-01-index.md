    * [Pakistani](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pakistan "Pakistan") [military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pakistan_Armed_Forces "Pakistan Armed Forces") and [Balochistan Police](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Balochistan_Police "Balochistan Police") forces announce the end to a 40-hour [counterterrorism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Counterterrorism "Counterterrorism") operation which resulted in the death of 145 [Baloch Liberation Army](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Baloch_Liberation_Army "Baloch Liberation Army") (BLA) insurgents, 18 civilians, and 15 security personnel.
