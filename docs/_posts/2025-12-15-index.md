#### International relations

* [Laos–South Korea relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laos%E2%80%93South_Korea_relations "Laos–South Korea relations")
  * [Laos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laos "Laos") and [South Korea](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Korea "South Korea") agree to upgrade their bilateral relations to a [comprehensive partnership](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Comprehensive_partnership "Comprehensive partnership"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/south-korea-laos-upgrade-relations-comprehensive-partnership-seoul-says-2025-12-15/)
* [Laos–Thailand relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laos%E2%80%93Thailand_relations "Laos–Thailand relations"), [2025 Cambodia–Thailand conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Cambodia%E2%80%93Thailand_conflict "2025 Cambodia–Thailand conflict")
  * [Thailand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thailand "Thailand")'s [military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Thai_Armed_Forces "Royal Thai Armed Forces") stops [fuel shipments](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Energy_in_Thailand "Energy in Thailand") through [its border crossing with Laos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laos%E2%80%93Thailand_border "Laos–Thailand border") in [Chong Mek](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chong_Mek "Chong Mek"), [Ubon Ratchathani province](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ubon_Ratchathani_province "Ubon Ratchathani province"), after [intelligence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Intelligence_Agency_%28Thailand%29 "National Intelligence Agency (Thailand)") indicates that supplies are being diverted to [Cambodian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cambodia "Cambodia") [forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Cambodian_Armed_Forces "Royal Cambodian Armed Forces") amid an ongoing conflict between Cambodia and Thailand. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/thailand-cuts-laos-fuel-route-cambodia-border-conflict-deepens-2025-12-15/)
* [Trinidad and Tobago–Venezuela relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trinidad_and_Tobago%E2%80%93Venezuela_relations "Trinidad and Tobago–Venezuela relations")
  * The [Venezuelan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Venezuela "Venezuela") government accuses [Trinidad and Tobago](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trinidad_and_Tobago "Trinidad and Tobago") of participating in the [United States seizure of the oil tanker *Skipper*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_seizure_of_the_oil_tanker_Skipper "United States seizure of the oil tanker Skipper") and suspends all oil trade agreements and contracts between the two countries. [(*The Guardian*)](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2025/dec/15/trinidad-tobago-us-access-airports-venezuela-tensions-grow)

#### Law and crime

* [2024 anti-tourism protests in Spain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2024_anti-tourism_protests_in_Spain "2024 anti-tourism protests in Spain")
  * The [Spanish government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spanish_government "Spanish government") fines [homestay](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Homestay "Homestay") company [Airbnb](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airbnb "Airbnb") €63.9 million (US$75 million) for advertising unlicensed tourist rentals as protests blaming companies like Airbnb and [Booking.com](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Booking.com "Booking.com") for driving up [property prices](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Real_estate_appraisal "Real estate appraisal") for residents across [the country](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spain "Spain") continue. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/spain-airbnb-fine-overtourism-crackdown-fa2bdc048a63aa5632f2c59373b70b46)
* [Capital punishment in Saudi Arabia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capital_punishment_in_Saudi_Arabia "Capital punishment in Saudi Arabia")
  * [Saudi Arabia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saudi_Arabia "Saudi Arabia") records a new annual high in executions, carrying out 340 deaths to date after [authorities](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Politics_of_Saudi_Arabia "Politics of Saudi Arabia") execute three additional individuals, exceeding the previous record of 338 executions in 2024. [(AFP via CTV News)](https://www.ctvnews.ca/world/article/saudi-sets-new-death-penalty-record-after-executing-340-in-2025-afp-tally/)
* [Terrorism in the United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Terrorism_in_the_United_States "Terrorism in the United States")
  * Five individuals associated with the [far-left](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Left "American Left") Turtle Island Liberation Front are arrested for allegedly plotting a series of bombings across [Southern California](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southern_California "Southern California"), [United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States"), on [New Year's Eve](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Year%27s_Eve "New Year's Eve"). [(BBC News)](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn41gqq8vyko)
* [Hong Kong](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hong_Kong "Hong Kong")'s [High Court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hong_Kong_High_Court "Hong Kong High Court") convicts [pro-democracy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pro-democracy_camp_%28Hong_Kong%29 "Pro-democracy camp (Hong Kong)") activist and media entrepreneur [Jimmy Lai](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jimmy_Lai "Jimmy Lai") of conspiracy to [collude](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Collusion "Collusion") with foreign forces and to publish [seditious](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sedition "Sedition") material under the [national security law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2020_Hong_Kong_national_security_law "2020 Hong Kong national security law"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/china/jimmy-lais-supporters-queue-hong-kong-overnight-verdict-2025-12-14/)
* A [court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Judiciary_of_Russia "Judiciary of Russia") in [Moscow](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moscow "Moscow"), [Russia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia "Russia"), designates [protest music](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Protest_music "Protest music") group [Pussy Riot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pussy_Riot "Pussy Riot") as an [extremist group](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_organizations_designated_as_terrorist_or_extremist_by_Russia "List of organizations designated as terrorist or extremist by Russia"), banning its activities within Russia. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/business/media-telecom/russian-anti-kremlin-punk-band-pussy-riot-designated-an-extremist-organisation-2025-12-15/)
* [Danske Bank](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Danske_Bank "Danske Bank") completes its three-year corporate [probation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Probation "Probation") with the [United States Department of Justice](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Department_of_Justice "United States Department of Justice"), concluding all regulatory proceedings related to suspicious transactions at its former [Estonian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Estonia "Estonia") branch, after pleading guilty to [bank fraud](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bank_fraud "Bank fraud") [conspiracy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Conspiracy "Conspiracy") and forfeiting US$2 billion as part of a 2022 settlement. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/sustainability/danske-bank-completes-us-probation-over-estonia-case-2025-12-15/)
* A judge in the [Dominican Republic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dominican_Republic "Dominican Republic") sentences seven former [government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Government_of_the_Dominican_Republic "Government of the Dominican Republic") officials to over a year in [preventative detention](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Preventative_detention "Preventative detention") in a US$250 million [corruption](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Corruption "Corruption") case involving the [health ministry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ministries_of_the_Dominican_Republic "Ministries of the Dominican Republic").
