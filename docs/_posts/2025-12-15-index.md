#### International relations

* [Laos–South Korea relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laos%E2%80%93South_Korea_relations "Laos–South Korea relations")

  * [Thailand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thailand "Thailand")'s [military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Thai_Armed_Forces "Royal Thai Armed Forces") stops [fuel shipments](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Energy_in_Thailand "Energy in Thailand") through [its border crossing with Laos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laos%E2%80%93Thailand_border "Laos–Thailand border") in [Chong Mek](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chong_Mek "Chong Mek"), [Ubon Ratchathani province](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ubon_Ratchathani_province "Ubon Ratchathani province"), after [intelligence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Intelligence_Agency_%28Thailand%29 "National Intelligence Agency (Thailand)") indicates that supplies are being diverted to [Cambodian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cambodia "Cambodia") [forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Cambodian_Armed_Forces "Royal Cambodian Armed Forces") amid an ongoing conflict between Cambodia and Thailand. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/thailand-cuts-laos-fuel-route-cambodia-border-conflict-deepens-2025-12-15/)
