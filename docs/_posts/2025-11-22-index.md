#### Law and crime

* [2022–2023 Brazilian coup plot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2022%E2%80%932023_Brazilian_coup_plot "2022–2023 Brazilian coup plot"), [Arrest of Jair Bolsonaro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arrest_of_Jair_Bolsonaro "Arrest of Jair Bolsonaro")

[(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/brazil-bolsonaro-prison-arrest-d68ae43b99762c577efe7e6ba20b0542)
