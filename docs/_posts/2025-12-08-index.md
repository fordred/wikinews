#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [Yemeni civil war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yemeni_civil_war_%282014%E2%80%93present%29 "Yemeni civil war (2014–present)")
  * [2025 Hadhramaut offensive](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Hadhramaut_offensive "2025 Hadhramaut offensive")
    * The [Southern Transitional Council](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southern_Transitional_Council "Southern Transitional Council") (STC) captures all eight southern [governorates](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Governorates_of_Yemen "Governorates of Yemen") of [Yemen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yemen "Yemen"), opening possibilities for it to claim [independence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Yemen "South Yemen"). [Saudi Arabia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saudi_Arabia "Saudi Arabia") retires all of its troops from [Aden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aden "Aden"). [(*The Guardian*)](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2025/dec/08/seizure-south-yemen-uae-backed-forces-could-lead-independence-claim)
    * Yemen's [airspace](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airspace "Airspace") briefly closes as a result of the STC offensive. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/yemen-civil-war-saudi-arabia-uae-d29e9a9d3cb60c8c1b009bfcbe1c28c0)
* [2025 Cambodian–Thai border crisis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Cambodian%E2%80%93Thai_border_crisis "2025 Cambodian–Thai border crisis")
  * A [Thai soldier](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Thai_Army "Royal Thai Army") is killed and eight others are injured after [Cambodia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cambodia "Cambodia") shells [Ubon Ratchathani province](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ubon_Ratchathani_province "Ubon Ratchathani province"), [Thailand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thailand "Thailand"). [(*Bangkok Post*)](https://www.bangkokpost.com/thailand/general/3152249/thai-soldiers-killed-injured-as-cambodia-resumes-fires-at-thai-soil)
  * [Thai](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Thai_Air_Force "Royal Thai Air Force") [F-16](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/General_Dynamics_F-16_Fighting_Falcon "General Dynamics F-16 Fighting Falcon") jets carry out [airstrikes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airstrike "Airstrike") on [Cambodian military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Cambodian_Armed_Forces "Royal Cambodian Armed Forces") targets in [Oddar Meanchey province](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oddar_Meanchey_province "Oddar Meanchey province") and [Preah Vihear](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Preah_Vihear "Preah Vihear"), killing four [Cambodian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cambodians "Cambodians") civilians and wounding ten more. [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2025/12/8/thailand-launches-air-raids-along-border-with-cambodia-after-deadly-clashes)
* [Israel–Hezbollah conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel%E2%80%93Hezbollah_conflict_%282023%E2%80%93present%29 "Israel–Hezbollah conflict (2023–present)")
  * [Israel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel "Israel") carries out a wave of [airstrikes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airstrike "Airstrike") in southern [Lebanon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lebanon "Lebanon"), allegedly targeting [Hezbollah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hezbollah "Hezbollah")'s site. [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2025/12/9/israel-launches-new-wave-of-air-attacks-on-lebanon-straining-fragile-truce)
* [Sudanese civil war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudanese_civil_war_%282023%E2%80%93present%29 "Sudanese civil war (2023–present)")
  * The [Rapid Support Forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rapid_Support_Forces "Rapid Support Forces") capture [Heglig](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heglig "Heglig"), [Sudan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudan "Sudan")'s largest [oilfield](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Petroleum_reservoir "Petroleum reservoir"), after the [Sudanese Armed Forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudanese_Armed_Forces "Sudanese Armed Forces") withdraw from the area. [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2025/12/8/rsf-claims-seizure-of-key-heglig-oil-field-in-sudan)
