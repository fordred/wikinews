#### Politics and elections

* [2025 Extremaduran regional election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Extremaduran_regional_election "2025 Extremaduran regional election")
  * In [Extremadura](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Extremadura "Extremadura"), [Spain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spain "Spain"), the ruling [People's Party of Extremadura](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/People%27s_Party_of_Extremadura "People's Party of Extremadura") of [regional president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_the_Regional_Government_of_Extremadura "President of the Regional Government of Extremadura") [María Guardiola](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mar%C3%ADa_Guardiola "María Guardiola") wins the elections for its first time since 2011, while [PSOE Extremadura](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spanish_Socialist_Workers%27_Party_of_Extremadura "Spanish Socialist Workers' Party of Extremadura") reports its worst result ever and the [far-right](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/History_of_the_far-right_in_Spain "History of the far-right in Spain") [Vox](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vox_%28political_party%29 "Vox (political party)") party more than doubles its share of the vote. [(RTVE)](https://www.rtve.es/noticias/20251221/elecciones-extremadura-2025-resultados-cronica/16868985.shtml)
