#### Law and crime

* [Colombian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colombia "Colombia") authorities detain 17 children and nine adults belonging to the [fundamentalist](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jewish_fundamentalism "Jewish fundamentalism") [Jewish](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jewish "Jewish") [cult](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cult "Cult") [Lev Tahor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lev_Tahor "Lev Tahor"), citing concerns about the safety of the children in the group. Five of the children had [American](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States") and [Guatemalan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guatemala "Guatemala") passports, and all 26 members were flown to [New York](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_City "New York City") to await investigations into their welfare. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/colombia-ultra-orthodox-jewish-sect-lev-tahor-45392d6b12f2b8223152f2cb231a7a72)
* Former [European Union](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/European_Union "European Union") [foreign and security policy chief](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/High_Representative_of_the_Union_for_Foreign_Affairs_and_Security_Policy "High Representative of the Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy") [Federica Mogherini](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Federica_Mogherini "Federica Mogherini") and two others are arrested in [Belgium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Belgium "Belgium") amid a [fraud](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Budget_accountability_in_the_European_Union "Budget accountability in the European Union") investigation. [(*The Guardian*)](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2025/dec/02/former-eu-top-diplomat-federica-mogherini-arrest-fraud-investigation)
* A [South Sudanese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Sudan "South Sudan") [Douglas DC-3-65TP](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Douglas_DC-3 "Douglas DC-3") operated by [Samaritan's Purse](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samaritan%27s_Purse "Samaritan's Purse") to distribute humanitarian aid to [Maiwut County](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maiwut_County "Maiwut County") is hijacked in [Juba](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Juba "Juba") by a gunman identified as a resident of the disputed [Abyei](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abyei "Abyei") region, disputed by [Sudan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudan "Sudan") and South Sudan. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/south-sudan-aid-plane-hijacked-gunman-d436a7cb44f3cfb03b2b85710c6f9ea6)
* [Tunisian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tunisia "Tunisia") police arrest former [human rights](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Human_rights_in_Tunisia "Human rights in Tunisia") minister [Ayachi Hammami](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ayachi_Hammami "Ayachi Hammami") to enforce a five-year prison sentence for a [state-security conspiracy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sedition "Sedition") conviction issued amid recent [appeals-court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Appellate_court "Appellate court") rulings that imposed long jail terms on [opposition](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Opposition_%28politics%29 "Opposition (politics)") figures.
