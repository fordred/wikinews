#### Politics and elections

* [Elections in Somalia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elections_in_Somalia "Elections in Somalia")
  * Residents of [Mogadishu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mogadishu "Mogadishu"), [Somalia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Somalia "Somalia"), vote to elect 390 district council members who will select [the city's mayor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mayor_of_Mogadishu "Mayor of Mogadishu"), marking Somalia's first [direct election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Direct_election "Direct election") since [1969](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1969_Somali_parliamentary_election "1969 Somali parliamentary election"), serving as a pilot for planned nationwide polls.
