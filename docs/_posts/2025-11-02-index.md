#### Arts and culture

* The [Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands "Netherlands") agrees to [repatriate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Repatriation_%28cultural_property%29 "Repatriation (cultural property)") an [Eighteenth Dynasty](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eighteenth_Dynasty_of_Egypt "Eighteenth Dynasty of Egypt") statue from the reign of [Pharaoh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pharaoh "Pharaoh") [Thutmose III](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thutmose_III "Thutmose III") to [Egypt](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Egypt "Egypt") after investigators determine it was illegally taken from the country and later found at an [art fair](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arts_festival "Arts festival"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/africa/netherlands-return-3500-year-old-sculpture-egypt-2025-11-02/)

#### International relations

* [Canada–Philippines relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canada%E2%80%93Philippines_relations "Canada–Philippines relations")
  * [Canada](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canada "Canada") and the [Philippines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippines "Philippines") sign a [visiting forces agreement](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Visiting_forces_agreement "Visiting forces agreement") allowing military personnel to train in each other's territories. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/americas/philippines-canada-sign-pact-troops-deepen-defence-ties-2025-11-02/)
* [Nigeria–United States relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nigeria%E2%80%93United_States_relations "Nigeria–United States relations"), [Boko Haram insurgency](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boko_Haram_insurgency "Boko Haram insurgency")
  * The [Nigerian government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nigerian_government "Nigerian government") says that it welcomes assistance from the [United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States") to combat militants, but adds that its sovereignty must be respected, in response to [U.S. president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/U.S._president "U.S. president") [Donald Trump](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Donald_Trump "Donald Trump") threatening aggressive military attacks to protect [Christians](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christians "Christians") from alleged [persecution](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Persecution_of_Christians_in_Nigeria "Persecution of Christians in Nigeria"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/africa/nigeria-welcomes-us-assistance-fight-terrorism-presidency-spokesperson-says-2025-11-02/)
