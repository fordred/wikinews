#### Accidents and disasters

* [2025 Balkh earthquake](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Balkh_earthquake "2025 Balkh earthquake")

#### International relations

* [Nigeria–United States relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nigeria%E2%80%93United_States_relations "Nigeria–United States relations")
  * The [Nigerian government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nigerian_government "Nigerian government") says that it welcomes assistance from the [United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States") to combat [Islamic terrorism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_terrorism "Islamic terrorism"), but adds that its [sovereignty](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sovereignty "Sovereignty") must be respected, as [US President](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/US_President "US President") [Donald Trump](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Donald_Trump "Donald Trump") threatened with military actions to protect [Christians](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christians "Christians") from alleged [persecution](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Persecution_of_Christians_in_Nigeria "Persecution of Christians in Nigeria"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/africa/nigeria-welcomes-us-assistance-fight-terrorism-presidency-spokesperson-says-2025-11-02/)
