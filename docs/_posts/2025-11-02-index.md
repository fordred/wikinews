#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [Gaza War](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_War "Gaza War")
  * [Al Jazeera](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Al_Jazeera "Al Jazeera") reports that Hamas has agreed to retreat via [Red Cross](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Cross "Red Cross") corridors from [Gaza](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza "Gaza") areas which are meant to be under Israeli control per the truce deal. Per the report, the mediators are working to ensure that the militants withdraw to the Hamas-controlled side of the Yellow Line without clashes with Israeli forces. The mediators are reportedly currently awaiting approval of Israel. [(*Haaretz*)](https://www.haaretz.com/israel-news/2025-11-02/ty-article-live/u-s-military-publishes-video-of-palestinians-looting-an-aid-truck-in-southern-gaza/0000019a-429d-d4b1-adbe-6fbfba3b0000#1844466313)

#### Politics and elections

* [2025 North Macedonian local elections](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_North_Macedonian_local_elections "2025 North Macedonian local elections")
  * [Citizens of North Macedonia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Demographics_of_North_Macedonia "Demographics of North Macedonia") vote in the second round of the local elections for councillors and mayors of 80 municipalities and [Skopje](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skopje "Skopje"), with the ruling conservative party [VMRO-DPMNE](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/VMRO-DPMNE "VMRO-DPMNE") expected to win by a landslide.
