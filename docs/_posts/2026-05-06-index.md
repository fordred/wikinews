* [MV *Hondius* hantavirus outbreak](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MV_Hondius_hantavirus_outbreak "MV Hondius hantavirus outbreak")
  * Three passengers infected with [hantavirus pulmonary syndrome](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hantavirus_pulmonary_syndrome "Hantavirus pulmonary syndrome") from the [Andes virus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andes_virus "Andes virus") are evacuated off the [MV *Hondius*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MV_Hondius "MV Hondius") cruise ship while it is stationed off the coast of [Cape Verde](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cape_Verde "Cape Verde") to receive treatment in the [Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands "Netherlands"). At least 145 people remain on board, including two suspected [asymptomatic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asymptomatic "Asymptomatic") cases. [(ABC News)](https://abcnews.com/Health/3-evacuated-off-cruise-ship-suspected-hantavirus-cluster/story?id=132699628) [(NBC News)](https://www.nbcnews.com/health/health-news/hantavirus-cruise-ship-evacuation-new-case-andes-strain-canary-islands-rcna343816)
  * [Spanish](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spain "Spain") [authorities](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Government_of_Spain "Government of Spain") allow the ship to dock at the [Canary Islands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canary_Islands "Canary Islands") as several passengers are [Spanish citizens](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spaniards "Spaniards"), however, Canarian [president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_the_Canary_Islands "President of the Canary Islands") [Fernando Clavijo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fernando_Clavijo_%28politician%29 "Fernando Clavijo (politician)") object to protect the health of those on the islands. [(*The Irish Independent*)](https://www.independent.ie/world-news/europe/canary-islands-government-rejects-hantavirus-hit-cruise-ship-docking-there-as-eighth-case-confirmed-and-three-evacuated/a1184256824.html)
