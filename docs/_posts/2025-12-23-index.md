#### Business and economy

* The [Italian Competition Authority](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italian_Competition_Authority "Italian Competition Authority") fines [Irish](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Republic_of_Ireland "Republic of Ireland") [low-cost carrier](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Low-cost_carrier "Low-cost carrier") [Ryanair](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ryanair "Ryanair") €255 million (US$300 million) for allegedly [abusing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/European_Union_competition_law "European Union competition law") its [dominant market position](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Market_domination "Market domination") from April 2023 to April 2025 by restricting how [travel agencies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Travel_agencies "Travel agencies") sell its flights. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/sustainability/boards-policy-regulation/italy-regulator-fines-ryanair-255-million-euros-alleged-abuse-dominant-position-2025-12-23/)

#### Politics and elections

* [2026 Portuguese presidential election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Portuguese_presidential_election "2026 Portuguese presidential election")
  * A [court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Judiciary_of_Portugal "Judiciary of Portugal") in [Lisbon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lisbon "Lisbon"), [Portugal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portugal "Portugal"), orders [Chega](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chega "Chega") [presidential](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Portugal "President of Portugal") candidate [André Ventura](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andr%C3%A9_Ventura "André Ventura") to remove campaign posters [targeting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Negative_campaigning "Negative campaigning") the [Romani people](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Romani_people_in_Portugal "Romani people in Portugal") within 24 hours, ruling that the materials are [discriminatory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Racism_in_Portugal "Racism in Portugal") and may [incite hatred](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Incitement_to_ethnic_or_racial_hatred "Incitement to ethnic or racial hatred"), or face daily fines of €2,500 (US$2,940) per poster.
