#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [Communal conflicts in Nigeria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Communal_conflicts_in_Nigeria "Communal conflicts in Nigeria")
  * Gunmen launch simultaneous attacks on three communities in [Niger State](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Niger_State "Niger State"), [Nigeria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nigeria "Nigeria"), killing at least 46 people and [kidnapping](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kidnapping_in_Nigeria "Kidnapping in Nigeria") a number of others. [(AFP via *The Daily Star*)](https://www.thedailystar.net/news/world/news/46-killed-fresh-nigeria-attacks-4106461)
* [Islamist insurgency in Burkina Faso](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamist_insurgency_in_Burkina_Faso "Islamist insurgency in Burkina Faso")
  * Seven [Ghanaian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ghanaians "Ghanaians") traders are killed when [Islamist insurgents](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ansarul_Islam_%28Sahel%29 "Ansarul Islam (Sahel)") attack [Titao](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Titao "Titao"), [Loroum Province](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Loroum_Province "Loroum Province"), [Burkina Faso](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Burkina_Faso "Burkina Faso"), with the assailants burning the bodies and a vehicle. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/africa/seven-ghanaian-traders-killed-burkina-faso-by-suspected-islamists-ghana-says-2026-02-16/)
* [Mexican drug war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mexican_drug_war "Mexican drug war")
  * Gunmen attack a bar in [Puebla](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Puebla_%28city%29 "Puebla (city)"), [Mexico](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mexico "Mexico"), killing three people and wounding five others. Authorities confirm the arrest of four criminals related to the attack. [(Aristegui Noticias)](https://aristeguinoticias.com/1402/mexico/ataque-armado-en-bar-sala-de-despecho-de-puebla-deja-tres-muertos/)
