#### Law and crime

* [Ethiopia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ethiopia "Ethiopia") revokes [Reuters](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reuters "Reuters") journalists' accreditation in response to a Reuters article on Febrary 10 that alleged the [Ethiopian military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ethiopian_National_Defense_Force "Ethiopian National Defense Force") was supporting the [Rapid Support Forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rapid_Support_Forces "Rapid Support Forces") in the [Sudanese civil war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudanese_civil_war_%282023%E2%80%93present%29 "Sudanese civil war (2023–present)") by running secret [training camps](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Training_camp "Training camp") for its fighters.
