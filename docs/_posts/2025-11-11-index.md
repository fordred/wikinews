#### Law and crime

* [2006 Noida serial murders](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2006_Noida_serial_murders "2006 Noida serial murders")
  * The [Supreme Court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_Court_of_India "Supreme Court of India") of [India](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/India "India") acquits Surendra Koli of all remaining charges in the [series of child murders](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Serial_killer "Serial killer") and [rapes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rape "Rape") which occurred between 2005 and 2006 in [Noida](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Noida "Noida"), [Uttar Pradesh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uttar_Pradesh "Uttar Pradesh"), and ordering his immediate release. Koli was originally [sentenced to death](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capital_punishment_in_India "Capital punishment in India") and was serving a [life sentence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Life_sentence "Life sentence") for the remaining of cases.
