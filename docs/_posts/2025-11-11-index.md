#### Law and crime

* [2006 Noida serial murders](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2006_Noida_serial_murders "2006 Noida serial murders")
  * The [Supreme Court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_Court_of_India "Supreme Court of India") of [India](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/India "India") acquits Surendra Koli of all remaining charges in the [series of child murders](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Serial_killer "Serial killer") and [rapes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rape "Rape") which occurred between 2005 and 2006 in [Noida](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Noida "Noida"), [Uttar Pradesh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uttar_Pradesh "Uttar Pradesh"), and ordering his immediate release. Koli was originally [sentenced to death](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capital_punishment_in_India "Capital punishment in India") and was serving a [life sentence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Life_sentence "Life sentence") for the remaining cases. [(*The Hindu*)](https://www.thehindu.com/news/national/nithari-killings-supreme-court-acquits-prime-accused-surendra-koli-in-last-pending-case/article70265652.ece)

  * A court in [Istanbul](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Istanbul "Istanbul"), [Turkey](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turkey "Turkey"), issues [arrest warrants](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arrest_warrant "Arrest warrant") for eight suspects involved in an investigation into alleged illegal [sports betting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sports_betting "Sports betting") by [Turkish Football Federation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turkish_Football_Federation "Turkish Football Federation") [referees](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Referee "Referee"), club presidents, and [players](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Footballer "Footballer").
