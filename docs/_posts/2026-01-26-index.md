  * [Spanish](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spain "Spain") [police](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Police_Corps_%28Spain%29 "National Police Corps (Spain)") dismantle an international [maritime cocaine smuggling](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maritime_cocaine_smuggling "Maritime cocaine smuggling") ring that uses [offshore vessels](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Patrol_boat "Patrol boat") and [high-speed crafts](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/High-speed_craft "High-speed craft") to deliver drugs to Europe, seizing 10 tonnes of [cocaine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cocaine "Cocaine"), confiscating dozens of vessels and vehicles, and arresting 105 suspects after a year-long multinational investigation. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/interpol-global-human-trafficking-arrests-operation-liberterra-ee830f01740aa705a42ba9785a10fab1)
