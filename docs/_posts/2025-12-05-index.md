  * [Afghanistan–Pakistan border skirmishes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghanistan%E2%80%93Pakistan_border_skirmishes "Afghanistan–Pakistan border skirmishes")
    * The [Pakistani](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pakistan "Pakistan") [military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pakistan_Armed_Forces "Pakistan Armed Forces") and [Afghan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghanistan "Afghanistan") [military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghan_Armed_Forces "Afghan Armed Forces") exchange fire along [their border](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Durand_Line "Durand Line") with Pakistani [artillery](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Artillery "Artillery") targeting several [Taliban](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taliban "Taliban") outposts in [Helmand Province](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Helmand_Province "Helmand Province"), [Kandahar Province](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kandahar_Province "Kandahar Province") and [Zabul Province](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zabul_Province "Zabul Province"). Both sides accuse the other of instigating the clashes. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/pakistan-afghanistan-exchange-heavy-fire-along-border-officials-say-2025-12-05/) [(WTOP News)](https://wtop.com/world/2025/12/pakistan-and-afghanistan-trade-fire-along-the-border-but-no-casualties-are-reported/)
* [Colombian conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colombian_conflict "Colombian conflict")
  * [Colombia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colombia "Colombia") and the [Clan del Golfo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clan_del_Golfo "Clan del Golfo") agree in [Doha](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Doha "Doha"), [Qatar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qatar "Qatar"), that the group's combatants will begin assembling in three designated zones in [Chocó](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Choc%C3%B3_Department "Chocó Department") and [Córdoba](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/C%C3%B3rdoba%2C_Colombia "Córdoba, Colombia") starting March 1, with arrest and [extradition](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Extradition "Extradition") orders suspended during the process. Colombia also designates additional [municipalities](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Municipalities_of_Colombia "Municipalities of Colombia") for pilot peace programs as part of broader negotiations aimed at reducing violence. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/americas/colombia-clan-del-golfo-crime-gang-agree-three-concentration-areas-combatants-2025-12-05/)
