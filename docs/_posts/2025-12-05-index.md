  * [Colombia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colombia "Colombia") and the [Clan del Golfo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clan_del_Golfo "Clan del Golfo") agree in [Doha](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Doha "Doha"), [Qatar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qatar "Qatar"), that the group's combatants will begin assembling in three designated zones in [Chocó](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Choc%C3%B3_Department "Chocó Department") and [Córdoba](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/C%C3%B3rdoba_Department "Córdoba Department") starting March 1, with arrest and [extradition](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Extradition "Extradition") orders suspended during the process. Colombia also designates additional [municipalities](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Municipalities_of_Colombia "Municipalities of Colombia") for pilot peace programs as part of broader negotiations aimed at reducing violence. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/americas/colombia-clan-del-golfo-crime-gang-agree-three-concentration-areas-combatants-2025-12-05/)
