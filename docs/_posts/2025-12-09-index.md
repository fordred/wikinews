#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [2025 Cambodian–Thai border crisis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Cambodian%E2%80%93Thai_border_crisis "2025 Cambodian–Thai border crisis")
  * The [Royal Thai Air Force](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Thai_Air_Force "Royal Thai Air Force") carries out several [airstrikes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airstrike "Airstrike") against [Cambodia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cambodia "Cambodia"), killing seven civilians and wounding 20 more. Separately, three [Thai soldiers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Thai_Army "Royal Thai Army") are killed and 29 more injured during clashes with [Cambodian soldiers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Cambodian_Army "Royal Cambodian Army"). [(Al Jazeera 1)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2025/12/9/thailand-cambodia-live?update=4161571)
  * The [Thai foreign minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ministry_of_Foreign_Affairs_%28Thailand%29 "Ministry of Foreign Affairs (Thailand)") declares that "the current conflict provides no space for a [diplomatic solution](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplomacy "Diplomacy")". [(Al Jazeera 2)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2025/12/9/thailand-cambodia-live?update=4161600)
