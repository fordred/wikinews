#### Armed conflicts and attacks

  * The [Royal Thai Air Force](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Thai_Air_Force "Royal Thai Air Force") carries out several [airstrikes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airstrike "Airstrike") against [Cambodia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cambodia "Cambodia"), killing nine civilians and wounding 20 more. [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2025/12/9/thailand-cambodia-live?update=4161571)

[(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2025/12/9/thailand-cambodia-live?update=4161571)

  * [2025 Uvira offensive](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Uvira_offensive "2025 Uvira offensive")

[(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2025/12/9/drc-accuses-rwanda-of-peace-deal-violations-as-m23-advances-in-the-east) [(SOS Media Burundi)](https://www.sosmediasburundi.org/en/2025/12/09/south-kivu-m23-seizes-sange-triggering-a-massive-influx-into-burundi-and-a-retreat-of-burundian-troops/)

[(CNN)](https://www.cnn.com/2025/12/09/australia/australia-social-media-ban-starts-intl-hnk)
