#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [2025 Cambodian–Thai border crisis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Cambodian%E2%80%93Thai_border_crisis "2025 Cambodian–Thai border crisis")
  * The [Royal Thai Air Force](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Thai_Air_Force "Royal Thai Air Force") carries out several [airstrikes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airstrike "Airstrike") against [Cambodia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cambodia "Cambodia"), killing seven civilians and wounding 20 more. [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2025/12/9/thailand-cambodia-live?update=4161571)
  * Three [Thai soldiers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Thai_Army "Royal Thai Army") are killed and 29 more injured during clashes with [Cambodian soldiers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Cambodian_Army "Royal Cambodian Army"). [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2025/12/9/thailand-cambodia-live?update=4161571)

[(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/office-building-jakarta-indonesia-deadly-fire-fa998b4135754fdcd58f1b1364a0776a)

#### Health and environment

* The [Government of Catalonia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Government_of_Catalonia "Government of Catalonia"), in [Spain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spain "Spain"), announces a state of emergency due to the rapid spread of the [African swine fever virus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/African_swine_fever_virus "African swine fever virus") in wild boars and the danger of it spreading to other regions of Spain and infecting people. [(*20minutos*)](https://www.20minutos.es/cataluna/catalunya-declara-emergencia-para-contener-paliar-los-efectos-brote-peste-porcina_6908177_0.html)
