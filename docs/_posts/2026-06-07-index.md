#### Politics and elections

* [2026 Armenian parliamentary election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Armenian_parliamentary_election "2026 Armenian parliamentary election")
  * [Armenia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Armenia "Armenia") begins conducting an election for the [National Assembly](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Assembly_%28Armenia%29 "National Assembly (Armenia)"), the first since the [expulsion of Nagorno-Karabakh Armenians](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Expulsion_of_Nagorno-Karabakh_Armenians "Expulsion of Nagorno-Karabakh Armenians") in late 2023. Incumbent [prime minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_Armenia "Prime Minister of Armenia") [Nikol Pashinyan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nikol_Pashinyan "Nikol Pashinyan") seeks a third term in office and the retention of a [Civil Contract](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Civil_Contract_%28Armenia%29 "Civil Contract (Armenia)") government. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/armenia-russia-parliament-elections-pashinyan-putin-2304d3b606c77108495486c977e49726)
* [2026 Kosovan parliamentary election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Kosovan_parliamentary_election "2026 Kosovan parliamentary election")
  * [Kosovo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kosovo "Kosovo") holds its third elections for the [Kuvendi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Assembly_of_Kosovo "Assembly of Kosovo") in 18 months amid continued [political deadlock](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Political_deadlock "Political deadlock") and the lack of a [two-thirds majority](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supermajority "Supermajority") to [elect](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Kosovan_presidential_election "2026 Kosovan presidential election") a [president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Kosovo "President of Kosovo"). [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/kosovo-election-politics-stalemate-osmani-kurti-vote-21b9c61aacdb142de6fbaad7059610d8)
