#### Politics and elections

* In an overnight session, the [National Assembly](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Assembly_%28France%29 "National Assembly (France)") in France approves a bill banning [social media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Social_media "Social media") for children under the age of 15. The bill will now go to the [Senate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Senate_of_France "Senate of France") for approval.
