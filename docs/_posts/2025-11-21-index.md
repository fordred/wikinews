This is denied by the [Armed Forces of Ukraine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Armed_Forces_of_Ukraine "Armed Forces of Ukraine"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/europe/putin-met-with-russian-top-military-brass-kremlin-says-2025-11-20/)
