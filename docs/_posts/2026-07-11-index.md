#### Business and economy

* [Housing in the United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Housing_in_the_United_States "Housing in the United States")
  * The [21st Century ROAD to Housing Act](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/21st_Century_ROAD_to_Housing_Act "21st Century ROAD to Housing Act"), a legislation passed in the [U.S. Congress](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Congress "United States Congress") to expand [affordable housing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Affordable_housing "Affordable housing") and reduce housing costs in the [United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States"), becomes law after [U.S. president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_the_United_States "President of the United States") [Donald Trump](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Donald_Trump "Donald Trump") allows the bill to take effect without his signature. * [India–New Zealand relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/India%E2%80%93New_Zealand_relations "India–New Zealand relations")
  * [India](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/India "India") and [New Zealand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Zealand "New Zealand") announce the formation of a [strategic partnership](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strategic_partnership_%28international_relations%29 "Strategic partnership (international relations)") during [Indian prime minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_India "Prime Minister of India") [Narendra Modi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Narendra_Modi "Narendra Modi")'s [visit](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_international_prime_ministerial_trips_made_by_Narendra_Modi "List of international prime ministerial trips made by Narendra Modi") to [Auckland](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Auckland "Auckland"), expanding cooperation on defense, security, trade, diplomacy, culture, sport, science, and [maritime issues](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Free_and_Open_Indo-Pacific "Free and Open Indo-Pacific") in the [Indo-Pacific](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indo-Pacific "Indo-Pacific").
