#### International relations

* [2026 United Nations Climate Change Conference](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_United_Nations_Climate_Change_Conference "2026 United Nations Climate Change Conference"), [Australia–Turkey relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia%E2%80%93Turkey_relations "Australia–Turkey relations")
  * [Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia "Australia") and [Turkey](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turkey "Turkey") confirm an agreement for Turkey to host the 2026 [United Nations Climate Change Conference](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations_Climate_Change_Conference "United Nations Climate Change Conference") (COP31) in [Antalya](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antalya "Antalya"), with Australia leading the conference's negotiation process and the [Asia–Pacific Group](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Group_of_Asia_and_the_Pacific_Small_Island_Developing_States "Group of Asia and the Pacific Small Island Developing States") convening a pre-COP31 meeting focused on [climate-related vulnerabilities](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Climate_change_vulnerability "Climate change vulnerability"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/sustainability/cop/australia-pm-says-formal-deal-reached-turkey-host-cop31-climate-summit-2025-11-22/)
