[(CBC)](https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/toronto/colorectal-cancer-screening-age-july-1-9.7255215) [(*Toronto Star*)](https://www.thestar.com/news/ontario/ontario-joins-pei-in-lowering-colorectal-cancer-screening-age-to-45/article_c2bf38de-86d3-5458-b0f3-b9fb1028ec5f.html)
