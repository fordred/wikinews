#### Health and environment

* The [Canadian province](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Provinces_and_territories_of_Canada "Provinces and territories of Canada") of [Ontario](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ontario "Ontario") reduces the eligibility age for [colorectal cancer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colorectal_cancer "Colorectal cancer") [screenings](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cancer_screening#Colon_(colorectal)_cancer "Cancer screening") from 50 to 45, becoming the second province in [Canada](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canada "Canada") to do so after [Prince Edward Island](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prince_Edward_Island "Prince Edward Island").
