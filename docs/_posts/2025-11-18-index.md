#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [Insurgency in Cabo Delgado](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Insurgency_in_Cabo_Delgado "Insurgency in Cabo Delgado")
  * [2021 Cabo Delgado offensive](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2021_Cabo_Delgado_offensive "2021 Cabo Delgado offensive")
    * The [European Center for Constitutional and Human Rights](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/European_Center_for_Constitutional_and_Human_Rights "European Center for Constitutional and Human Rights") accuses [French](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/France "France") energy and petroleum company [TotalEnergies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/TotalEnergies "TotalEnergies") of committing [war crimes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/War_crimes "War crimes") through a [joint task force](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joint_task_force "Joint task force") deployed to protect [natural gas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Natural_gas "Natural gas") sites in [Cabo Delgado](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cabo_Delgado_Province "Cabo Delgado Province"), [Mozambique](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mozambique "Mozambique"). The NGO alleges that the joint task force illegally imprisoned, beat, tortured, and killed over 220 civilians. #### Law and crime

* [Gaza Strip under Hamas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_Strip_under_Hamas "Gaza Strip under Hamas"), [Blockade of the Gaza Strip](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blockade_of_the_Gaza_Strip "Blockade of the Gaza Strip")

#### Politics and elections

* [2025 Texas redistricting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Texas_redistricting "2025 Texas redistricting")
  * A three-judge panel of the [U.S. District Court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/U.S._District_Court "U.S. District Court") bars Texas from using a newly drawn congressional map in next year’s midterm elections, ruling that it is likely an unconstitutional racial [gerrymander](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gerrymander "Gerrymander"). [(CNN)](https://www.cnn.com/2025/11/18/politics/texas-redistricting-trump-court-ruling)
* [Epstein Files Transparency Act](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Epstein_Files_Transparency_Act "Epstein Files Transparency Act")
  * The [United States House of Representatives](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_House_of_Representatives "United States House of Representatives") votes 427–1 in favor of a bill to force the [United States Justice Department](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Justice_Department "United States Justice Department") to release [more information](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Epstein_files "Epstein files") about the [Jeffrey Epstein case](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeffrey_Epstein "Jeffrey Epstein"). The bill heads to the [Senate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Senate "United States Senate") for approval. The sole no vote is Representative [Clay Higgins](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clay_Higgins "Clay Higgins") of [Louisana](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Louisana "Louisana"). [(*The New York Times*)](https://www.nytimes.com/live/2025/11/18/us/trump-epstein-files-news)
