#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [2025 Cambodia–Thailand conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Cambodia%E2%80%93Thailand_conflict "2025 Cambodia–Thailand conflict")
  * [Cambodia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cambodia "Cambodia") and [Thailand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thailand "Thailand") sign a new [ceasefire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ceasefire "Ceasefire") agreement, calling for a pause in military movements and [airspace](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airspace "Airspace") encroachment for military purposes. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/thailand-cambodia-border-fighting-ceasefire-0019310e1c062cd211f9f5398b3bc463)
* [Russo-Ukrainian war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russo-Ukrainian_war_%282022%E2%80%93present%29 "Russo-Ukrainian war (2022–present)")
  * [Denis Kapustin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Denis_Kapustin_%28militant%29 "Denis Kapustin (militant)"), leader of the [Ukraine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ukraine "Ukraine")-aligned [Russian Volunteer Corps](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian_Volunteer_Corps "Russian Volunteer Corps"), is killed in a Russian [drone strike](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Drone_strike "Drone strike") in [Zaporizhzhia Oblast](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zaporizhzhia_Oblast "Zaporizhzhia Oblast"). [(*The Kyiv Independent*)](https://kyivindependent.com/top-anti-putin-russian-commander-killed-in-ukraine/) [(*The Moscow Times*)](https://www.themoscowtimes.com/2025/12/27/leader-of-russian-volunteer-corps-killed-fighting-for-kyiv-a91564)
  * [Kyiv strikes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kyiv_strikes_%282022%E2%80%93present%29 "Kyiv strikes (2022–present)"), [Russian strikes against Ukrainian infrastructure](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian_strikes_against_Ukrainian_infrastructure "Russian strikes against Ukrainian infrastructure")
    * Two people are killed and 32 others injured during a [Russian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian_Armed_Forces "Russian Armed Forces") missile and drone attack on [Kyiv](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kyiv "Kyiv"), [Ukraine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ukraine "Ukraine"). Damage to [critical infrastructure](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Critical_infrastructure "Critical infrastructure") leaves 40% of Kyiv's residential areas and surrounding [districts](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Districts_of_Ukraine "Districts of Ukraine") without [heating](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heating%2C_ventilation%2C_and_air_conditioning "Heating, ventilation, and air conditioning"). [(BBC News)](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1dzvpg1341o)

#### Business and economy

* [Trade policy of China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trade_policy_of_China "Trade policy of China")
  * The [National People's Congress](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_People%27s_Congress "National People's Congress") of [China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China "China") passes a revised [trade law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trade_law "Trade law") that will expand [the government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Government_of_China "Government of China")'s authority to counter [trade restrictions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chinese_government_sanctions "Chinese government sanctions"), regulate [exports](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_exports_of_China "List of exports of China") of [strategic goods](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strategic_material "Strategic material"), and update provisions on [digital](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electronic_trading "Electronic trading"), [green](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Green_trading "Green trading"), and [intellectual-property](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intellectual_property_in_China "Intellectual property in China") trade from March 1, 2026. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/china/china-passes-revised-foreign-trade-law-bolster-trade-war-capabilities-2025-12-27/)

#### Disasters and accidents

* Fifteen people are killed and 19 more injured as a bus plunges in to a [ravine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ravine "Ravine") while driving on the [Inter-American Highway](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inter-American_Highway "Inter-American Highway") in [Sololá Department](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solol%C3%A1_Department "Sololá Department"), [Guatemala](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guatemala "Guatemala"). [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2025/12/27/at-least-15-dead-19-injured-in-guatemala-as-bus-plunges-into-ravine)
* At least nine people are killed and eight other injured when a bus carrying 19 [charity workers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charitable_organization "Charitable organization") overturns on a mountain in Phinh Ho, [Lào Cai province](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/L%C3%A0o_Cai_province "Lào Cai province"), [Vietnam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vietnam "Vietnam"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/least-7-killed-vietnam-after-bus-overturns-2025-12-27/) [(Tuổi Trẻ News)](https://news.tuoitre.vn/9-killed-8-injured-after-bus-carrying-charity-group-overturns-in-northern-vietnam-103251227142029558.htm)
* A Mw 7.0 [earthquake](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earthquake "Earthquake") strikes 32 km off the coast of [Yilan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yilan_City "Yilan City"), [Taiwan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taiwan "Taiwan"), causing a temporary [power outage](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Power_outage "Power outage") for over 3,000 households.
