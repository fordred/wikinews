  * [Chilean](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chile "Chile") state-owned [mining](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mining_in_Chile "Mining in Chile") company [Codelco](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Codelco "Codelco") and private miner [SQM](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sociedad_Qu%C3%ADmica_y_Minera "Sociedad Química y Minera") create a [joint venture](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joint_venture "Joint venture") to develop [lithium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lithium "Lithium") in the [Atacama](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Salar_de_Atacama "Salar de Atacama") [salt flat](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Salt_pan_%28geology%29 "Salt pan (geology)") until 2060, with the Chilean state set to receive the majority of [operating margins](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Operating_margin "Operating margin").   * The [National People's Congress](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_People%27s_Congress "National People's Congress") of [China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China "China") passes a revised [trade law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trade_law "Trade law") that will expand [the government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Government_of_China "Government of China")'s authority to counter [trade restrictions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chinese_government_sanctions "Chinese government sanctions"), regulate [exports](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_exports_of_China "List of exports of China") of [strategic goods](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strategic_material "Strategic material"), and update provisions on [digital](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electronic_trading "Electronic trading"), [green](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Green_trading "Green trading"), and [intellectual-property](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intellectual_property_in_China "Intellectual property in China") trade from March 1, 2026. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/china/china-passes-revised-foreign-trade-law-bolster-trade-war-capabilities-2025-12-27/)
