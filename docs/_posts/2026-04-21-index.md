  * [NASA](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/NASA "NASA") announces that the *[Curiosity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Curiosity_%28rover%29 "Curiosity (rover)")* [rover](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mars_rover "Mars rover") has detected more than 20 [organic molecules](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Organic_molecules "Organic molecules") on [Mars](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mars "Mars") using a [chemical analysis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chemical_analysis "Chemical analysis") method involving [tetramethylammonium hydroxide](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tetramethylammonium_hydroxide "Tetramethylammonium hydroxide"), conducted for the first time on another planet, indicating the long-term preservation of compounds associated with [prebiotic chemistry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prebiotic_chemistry "Prebiotic chemistry").
