#### Law and crime

* [2025 Indonesian protests](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Indonesian_protests "2025 Indonesian protests")
  * A joint investigation by the [Indonesian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indonesia "Indonesia") [human rights commission](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Commission_on_Human_Rights "National Commission on Human Rights"), the [ombudsman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ombudsman "Ombudsman"), and the child protection commission finds that [police](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indonesian_National_Police "Indonesian National Police") and other [state security](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Law_enforcement_in_Indonesia "Law enforcement in Indonesia") personnel violated [human rights](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Human_rights_in_Indonesia "Human rights in Indonesia"), including [arbitrary arrests](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arbitrary_arrest_and_detention "Arbitrary arrest and detention"), torture, and alleged [child sexual abuse](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Child_sexual_abuse "Child sexual abuse"), during anti-[government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_and_White_Cabinet "Red and White Cabinet") protests in 2025 that left at least 11 people dead and thousands detained. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/indonesia-rights-bodies-find-widespread-abuses-2025-protests-2026-04-21/)
