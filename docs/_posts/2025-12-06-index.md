#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [Israeli–Palestinian conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli%E2%80%93Palestinian_conflict "Israeli–Palestinian conflict")
  * Two [Palestinians](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Palestinians "Palestinians") are killed and a soldier is injured when [Israel Defense Forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel_Defense_Forces "Israel Defense Forces") soldiers open fire on a car speeding toward troops in [Hebron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hebron "Hebron"), [West Bank](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Bank "West Bank"). [(*The Times of Israel*)](https://www.timesofisrael.com/idf-two-palestinians-killed-after-speeding-car-toward-troops-in-hebron-soldier-lightly-hurt/)
* [Sudanese civil war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudanese_civil_war_%282023%E2%80%93present%29 "Sudanese civil war (2023–present)")
  * Fifty people are killed, including 33 children, in a [drone strike](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Drone_warfare "Drone warfare") by the [Rapid Support Forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rapid_Support_Forces "Rapid Support Forces") on a [kindergarten](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kindergarten "Kindergarten") in Kalogi, [South Kordofan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Kordofan "South Kordofan"), [Sudan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudan "Sudan"). [(MSN)](https://www.msn.com/en-ca/news/world/sudanese-paramilitary-drone-attack-kills-50-including-33-children-in-kordofan-doctor-group-says/ar-AA1RPpCa?ocid=winp1taskbar&cvid=693450db9e2f4e53acf13f92543a5613&ei=14)

#### Business and economy

* [2025 IndiGo disruption](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_IndiGo_disruption "2025 IndiGo disruption")
  * The [Indian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/India "India") [civil aviation ministry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ministry_of_Civil_Aviation_%28India%29 "Ministry of Civil Aviation (India)") caps [domestic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Domestic_flight "Domestic flight") [airfares](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airfare "Airfare") following widespread [IndiGo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IndiGo "IndiGo") cancellations caused by pilot shortages and operational failures, setting a maximum [one-way](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/One-way_travel "One-way travel") price of [₹](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_rupee "Indian rupee")7,500 ([US$](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_dollar "United States dollar")83.38) for routes up to 500 kilometres (310 mi). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/india-air-travel-chaos-eases-indigo-crisis-still-leaves-hundreds-stranded-2025-12-06/)

#### Disasters and accidents

* At least 23 people are killed, including several tourists, in an explosion and fire at a [nightclub](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nightclub "Nightclub") in [Goa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Goa "Goa"), [India](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/India "India"). [(DW)](https://www.dw.com/en/india-nightclub-fire-in-goa-kills-at-least-23/a-75048726)
* At least 18 people are killed and two others are critically injured when a [dinghy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dinghy "Dinghy") carrying migrants [sinks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shipwreck "Shipwreck") off [Crete](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crete "Crete"), [Greece](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greece "Greece"). [(Greek Reporter)](https://greekreporter.com/2025/12/06/migrant-shipwreck-leaves-eighteen-dead-off-greeces-crete/)
* At least two police officers are killed and seven others are injured in an explosion outside of a police station in [Coahuayana](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coahuayana "Coahuayana"), [Michoacán](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michoac%C3%A1n "Michoacán"), [Mexico](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mexico "Mexico"). [(CTV News)](https://www.ctvnews.ca/world/article/at-least-2-killed-7-wounded-in-explosion-in-western-mexican-state-of-michoacan/)
