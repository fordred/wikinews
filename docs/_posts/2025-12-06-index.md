#### Business and economy

* The [Indian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/India "India") [civil aviation ministry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ministry_of_Civil_Aviation_%28India%29 "Ministry of Civil Aviation (India)") caps [domestic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Domestic_flight "Domestic flight") [airfares](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airfare "Airfare") following widespread [IndiGo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IndiGo "IndiGo") flight cancellations caused by pilot shortages and operational failures, setting a maximum [one-way](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/One-way_travel "One-way travel") price of [₹](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_rupee "Indian rupee")7,500 ([US$](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_dollar "United States dollar")83.38) for routes up to 500 kilometres (310 mi). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/india-air-travel-chaos-eases-indigo-crisis-still-leaves-hundreds-stranded-2025-12-06/)
