#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [Russo-Ukrainian war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russo-Ukrainian_war_%282022%E2%80%93present%29 "Russo-Ukrainian war (2022–present)")
  * Eight people are wounded after [Russia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia "Russia") launches an attack on [Ukraine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ukraine "Ukraine") with 653 drones and 51 missiles, with 585 drones and 30 missiles claimed to be shot down, targeting primarily electrical infrastructure. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/russia-ukraine-war-drones-witkoff-trump-7f5e063223a426a4629b67c5991d1a73)
    * [NATO](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/NATO "NATO") fighter jets are scrambled and air defense systems are put on alert in [Poland](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Poland "Poland"). [(ABC News)](https://abcnews.go.com/International/nato-fighters-scrambled-poland-russia-bombards-ukraine/story?id=128167479)
  * [Zaporizhzhia Nuclear Power Plant crisis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zaporizhzhia_Nuclear_Power_Plant_crisis "Zaporizhzhia Nuclear Power Plant crisis")
    * Power is temporarily lost at the non-operational [Zaporizhzhia Nuclear Power Plant](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zaporizhzhia_Nuclear_Power_Plant "Zaporizhzhia Nuclear Power Plant") overnight before being restored. #### Business and economy

* The [Indian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/India "India") [civil aviation ministry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ministry_of_Civil_Aviation_%28India%29 "Ministry of Civil Aviation (India)") caps [domestic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Domestic_flight "Domestic flight") [airfares](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airfare "Airfare") following widespread [IndiGo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IndiGo "IndiGo") flight cancellations caused by pilot shortages and operational failures, setting a maximum [one-way](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/One-way_travel "One-way travel") price of [₹](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_rupee "Indian rupee")7,500 ([US$](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_dollar "United States dollar")83.38) for routes up to 500 kilometres (310 mi). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/india-air-travel-chaos-eases-indigo-crisis-still-leaves-hundreds-stranded-2025-12-06/)
