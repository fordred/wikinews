#### Business and economy

* The [Indian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/India "India") [civil aviation ministry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ministry_of_Civil_Aviation_%28India%29 "Ministry of Civil Aviation (India)") caps [domestic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Domestic_flight "Domestic flight") [airfares](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airfare "Airfare") following widespread [IndiGo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IndiGo "IndiGo") cancellations caused by pilot shortages and operational failures, setting a maximum [one-way](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/One-way_travel "One-way travel") price of [₹](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_rupee "Indian rupee")7,500 ([US$](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_dollar "United States dollar")83.38) for routes up to 500 kilometres (310 mi). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/india-air-travel-chaos-eases-indigo-crisis-still-leaves-hundreds-stranded-2025-12-06/)

#### International relations

* [International sanctions against Afghanistan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_sanctions_against_Afghanistan "International sanctions against Afghanistan")
  * [Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia "Australia") imposes [economic sanctions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Economic_sanctions "Economic sanctions") and [travel bans](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Travel_ban "Travel ban") on four [Afghan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghanistan "Afghanistan") [government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Government_of_Afghanistan "Government of Afghanistan") officials over their involvement in [human rights](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Human_rights_in_Afghanistan "Human rights in Afghanistan") violations, particularly the [Taliban](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taliban "Taliban")'s [treatment of women](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treatment_of_women_by_the_Taliban "Treatment of women by the Taliban"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/australia-hits-afghan-taliban-officials-with-sanctions-travel-bans-2025-12-06/)
