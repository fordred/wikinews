#### Politics and elections

* [2026 Myanmar presidential election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Myanmar_presidential_election "2026 Myanmar presidential election")
  * [Myanmar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Myanmar "Myanmar")'s [military ruler](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/State_Security_and_Peace_Commission "State Security and Peace Commission"), General [Min Aung Hlaing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Min_Aung_Hlaing "Min Aung Hlaing"), is elected [President of the Republic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Myanmar "President of Myanmar") by the [Electoral College](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Presidential_Electoral_College "Presidential Electoral College"). Min secures 429 votes, against 126 for [Nyo Saw](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nyo_Saw "Nyo Saw") and 29 for [Nan Ni Ni Aye](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nan_Ni_Ni_Aye "Nan Ni Ni Aye"), who become first and second [vice presidents](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vice_President_of_Myanmar "Vice President of Myanmar"), respectively.
