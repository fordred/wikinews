#### Politics and elections

* [2026 Italian constitutional referendum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Italian_constitutional_referendum "2026 Italian constitutional referendum")
  * [Italians](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italians "Italians") begin voting on a [constitutional amendment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constitutional_amendment "Constitutional amendment") that proposes separating the careers of judges and public prosecutors by splitting the [High Court of the Judiciary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/High_Council_of_the_Judiciary_%28Italy%29 "High Council of the Judiciary (Italy)") into two bodies, while also establishing a High Disciplinary Court to oversee disciplinary proceedings. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/italys-justice-referendum-tests-pm-meloni-divided-opposition-2026-03-19/)
* [2026 Slovenian parliamentary election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Slovenian_parliamentary_election "2026 Slovenian parliamentary election")
  * [Slovenians](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slovenians "Slovenians") vote for 90 seats in the [National Assembly](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Assembly_%28Slovenia%29 "National Assembly (Slovenia)"). A tight race is forecasted between the incumbent [Prime Minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_Slovenia "Prime Minister of Slovenia") [Robert Golob](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Golob "Robert Golob") and the previous Prime Minister [Janez Janša](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Janez_Jan%C5%A1a "Janez Janša"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/europe/slovenians-vote-tight-race-between-liberals-populist-right-2026-03-22/)
