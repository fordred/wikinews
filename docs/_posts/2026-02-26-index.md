#### Law and crime

* [2022 Greek surveillance scandal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2022_Greek_surveillance_scandal "2022 Greek surveillance scandal")
  * A [Greek](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greece "Greece") [court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Judiciary_of_Greece "Judiciary of Greece") sentences four individuals, including [Intellexa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intellexa "Intellexa")'s [Israel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel "Israel")-based founder [Tal Dilian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tal_Dilian "Tal Dilian"), to eight years in prison for [breaching data](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Data_breach "Data breach") and violating [privacy laws](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Access_to_public_information_in_Greece "Access to public information in Greece") after they deployed the [Predator](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Predator_%28spyware%29 "Predator (spyware)") [spyware](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spyware "Spyware"), targeting the mobile phones of more than 90 [politicians](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Politics_of_Greece "Politics of Greece"), [journalists](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mass_media_in_Greece "Mass media in Greece"), [businesspeople](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Economy_of_Greece "Economy of Greece"), and [military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hellenic_Armed_Forces "Hellenic Armed Forces") officials in 2022.
