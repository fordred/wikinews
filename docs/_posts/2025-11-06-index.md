#### Disasters and accidents

* [2025 Pacific typhoon season](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Pacific_typhoon_season "2025 Pacific typhoon season")
  * The death toll from [Typhoon Kalmaegi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Typhoon_Kalmaegi "Typhoon Kalmaegi")'s impact on the [Philippines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippines "Philippines") rises to at least 142, with 127 others missing. The storm has left the [country's territory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippine_Area_of_Responsibility "Philippine Area of Responsibility") and made landfall in central [Vietnam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vietnam "Vietnam"). [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2025/11/6/typhoon-kalmaegi-bears-down-on-vietnam-after-114-killed-in-the-philippines)
  * [Philippine president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippine_president "Philippine president") [Bongbong Marcos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bongbong_Marcos "Bongbong Marcos") declares a national [state of calamity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/State_of_calamity_%28Philippines%29 "State of calamity (Philippines)") in relation to the devastation caused by Typhoon Kalmaegi and in anticipation of the upcoming tropical cyclone Fung-wong. [(ABS-CBN News)](https://www.abs-cbn.com/news/nation/2025/11/6/marcos-oks-declaration-state-of-national-calamity-over-tino-upcoming-typhoon-1012)

#### International relations

* [Israel–Kazakhstan relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel%E2%80%93Kazakhstan_relations "Israel–Kazakhstan relations")
  * [Kazakhstan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kazakhstan "Kazakhstan") formally joins the [Abraham Accords](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abraham_Accords "Abraham Accords"), with the aim to enhance bilateral cooperation with [Israel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel "Israel"). Formal diplomatic relations between both countries date back to 1992. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/us-israel-kazakhstan-abraham-accords-5bf062712bd7bb326640bd78ba505d19)
* [Khaled El-Enany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Khaled_El-Enany "Khaled El-Enany") is elected director-general of [UNESCO](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/UNESCO "UNESCO") by the General Conference held in [Samarkand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samarkand "Samarkand"), [Uzbekistan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uzbekistan "Uzbekistan"), succeeding [Audrey Azoulay](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Audrey_Azoulay "Audrey Azoulay").
