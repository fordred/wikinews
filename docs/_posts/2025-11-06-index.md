#### Disasters and accidents

* [2025 Pacific typhoon season](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Pacific_typhoon_season "2025 Pacific typhoon season")
  * The death toll from [Typhoon Kalmaegi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Typhoon_Kalmaegi "Typhoon Kalmaegi")'s impact on the [Philippines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippines "Philippines") rises to at least 142, with 127 others missing. The storm has left the [country's territory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippine_Area_of_Responsibility "Philippine Area of Responsibility") and made landfall in central [Vietnam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vietnam "Vietnam"). [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2025/11/6/typhoon-kalmaegi-bears-down-on-vietnam-after-114-killed-in-the-philippines)
  * [Philippine president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippine_president "Philippine president") [Bongbong Marcos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bongbong_Marcos "Bongbong Marcos") declares a national [state of calamity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/State_of_calamity_%28Philippines%29 "State of calamity (Philippines)") in relation to the devastation caused by Typhoon Kalmaegi and in anticipation of the upcoming [tropical cyclone](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tropical_cyclone "Tropical cyclone") Fung-wong. [(ABS-CBN News)](https://www.abs-cbn.com/news/nation/2025/11/6/marcos-oks-declaration-state-of-national-calamity-over-tino-upcoming-typhoon-1012)

#### International relations

* [Khaled El-Enany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Khaled_El-Enany "Khaled El-Enany") is elected director-general of [UNESCO](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/UNESCO "UNESCO") by the General Conference held in [Samarkand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samarkand "Samarkand"), [Uzbekistan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uzbekistan "Uzbekistan"), succeeding [Audrey Azoulay](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Audrey_Azoulay "Audrey Azoulay").   * A [court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Judiciary_of_Russia "Judiciary of Russia") in [Russian-occupied](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian-occupied_territories_of_Ukraine "Russian-occupied territories of Ukraine") [Donetsk](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Donetsk_People%27s_Republic "Donetsk People's Republic") sentences two [Colombians](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colombians "Colombians") to 13 years in [prison](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prisons_in_Russia "Prisons in Russia") for fighting with [Ukraine's armed forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Armed_Forces_of_Ukraine "Armed Forces of Ukraine"), after they were detained while traveling [home](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colombia "Colombia") through [Venezuela](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Venezuela "Venezuela") and transferred into Russian custody. [(AFP via *Kyiv Post*)](https://www.kyivpost.com/post/63800)
  * A [court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Judiciary_of_Ukraine "Judiciary of Ukraine") in [Zaporizhzhia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zaporizhzhia "Zaporizhzhia"), [Ukraine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ukraine "Ukraine"), finds a [Russian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia "Russia") [soldier](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian_Armed_Forces "Russian Armed Forces") guilty of killing a [surrendered](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prisoner_of_war "Prisoner of war") [Ukrainian soldier](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Armed_Forces_of_Ukraine "Armed Forces of Ukraine") in January 2024 and sentences him to [life imprisonment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Life_imprisonment "Life imprisonment"), marking the first conviction of a Russian soldier for killing a captured Ukrainian since [the war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russo-Ukrainian_war_%282022%E2%80%93present%29 "Russo-Ukrainian war (2022–present)") began. [(CNN)](https://edition.cnn.com/2025/11/06/europe/russian-soldier-guilty-killing-ukrainian-prisoner-intl)
