    * [Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran "Iran") fires two [intermediate-range ballistic missiles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intermediate-range_ballistic_missile "Intermediate-range ballistic missile") at the [Diego Garcia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diego_Garcia "Diego Garcia") [military base](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Naval_Support_Facility_Diego_Garcia "Naval Support Facility Diego Garcia") in the [Indian Ocean](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_Ocean "Indian Ocean"). [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/3/21/iran-says-us-and-israel-attacked-natanz-nuclear-facility)
