<p><b>Armed conflicts and attacks</b>

<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Kivu_conflict" title="Kivu conflict">Kivu conflict</a>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/M23_campaign_(2022%E2%80%93present)" title="M23 campaign (2022–present)">M23 campaign</a>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/2025_Uvira_offensive" title="2025 Uvira offensive">2025 Uvira offensive</a>
<ul><li>Widespread <a href="/wiki/Civil_disorder" title="Civil disorder">civil disorder</a> arises in <a href="/wiki/Uvira" title="Uvira">Uvira</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Kivu_Province" class="mw-redirect" title="South Kivu Province">South Kivu Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo" title="Democratic Republic of the Congo">Democratic Republic of the Congo</a> amid <a href="/wiki/March_23_Movement" title="March 23 Movement">M23</a> rebel advances from <a href="/wiki/Bukavu" title="Bukavu">Bukavu</a>. At least thirty deaths are reported, along with <a href="/wiki/Looting" title="Looting">looting</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Prison_escape" title="Prison escape">prison break</a> of 228 <a href="/wiki/Armed_Forces_of_the_Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo" title="Armed Forces of the Democratic Republic of the Congo">Congolese soldiers</a> imprisoned for <a href="/wiki/Desertion" title="Desertion">desertion</a>. <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://www.reuters.com/world/africa/rebel-advance-causes-panic-congolese-border-town-uvira-2025-02-19/">(Reuters)</a></li></ul></li>
<li>Following a meeting between <a href="/wiki/President_of_Chad" class="mw-redirect" title="President of Chad">Chadian president</a> <a href="/wiki/Mahamat_D%C3%A9by" title="Mahamat Déby">Mahamat Déby</a> and a diplomatic envoy of <a href="/wiki/President_of_the_Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo" title="President of the Democratic Republic of the Congo">Congolese president</a> <a href="/wiki/F%C3%A9lix_Tshisekedi" title="Félix Tshisekedi">Félix Tshisekedi</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Government_of_the_Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo" title="Government of the Democratic Republic of the Congo">Congolese government</a> formally requests military support from <a href="/wiki/Chad" title="Chad">Chad</a> in their ongoing conflict against the M23 movement. <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://www.aa.com.tr/en/africa/dr-congo-seeks-chad-s-support-to-combat-m23-rebels-report/3486999">(Anadolou Agency)</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/United_Nations_Security_Council" title="United Nations Security Council">United Nations Security Council</a> holds an emergency meeting in response to rapid territorial gains made by M23 rebels, with U.N. special envoy for Congo <a href="/wiki/Bintou_Keita" title="Bintou Keita">Bintou Keita</a> saying the council needed to take "urgent and decisive steps to avert a wider regional war." <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://apnews.com/article/un-congo-m23-rwanda-congo-1bdddcf00dd15e0e4898fb0197e96920">(AP)</a></li></ul></li></ul></li>
