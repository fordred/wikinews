#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [Turkey–Islamic State conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turkey%E2%80%93Islamic_State_conflict "Turkey–Islamic State conflict")
  * Three [police officers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Law_enforcement_in_Turkey "Law enforcement in Turkey") and six [Islamic State](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_State "Islamic State") gunmen are killed in a [shootout](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shootout "Shootout") during a raid at a house in Elmalık, [Yalova Province](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yalova_Province "Yalova Province"), [Turkey](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turkey "Turkey"). Eight other officers and a civilian are also wounded. [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2025/12/29/seven-turkish-police-officers-wounded-in-clash-with-isil-fighters-report)

#### Disasters and accidents

[(*Daily Express*)](https://www.express.co.uk/news/world/2151481/panticosa-ski-resort-avalanche-horror)
* Two people are killed and British [boxer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boxing "Boxing") [Anthony Joshua](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anthony_Joshua "Anthony Joshua") is injured when a car crashes into a stationary truck on the [Ogun–Lagos state expressway](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lagos%E2%80%93Abeokuta_Expressway "Lagos–Abeokuta Expressway") near [Lagos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lagos "Lagos"), [Nigeria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nigeria "Nigeria"). #### International relations

* [Cross-strait relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cross-strait_relations "Cross-strait relations")
  * [China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China "China") [conducts](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Justice_Mission_2025 "Justice Mission 2025") the largest [live fire exercises](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Justice_Mission_2025 "Justice Mission 2025") to date around [Taiwan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taiwan "Taiwan"), designating multiple sea and air zones for drills by [naval](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/People%27s_Liberation_Army_Navy "People's Liberation Army Navy") and [air units](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/People%27s_Liberation_Army_Air_Force "People's Liberation Army Air Force") to rehearse [blockade](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blockade "Blockade") and to signal opposition to recent [United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States") [arms sales](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arms_industry "Arms industry") to Taiwan. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/china/china-launches-live-firing-drills-around-taiwan-its-biggest-war-games-date-2025-12-30/)

#### Law and crime

* [Corruption in Malaysia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Corruption_in_Malaysia "Corruption in Malaysia")
  * The [Malaysian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malaysia "Malaysia") [Anti-Corruption Commission](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malaysian_Anti-Corruption_Commission "Malaysian Anti-Corruption Commission") raids multiple companies and [freezes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asset_freezing "Asset freezing") six bank accounts while investigating alleged [bribery](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bribery "Bribery") linked to [army](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malaysian_Army "Malaysian Army") [procurement](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Military_acquisition "Military acquisition") contracts, as [army chief](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chief_of_Army_%28Malaysia%29 "Chief of Army (Malaysia)") [Muhammad Hafizuddeain Jantan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muhammad_Hafizuddeain_Jantan "Muhammad Hafizuddeain Jantan") is placed [on leave](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leave_of_absence "Leave of absence") pending the inquiry. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/malaysia-raids-several-firms-part-anti-graft-probe-into-army-procurement-2025-12-29/)
* [War crimes in the Syrian civil war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/War_crimes_in_the_Syrian_civil_war "War crimes in the Syrian civil war")
  * The [Syrian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syria "Syria") [transitional government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syrian_transitional_government "Syrian transitional government") orders the [military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syrian_Armed_Forces "Syrian Armed Forces") to secure a [mass grave](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mass_graves_in_Syria "Mass graves in Syria") at a former military base near [Al-Dumayr](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Al-Dumayr "Al-Dumayr"), [Rif Dimashq Governorate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rif_Dimashq_Governorate "Rif Dimashq Governorate"), and opens a criminal investigation into a [covert operation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Covert_operation "Covert operation") that transferred thousands of bodies there from another site during [the previous administration](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Presidency_of_Bashar_al-Assad "Presidency of Bashar al-Assad"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/middle-east/syria-secures-assad-era-mass-grave-revealed-by-reuters-opens-criminal-2025-12-29/)
* [President](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Russia "President of Russia") [Vladimir Putin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vladimir_Putin "Vladimir Putin") signs a [law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Law_of_Russia "Law of Russia") allowing [Russia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia "Russia") to disregard criminal judgments issued by foreign or [international courts](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_court "International court") that lack a treaty basis or a [United Nations Security Council](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations_Security_Council "United Nations Security Council") mandate. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/putin-amends-law-let-russia-ignore-foreign-criminal-courts-2025-12-29/)
