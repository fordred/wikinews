#### Disasters and accidents

* [2025 Fez buildings collapse](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Fez_buildings_collapse "2025 Fez buildings collapse")
  * Nineteen people are killed and 16 more injured during the collapse of two adjacent buildings in [Fez](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fez%2C_Morocco "Fez, Morocco"), [Morocco](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Morocco "Morocco"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/africa/least-19-killed-collapse-two-buildings-moroccos-fes-state-news-agency-says-2025-12-10/)

#### Politics and elections

* [Arrest of Jair Bolsonaro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arrest_of_Jair_Bolsonaro "Arrest of Jair Bolsonaro")
  * The [Brazilian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brazil "Brazil") [Chamber of Deputies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chamber_of_Deputies_%28Brazil%29 "Chamber of Deputies (Brazil)") approves a bill that reduces penalties for certain [crimes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crime_in_Brazil "Crime in Brazil"), including attempted [coup](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coup_d%27%C3%A9tat "Coup d'état"), which could significantly shorten former [president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Brazil "President of Brazil") [Jair Bolsonaro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jair_Bolsonaro "Jair Bolsonaro")'s 27-year prison sentence.
