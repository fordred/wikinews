#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [Israeli invasion of Syria (2024–present)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli_invasion_of_Syria_%282024%E2%80%93present%29 "Israeli invasion of Syria (2024–present)")
  * The [Israeli military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli_Defense_Forces "Israeli Defense Forces") launches a raid in the city of [Beit Jinn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beit_Jinn "Beit Jinn"), [Syria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syria "Syria"), targeting unknown gunman and carrying out [airstrikes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airstrike "Airstrike"), killing 13 [Syrians](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syrians "Syrians"), including 2 children, and wounding 25 others. Six Israeli soldiers are also wounded in a [shootout](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shootout "Shootout") with residents. Isreal says it was targeting [Jaama Islamiya](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_Group_%28Lebanon%29 "Islamic Group (Lebanon)"). [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2025/11/28/israeli-forces-syrians-clash-in-damascus-countryside-casualties-reported) [(BBC)](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp89k31krz4o) [(*The Jerusalem Post*)](https://www.jpost.com/israel-news/defense-news/article-876513)
