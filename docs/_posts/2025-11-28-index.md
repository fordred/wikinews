#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [Syrian conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syrian_conflict_%282024%E2%80%93present%29 "Syrian conflict (2024–present)"), [Israeli invasion of Syria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli_invasion_of_Syria_%282024%E2%80%93present%29 "Israeli invasion of Syria (2024–present)")
  * The [Israeli military](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli_Defense_Forces "Israeli Defense Forces") launches a raid in [Beit Jinn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beit_Jinn "Beit Jinn"), [Syria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syria "Syria"), targeting unknown gunmen and carrying out [airstrikes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airstrike "Airstrike"), killing 13 [Syrians](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syrians "Syrians"), including two children, and wounding 25 others. Six Israeli soldiers are also wounded in a [shootout](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shootout "Shootout") with local residents. Israel says it was targeting [Jaama Islamiya](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_Group_%28Lebanon%29 "Islamic Group (Lebanon)"). [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2025/11/28/israeli-forces-syrians-clash-in-damascus-countryside-casualties-reported) [(BBC News)](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp89k31krz4o) [(*The Jerusalem Post*)](https://www.jpost.com/israel-news/defense-news/article-876513)

#### Science and technology

* [2025 Airbus A320 software update](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Airbus_A320_software_update "2025 Airbus A320 software update")
  * [Airbus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airbus "Airbus") launches an emergency [software update](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Software_update "Software update") on [A320](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airbus_A320 "Airbus A320") aircraft worldwide to prevent potential corruption to guidance systems caused by intense solar radiation, affecting 6000 aeroplanes. This update is expected to cause major travel disruptions, especially given the [American holiday of Thanksgiving](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thanksgiving_%28United_States%29 "Thanksgiving (United States)"). [(BBC News)](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/live/cvg4y6g74ert)
* A team of scientists announce the detection of [electric discharges](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electric_discharge "Electric discharge") in the [atmosphere of Mars](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atmosphere_of_Mars "Atmosphere of Mars") for the first time by [NASA](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/NASA "NASA")'s [*Perseverance*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Perseverance_%28rover%29 "Perseverance (rover)") [rover](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mars_rover "Mars rover"), detected near the [Jezero crater](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jezero_%28crater%29 "Jezero (crater)") region; a phenomenon described as "mini lightning", though not confirmed to be the same as actual [lightning](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lightning "Lightning"). [(BBC News)](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c0q5w13e58zo)
