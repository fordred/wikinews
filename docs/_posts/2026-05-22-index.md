#### Health and environment

* [2026 Ituri Province Ebola epidemic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Ituri_Province_Ebola_epidemic "2026 Ituri Province Ebola epidemic")
  * The [World Health Organization](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_Health_Organization "World Health Organization") raises its assessment of the risk from the [Ebola](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ebola "Ebola") outbreak within the [Democratic Republic of the Congo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo "Democratic Republic of the Congo") (DRC) from "high" to "very high" due to its rapid spread, while keeping it "low" at the global level. [Director-general](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Director-General_of_the_World_Health_Organization "Director-General of the World Health Organization") [Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tedros_Adhanom_Ghebreyesus "Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus") says that the outbreak in [Uganda](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uganda "Uganda") is stable. [(AA)](https://www.aa.com.tr/en/africa/who-chief-says-ebola-situation-in-dr-congo-deeply-worrisome/3945711) [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/congo-ebola-outbreak-who-4e08d8df6d9c34039a9e0b8bad7a8954)
  * The [Ituri](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ituri_Province "Ituri Province") provincial government orders funerals to be conducted only by specialized personnel, limits public gatherings to no more than fifty people, and suspends the local [football](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Association_football "Association football") league. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/business/healthcare-pharmaceuticals/un-sends-emergency-funds-staff-tackle-congo-ebola-crisis-2026-05-22/)
  * [Rwanda](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rwanda "Rwanda") imposes a [travel ban](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Travel_ban "Travel ban") on foreign nationals that have been to the DRC in the past thirty days, and requires a [quarantine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quarantine "Quarantine") for [Rwandan citizens](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rwandan_nationality_law "Rwandan nationality law") or residents who have been there. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/business/healthcare-pharmaceuticals/un-sends-emergency-funds-staff-tackle-congo-ebola-crisis-2026-05-22/)
  * [United Nations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations "United Nations") [emergency relief coordinator](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Under-Secretary-General_for_Humanitarian_Affairs_and_Emergency_Relief_Coordinator "Under-Secretary-General for Humanitarian Affairs and Emergency Relief Coordinator") [Tom Fletcher](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tom_Fletcher_%28diplomat%29 "Tom Fletcher (diplomat)") announces that the organization is providing [US$](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_dollar "United States dollar")60 million in funding for the outbreak response.
