* [2025 Polish presidential election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Polish_presidential_election "2025 Polish presidential election")

As no candidates received more than 50% of the vote, a [runoff](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Runoff_voting "Runoff voting") between the two candidates is expected to be held on June 1. Left-wing candidates gathered over 9%.
