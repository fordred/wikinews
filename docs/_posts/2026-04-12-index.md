  * [Hungarians](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungarians "Hungarians") vote to elect all 199 members of the [National Assembly](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Assembly_%28Hungary%29 "National Assembly (Hungary)"). The incumbent [prime minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_Hungary "Prime Minister of Hungary"), [Viktor Orbán](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Viktor_Orb%C3%A1n "Viktor Orbán"), who was seeking a fifth consecutive term in office, [concedes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Concession_%28politics%29 "Concession (politics)") defeat and calls [TISZA](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tisza_Party "Tisza Party") leader [Péter Magyar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/P%C3%A9ter_Magyar "Péter Magyar") on the telephone to congratulate him on his victory. The Pro-European [Tisza Party](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tisza_Party "Tisza Party") wins two-thirds of the seats in Hungary’s legislature.
