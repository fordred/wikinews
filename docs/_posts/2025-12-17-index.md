#### Politics and elections

* [Arrest of Jair Bolsonaro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arrest_of_Jair_Bolsonaro "Arrest of Jair Bolsonaro")
  * The [Brazilian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brazil "Brazil") [Senate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Federal_Senate_%28Brazil%29 "Federal Senate (Brazil)") approves a bill that reduces penalties for certain [crimes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crime_in_Brazil "Crime in Brazil"), including attempted [coup](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coup_d%27%C3%A9tat "Coup d'état"), which could significantly shorten former [president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Brazil "President of Brazil") [Jair Bolsonaro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jair_Bolsonaro "Jair Bolsonaro")'s 27-year prison sentence. The bill now heads to [President](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Brazil "President of Brazil") [Luiz Inácio Lula da Silva](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Luiz_In%C3%A1cio_Lula_da_Silva "Luiz Inácio Lula da Silva") for signature. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/brazil-jair-bolsonaro-prison-sentence-senate-27fb5a646fb6c33353cf11eac3e7ea92)
* [Russo-Ukrainian war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russo-Ukrainian_war "Russo-Ukrainian war")
  * [Russian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia "Russia") [president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Russia "President of Russia") [Vladimir Putin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vladimir_Putin "Vladimir Putin") signs a [law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Law_of_Russia "Law of Russia") to redistribute ownerless homes on [occupied territories](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian-occupied_territories_of_Ukraine "Russian-occupied territories of Ukraine") to those who have lost homes during the conflict. [(Euromaidan Press)](https://euromaidanpress.com/2025/12/17/putin-signs-law-to-seize-ukrainian-homes-on-occupied-territories-until-2030/)
* [Brazil](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brazil "Brazil")'s [Supreme Federal Court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_Federal_Court "Supreme Federal Court") rules that [Indigenous land rights](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indigenous_land_rights "Indigenous land rights") constitute unamendable [constitutional](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constitution_of_Brazil "Constitution of Brazil") guarantees, thereby blocking [congressional](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brazilian_National_Congress "Brazilian National Congress") efforts to restrict the recognition of [Indigenous territories](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indigenous_territory_%28Brazil%29 "Indigenous territory (Brazil)") and reinforcing existing protections despite pending legislative opposition. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/brazil-supreme-court-affirms-indigenous-land-rights-defying-congress-2025-12-17/)
