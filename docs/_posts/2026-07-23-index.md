    * The [Houthis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Houthis "Houthis") claim to have attacked two [Saudi Arabian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saudi_Arabia "Saudi Arabia") oil tankers in the [Red Sea](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Sea "Red Sea") for their violation of the blockade by [Yemen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yemen "Yemen")'s Houthi-led [armed forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yemeni_Armed_Forces "Yemeni Armed Forces"). [(*The Standard*)](https://www.thestandard.com.hk/china/article/337997/Two-Chinese-supertankers-with-Saudi-oil-head-to-Bab-el-Mandeb-for-Red-Sea-exit)
    * [Reuters](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reuters "Reuters") reports that Iran sent [Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_Revolutionary_Guard_Corps "Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps") officers, along with [drone](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Unmanned_aerial_vehicles_in_the_Iranian_military "Unmanned aerial vehicles in the Iranian military")- and [missile](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ballistic_missile_program_of_Iran "Ballistic missile program of Iran")-related equipment, on a [Mahan Air](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mahan_Air "Mahan Air") flight to [Houthi-controlled Yemen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Houthi-controlled_Yemen "Houthi-controlled Yemen") on July 13, in preparation for the blockade on Saudi Arabia.
