#### WMF History

* [Wikinews](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikinews "Wikinews"), the free news source, closes indefinitely after 21 years of service. Existing pages on the site will remain accessible, but from now on, no new editing or page creation can be done on the site. [(Wikinews)](https://en.wikinews.org/wiki/Wikimedia_Foundation_closes_Wikinews_after_21_years)
