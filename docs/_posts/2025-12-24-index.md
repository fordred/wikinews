* An [explosive device](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Explosive_device "Explosive device") kills two [police officers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moscow_City_Police "Moscow City Police") and one civilian in [Moscow](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moscow "Moscow"), [Russia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia "Russia"), near where Russian lieutenant general [Fanil Sarvarov](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fanil_Sarvarov "Fanil Sarvarov") was killed in a separate bombing two days prior.
