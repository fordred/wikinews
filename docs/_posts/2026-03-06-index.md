#### Politics and elections

* [2026 Kosovan presidential election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Kosovan_presidential_election "2026 Kosovan presidential election"), [2026 Kosovan parliamentary election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Kosovan_parliamentary_election "2026 Kosovan parliamentary election")
  * [Kosovo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kosovo "Kosovo") [President](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Kosovo "President of Kosovo") [Vjosa Osmani](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vjosa_Osmani "Vjosa Osmani") dissolves the [Kuvendi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Assembly_of_the_Republic_of_Kosovo "Assembly of the Republic of Kosovo"), attempting to set new parliamentary elections within 45 days. [Vetëvendosje](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vet%C3%ABvendosje "Vetëvendosje") maintains that the constitution allows parliament 60 more days to try to elect a president, seeking to challenge Osmani's decree to [dissolve](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dissolution_of_parliament "Dissolution of parliament") the Kuvendi at the [Constitutional Court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constitutional_Court_of_Kosovo "Constitutional Court of Kosovo").
