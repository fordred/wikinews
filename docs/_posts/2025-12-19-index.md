#### Business and economy

* [Restrictions on TikTok in the United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Restrictions_on_TikTok_in_the_United_States "Restrictions on TikTok in the United States")
  * Social media platform [TikTok](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/TikTok "TikTok") announces a deal to sell 80.1% of its [United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States")-based assets to a consortium of companies, including [Oracle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oracle_Corporation "Oracle Corporation"), [Silver Lake](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Silver_Lake_%28investment_firm%29 "Silver Lake (investment firm)"), [MGX](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MGX_Fund_Management_Limited "MGX Fund Management Limited"), and existing [ByteDance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ByteDance "ByteDance") investors, to avoid a ban in the US.
