#### Politics and elections

* [2026 Bulgarian parliamentary election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Bulgarian_parliamentary_election "2026 Bulgarian parliamentary election"), [Bulgarian political crisis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2021%E2%80%93present_Bulgarian_political_crisis "2021–present Bulgarian political crisis")
  * [Bulgarians](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bulgarians "Bulgarians") vote for 240 seats of the [National Assembly](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Assembly_%28Bulgaria%29 "National Assembly (Bulgaria)") in the nation's eighth election since [April 2021](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/April_2021_Bulgarian_parliamentary_election "April 2021 Bulgarian parliamentary election"). Former [president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Bulgaria "President of Bulgaria") [Rumen Radev](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rumen_Radev "Rumen Radev") leads the [soft eurosceptic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soft_eurosceptic "Soft eurosceptic") [Progressive Bulgaria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Progressive_Bulgaria "Progressive Bulgaria") coalition, which is seen as the [frontrunner](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Front-runner "Front-runner") for the election according to opinion polls.
