#### Science and technology

* [Chinese space program](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chinese_space_program "Chinese space program")
  * The [Shenzhou 22](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shenzhou_22 "Shenzhou 22") spacecraft enters [orbit](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geocentric_orbit "Geocentric orbit") after [China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China_National_Space_Administration "China National Space Administration") conducts its first emergency space launch to restore a safe return option for the three astronauts aboard the [Tiangong space station](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tiangong_space_station "Tiangong space station") following damage to the [Shenzhou 20](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shenzhou_20 "Shenzhou 20") return capsule earlier in November. The uncrewed spacecraft, launched on a [Long March 2F](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Long_March_2F "Long March 2F") rocket, carries replacement parts and supplies and aims to return the current crew to [Earth](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth "Earth") in 2026. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/business/media-telecom/china-launch-shenzhou-22-spaceship-0411-gmt-state-media-reports-2025-11-25/)
