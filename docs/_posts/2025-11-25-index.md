#### Business and economy

* [Insurance in Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Insurance_in_Australia "Insurance in Australia")
  * The [Australian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia "Australia") [Federal Court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Federal_Court_of_Australia "Federal Court of Australia") fines United Super, the [Cbus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cbus_%28superannuation_fund%29 "Cbus (superannuation fund)") [pension fund](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pension_fund "Pension fund")'s [trustee](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trustee "Trustee"), [A$](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australian_dollar "Australian dollar")23.5 million ([US$](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_dollar "United States dollar")15.2 million) for systemic failures that caused extensive delays in processing [death](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Life_insurance "Life insurance") and [disability insurance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Disability_insurance "Disability insurance") claims affecting over 7,000 members. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/australian-pension-fund-cbus-fined-over-15-million-death-disability-claim-delays-2025-11-25/)

#### Law and crime

* [LGBTQ rights in the European Union](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LGBTQ_rights_in_the_European_Union "LGBTQ rights in the European Union"), [Same-sex union legislation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Same-sex_union_legislation "Same-sex union legislation")
  * The [European Court of Justice](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/European_Court_of_Justice "European Court of Justice"), the highest court in the [European Union](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/European_Union "European Union"), rules for all countries in the bloc to [recognize](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Recognition_of_same-sex_unions_in_Europe "Recognition of same-sex unions in Europe") [same-sex marriage](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Same-sex_marriage "Same-sex marriage") and not discriminate against [same-sex](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Homosexuality "Homosexuality") couples. The ruling does not force individual countries to legalize same-sex marriages. [(DW)](https://www.dw.com/en/eu-same-sex-marriage-lgbtq-poland/a-74876738)

#### Science and technology

* [Chinese space program](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chinese_space_program "Chinese space program")
  * The [Shenzhou 22](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shenzhou_22 "Shenzhou 22") spacecraft enters [orbit](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geocentric_orbit "Geocentric orbit") after [China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China_National_Space_Administration "China National Space Administration") conducts its first emergency space launch to restore a [safe return option](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Launch_escape_system "Launch escape system") for the three astronauts aboard the [Tiangong space station](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tiangong_space_station "Tiangong space station") following damage to the [Shenzhou 20](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shenzhou_20 "Shenzhou 20") return capsule earlier in November. The uncrewed spacecraft, launched on a [Long March 2F](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Long_March_2F "Long March 2F") rocket, carries replacement parts and supplies and aims to return the current crew to [Earth](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth "Earth") in 2026. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/business/media-telecom/china-launch-shenzhou-22-spaceship-0411-gmt-state-media-reports-2025-11-25/)
