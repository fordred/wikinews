#### Disasters and accidents

* Twelve people are killed and four injured in a fire in [Los Gallardos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Los_Gallardos "Los Gallardos"), [Andalusia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andalusia "Andalusia"), [Spain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spain "Spain"). It is suspected that the fire started after a [power line](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Overhead_power_line "Overhead power line") fell, igniting [dry vegetation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tropical_vegetation "Tropical vegetation") before spreading rapidly through surrounding [woodland](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Woodland "Woodland").
