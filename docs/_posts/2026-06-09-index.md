  * [NASA](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/NASA "NASA") announces that its astronauts [Randy Bresnik](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Randy_Bresnik "Randy Bresnik"), [Frank Rubio](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frank_Rubio "Frank Rubio"), and [Andre Douglas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andre_Douglas "Andre Douglas"), and [ESA](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/European_Space_Agency "European Space Agency") astronaut [Luca Parmitano](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Luca_Parmitano "Luca Parmitano") will crew the [Artemis III](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Artemis_III "Artemis III") mission in late 2027.
