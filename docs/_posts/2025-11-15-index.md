#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [Kivu conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kivu_conflict "Kivu conflict")
  * The [Democratic Republic of the Congo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo "Democratic Republic of the Congo") and the [M23 rebels](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/March_23_Movement "March 23 Movement") sign a [framework agreement](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Framework_agreement "Framework agreement") in [Doha](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Doha "Doha"), [Qatar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qatar "Qatar"), for a [peace treaty](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peace_treaty "Peace treaty") to end the conflict in [Kivu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kivu "Kivu"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/africa/congo-m23-sign-framework-agreement-peace-deal-qatar-2025-11-15/)
* [Syrian conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syrian_conflict_%282024%E2%80%93present%29 "Syrian conflict (2024–present)")
  * Four people are killed, including the mayor of Wady Al-Mawla, and another is severly injured in a [mass shooting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mass_shooting "Mass shooting") attack by unidentified gunmen at a [coffeehouse](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coffeehouse "Coffeehouse") in [Homs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Homs "Homs"), [Syria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syria "Syria"). [(Syrian Observatory for Human Rights)](https://www.syriahr.com/en/373435/) [(SANA)](https://sana.sy/en/governorates-news/homs/2277872/)
  * Three people are injured, including two critically, in a [drive-by shooting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Drive-by_shooting "Drive-by shooting") by unidentified gunmen in [Aleppo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aleppo "Aleppo"), Syria. [(Syrian Observatory for Human Rights)](https://www.syriahr.com/en/373437/)

#### Politics and elections

* [Modern immigration to the United Kingdom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Modern_immigration_to_the_United_Kingdom "Modern immigration to the United Kingdom")
  * [British](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom "United Kingdom") [home secretary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Home_secretary "Home secretary") [Shabana Mahmood](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shabana_Mahmood "Shabana Mahmood") announces plans to reduce the length of [refugee status](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Refugee_status "Refugee status"), extend the required period before applying for [long-term residency](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Right_of_abode_in_the_United_Kingdom "Right of abode in the United Kingdom"), and end automatic [state benefits](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Welfare_state_in_the_United_Kingdom "Welfare state in the United Kingdom") for asylum seekers as part of a broad reform of its asylum system. [(AFP via CBS News)](https://www.cbsnews.com/news/u-k-plans-to-drastically-reduce-protections-for-refugees-overhaul-asylum-policies/)
