#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [Afghanistan–Pakistan war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghanistan%E2%80%93Pakistan_war "Afghanistan–Pakistan war")
  * Clashes break out on the [border between Afghanistan and Pakistan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Durand_Line "Durand Line"), with [Pakistan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pakistan "Pakistan") launching [airstrikes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airstrike "Airstrike") on several locations in [Afghanistan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghanistan "Afghanistan"), including the capital of [Kabul](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kabul "Kabul"), targeting [Taliban](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taliban "Taliban") [government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Government_of_Afghanistan "Government of Afghanistan") forces directly, in what [Pakistani defense minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minister_of_Defence_%28Pakistan%29 "Minister of Defence (Pakistan)") [Khawaja Asif](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Khawaja_Asif "Khawaja Asif") describes as "open war". [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/2/27/pakistan-warplanes-bomb-kabul-as-clashes-with-afghanistan-intensify)
* [Gaza war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_war "Gaza war")
  * [Israeli](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel_Defense_Forces "Israel Defense Forces") [air](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airstrike "Airstrike") and [drone strikes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Drone_warfare "Drone warfare") kill at least five people and injure several others in central and southern [Gaza](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_Strip "Gaza Strip"), despite an ongoing [United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States")-brokered [truce](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_peace_plan "Gaza peace plan") between [Israel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel "Israel") and [Hamas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hamas "Hamas").
