* [2026 Israeli–United States strikes on Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Israeli%E2%80%93United_States_strikes_on_Iran "2026 Israeli–United States strikes on Iran")
  * [Assassination of Ali Khamenei](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Assassination_of_Ali_Khamenei "Assassination of Ali Khamenei")
    * [Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran "Iran") confirms the death of [its supreme leader](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_Leader_of_Iran "Supreme Leader of Iran") [Ali Khamenei](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ali_Khamenei "Ali Khamenei") in a [decapitation strike](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Decapitation_%28military_strategy%29 "Decapitation (military strategy)") yesterday in [Tehran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tehran "Tehran") by [Israel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel "Israel") and the [United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States"). [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/2/28/ayatollah-ali-khamenei-the-leader-who-shaped-irans-defiance)
  * Key Iranian security officials [Abdolrahim Mousavi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abdolrahim_Mousavi "Abdolrahim Mousavi"), [Aziz Nasirzadeh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aziz_Nasirzadeh "Aziz Nasirzadeh"), [Mohammad Pakpour](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mohammad_Pakpour "Mohammad Pakpour") and [Ali Shamkhani](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ali_Shamkhani "Ali Shamkhani") are confirmed killed after an airstrike during a meeting yesterday in Tehran. [(*The Hill*)](https://thehill.com/homenews/ap/ap-international/ap-trump-warns-iran-not-to-escalate-attacks-saying-us-will-strike-back-with-force/mlite/)
  * A person is injured in a [drone strike](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Drone_warfare "Drone warfare") on [Duqm](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Duqm "Duqm") port in [Oman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oman "Oman"). [(AA)](https://www.aa.com.tr/en/middle-east/1-injured-in-drone-strike-on-oman-s-duqm-port-amid-us-israeli-confrontation-with-iran/3844783)
  * Iran launches two [ballistic missiles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ballistic_missile "Ballistic missile") towards [Cyprus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cyprus "Cyprus"), not far from the [British military bases](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akrotiri_and_Dhekelia "Akrotiri and Dhekelia") on the island. #### Politics and elections

* [2026 Iranian leadership crisis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Iranian_leadership_crisis "2026 Iranian leadership crisis")
