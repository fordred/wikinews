#### Law and crime

* Ten people are injured, four critically, in a [vehicle-ramming attack](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vehicle-ramming_attack "Vehicle-ramming attack") in [Oléron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ol%C3%A9ron "Oléron"), [France](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/France "France"). The perpetrator is arrested. [(The Guardian)](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2025/nov/05/car-french-island-ile-d-oleron-france)
* [Japanese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan "Japan") [defense minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minister_of_Defense_%28Japan%29 "Minister of Defense (Japan)") [Shinjiro Koizumi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shinjir%C5%8D_Koizumi "Shinjirō Koizumi") says that [troops](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan_Self-Defense_Forces "Japan Self-Defense Forces") were sent to [Akita Prefecture](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akita_Prefecture "Akita Prefecture") after a series of more than 50 [bear](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bear "Bear") attacks since April, which resulted in 12 deaths and over 100 injuries. Governor Kenta Suzuki requested aid, saying that local authorities lack manpower to handle the situation. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/japan-bear-attack-army-12a2a3233790deeefd9ec379d0121f33)
