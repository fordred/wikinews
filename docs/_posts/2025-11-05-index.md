#### Arts and culture

* The [United Methodist Church](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Methodist_Church "United Methodist Church") ratifies a constitutional amendment restructuring the denomination, enabling each region of the church worldwide to have equal autonomy. Other amendments involve expanding membership allowances to account for gender and ability, and condemning [white supremacy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/White_supremacy "White supremacy") and [colonialism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colonialism "Colonialism"). [(UMNews)](https://www.umnews.org/en/news/church-strengthens-stands-on-inclusion-racism?fbclid=IwZnRzaAN5AatleHRuA2FlbQIxMQBzcnRjBmFwcF9pZAo2NjI4NTY4Mzc5AAEeHbe-wdhZVzIj99vw2e2HT9LLIjjPNQ3PzWY6Z9JKkANL8wHZx-zqTUuL89E_aem_8qe5z4lPEjpdLyJkKBhWLg) [(*The Tennessean*)](https://www.tennessean.com/story/news/religion/2025/11/05/umc-restructuring-vote-results/87069417007/)

#### Law and crime

* [2025 Island of Oléron car ramming](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Island_of_Ol%C3%A9ron_car_ramming "2025 Island of Oléron car ramming")
  * Five people are injured, two critically, in a [vehicle-ramming attack](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vehicle-ramming_attack "Vehicle-ramming attack") on the [French](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/France "France") island of [Oléron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ol%C3%A9ron "Oléron"). Muslim perpetrator attempts to set the car [on fire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arson "Arson"), before being arrested by the [gendarmerie](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Gendarmerie "National Gendarmerie"). [(*The Guardian*)](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2025/nov/05/car-french-island-ile-d-oleron-france)
* [Bolivia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bolivia "Bolivia")'s [Supreme Court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_Tribunal_of_Justice_of_Bolivia "Supreme Tribunal of Justice of Bolivia") overturns the 10-year [prison](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prisons_in_Bolivia "Prisons in Bolivia") sentence of former [president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Bolivia "President of Bolivia") [Jeanine Áñez](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeanine_%C3%81%C3%B1ez "Jeanine Áñez"), ruling that she should have been tried by a [special judicial body](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Extraordinary_court "Extraordinary court") responsible for cases involving officials and ordering her release. [(AFP via France 24)](https://www.france24.com/en/live-news/20251105-bolivia-supreme-court-orders-release-of-jailed-ex-president-jeanine-anez)
* A [court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Judiciary_of_Germany "Judiciary of Germany") in [Aachen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aachen "Aachen"), [North Rhine-Westphalia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Rhine-Westphalia "North Rhine-Westphalia"), [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany "Germany"), sentences a 44-year-old [palliative care](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Palliative_care "Palliative care") nurse to [life imprisonment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Life_imprisonment_in_Germany "Life imprisonment in Germany") for [murdering](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Murder_in_German_law "Murder in German law") 10 patients and attempting to murder 27 others with [lethal injections](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lethal_injection "Lethal injection") at a hospital in [Würselen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/W%C3%BCrselen "Würselen"). [(AFP via CBS News)](https://www.cbsnews.com/news/nurse-life-sentence-murder-10-patients-germany/)
* [Japanese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan "Japan") [defense minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minister_of_Defense_%28Japan%29 "Minister of Defense (Japan)") [Shinjiro Koizumi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shinjir%C5%8D_Koizumi "Shinjirō Koizumi") says that [troops](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan_Self-Defense_Forces "Japan Self-Defense Forces") were sent to [Akita Prefecture](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akita_Prefecture "Akita Prefecture") after a series of more than 50 [bear attacks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bear_attack "Bear attack") since April, which resulted in 12 deaths and over 100 injuries. [Governor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Governor_%28Japan%29 "Governor (Japan)") [Kenta Suzuki](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kenta_Suzuki "Kenta Suzuki") requested aid, saying that local authorities lack manpower to handle the situation. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/japan-bear-attack-army-12a2a3233790deeefd9ec379d0121f33)
* A [Malaysian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malaysia "Malaysia") [high court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/High_court_%28Malaysia%29 "High court (Malaysia)") rules that [police](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Malaysia_Police "Royal Malaysia Police") and [the government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_Najib_cabinet "Second Najib cabinet") are responsible for the [enforced disappearances](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Enforced_disappearances "Enforced disappearances") of activist [Amri Che Mat](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amri_Che_Mat "Amri Che Mat") and pastor [Raymond Koh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Raymond_Koh "Raymond Koh") in 2016 and 2017, respectively, ordering investigations to be reopened and awarding [damages](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Damages "Damages") exceeding [RM](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malaysian_ringgit "Malaysian ringgit") 33 million ([US$](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_dollar "United States dollar")8 million) to their families. The decision marks the first judicial finding of state involvement in [enforced disappearances in Malaysia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Human_rights_in_Malaysia "Human rights in Malaysia"). [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/malaysia-religious-abduction-enforced-disappearance-dcd95173a461162fd382b8f31abd2e31)
