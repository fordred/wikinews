#### Law and crime

* Ten people are injured, four critically, in a [vehicle-ramming attack](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vehicle-ramming_attack "Vehicle-ramming attack") in [Oléron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ol%C3%A9ron "Oléron"), [France](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/France "France"). The perpetrator attempts to set the car [on fire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arson "Arson"), before being arrested by the [gendarmerie](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gendarmerie "Gendarmerie"). [(The Guardian)](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2025/nov/05/car-french-island-ile-d-oleron-france)
* [Japanese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan "Japan") [defense minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minister_of_Defense_%28Japan%29 "Minister of Defense (Japan)") [Shinjiro Koizumi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shinjir%C5%8D_Koizumi "Shinjirō Koizumi") says that [troops](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan_Self-Defense_Forces "Japan Self-Defense Forces") were sent to [Akita Prefecture](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akita_Prefecture "Akita Prefecture") after a series of more than 50 [bear attacks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bear_attack "Bear attack") since April, which resulted in 12 deaths and over 100 injuries. [Governor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Governor_%28Japan%29 "Governor (Japan)") Kenta Suzuki [[ja](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E9%88%B4%E6%9C%A8%E5%81%A5%E5%A4%AA_%28%E6%94%BF%E6%B2%BB%E5%AE%B6%29 "ja:鈴木健太 (政治家)")] requested aid, saying that local authorities lack manpower to handle the situation.
