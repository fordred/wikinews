#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [2026 Iran war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Iran_war "2026 Iran war")
  * [Khatam al-Anbiya Central Headquarters](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Khatam_al-Anbiya_Central_Headquarters "Khatam al-Anbiya Central Headquarters") spokesperson [Ebrahim Zolfaghari](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ebrahim_Zolfaghari "Ebrahim Zolfaghari") accuses the [United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States") of violating the [ceasefire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ceasefire "Ceasefire") after it targeted two ships at the [Strait of Hormuz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strait_of_Hormuz "Strait of Hormuz") and strikes in the [Hormozgan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hormozgan_province "Hormozgan province") [province](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Provinces_of_Iran "Provinces of Iran") including [Bandar Khamir](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bandar_Khamir "Bandar Khamir"), [Sirik](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bandar_Sirik "Bandar Sirik"), and [Qeshm Island](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qeshm_Island "Qeshm Island") yesterday.
