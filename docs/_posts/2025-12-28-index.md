#### Politics and elections

* [2025 Central African general election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Central_African_general_election "2025 Central African general election")
  * Citizens of the [Central African Republic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Central_African_Republic "Central African Republic") vote to elect a [president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_heads_of_state_of_the_Central_African_Republic "List of heads of state of the Central African Republic") and 140 members of the [National Assembly](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Assembly_%28Central_African_Republic%29 "National Assembly (Central African Republic)"). The incumbent [Faustin-Archange Touadéra](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Faustin-Archange_Touad%C3%A9ra "Faustin-Archange Touadéra") seeks a third term in office. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/africa/central-african-republic-votes-russia-ally-touadera-seeks-third-term-2025-12-28/)
* [2025 Guinean presidential election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Guinean_presidential_election "2025 Guinean presidential election")
  * Citizens of [Guinea](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guinea "Guinea") vote for a [president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Guinea "President of Guinea") as incumbent [Mamady Doumbouya](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mamady_Doumbouya "Mamady Doumbouya") seeks to be elected four years after leading a [coup](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2021_Guinean_coup_d%27%C3%A9tat "2021 Guinean coup d'état"). [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/africa/guinea-votes-presidential-election-expected-cement-doumbouyas-rule-2025-12-28/)
* [2025–26 Myanmar general election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025%E2%80%9326_Myanmar_general_election "2025–26 Myanmar general election")
  * Citizens of [Myanmar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Myanmar "Myanmar") vote in the first phase to elect the nation's [legislature](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pyidaungsu_Hluttaw "Pyidaungsu Hluttaw"), held under the supervision of a [military junta](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/State_Security_and_Peace_Commission "State Security and Peace Commission") amidst a [civil war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Myanmar_civil_war_%282021%E2%80%93present%29 "Myanmar civil war (2021–present)"). [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/myanmar-election-yangon-suu-kyi-hlaing-military-f361d4962d30b9c5c4741110bb1271ef)
* [December 2025 Kosovan parliamentary election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/December_2025_Kosovan_parliamentary_election "December 2025 Kosovan parliamentary election")
  * Citizens of [Kosovo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kosovo "Kosovo") vote for the second time in a year to elect the members of the [Kuvendi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Assembly_of_the_Republic_of_Kosovo "Assembly of the Republic of Kosovo"). [(AP via ABC News)](https://abcnews.go.com/International/wireStory/kosovo-heads-snap-vote-end-political-deadlock-128716540)
