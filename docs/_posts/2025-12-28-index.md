#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [Gaza war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_war "Gaza war")
  * [Israel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel "Israel") introduces new rules for [aid groups](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aid_organization "Aid organization"), forbidding activities or criticism that ["delegitimizes" Israel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Legitimacy_of_the_State_of_Israel "Legitimacy of the State of Israel"), without providing specific definitions. Aid groups say that the requirements are political and may hinder [criticism of Israel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Criticism_of_Israel "Criticism of Israel")'s actions in [Gaza](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_Strip "Gaza Strip"). [(ABC News)](https://www.abc.net.au/news/2025-12-29/aid-agencies-fear-lifesaving-program-blockage-into-gaza-/106181964)
* [Syrian conflict](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syrian_conflict_%282024%E2%80%93present%29 "Syrian conflict (2024–present)")
  * [Western Syria clashes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Western_Syria_clashes "Western Syria clashes")
    * At least three people are killed and dozens of others are injured after gunfire erupts during [Alawite](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alawites "Alawites")-led protests in [Latakia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Latakia "Latakia"), [Syria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syria "Syria"), demanding [decentralization](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Decentralization "Decentralization") and for the [transitional government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syrian_transitional_government "Syrian transitional government") to release [Assadist](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Assadist "Assadist") detainees. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/business/media-telecom/three-dead-alawite-protests-syrian-coast-local-officials-say-2025-12-28/)
* [Ecuadorian security crisis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ecuadorian_security_crisis "Ecuadorian security crisis")
  * Six people, including a two-year-old girl, are killed and three others are injured in a [mass shooting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mass_shooting "Mass shooting") at a beach in [Puerto López](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Puerto_L%C3%B3pez "Puerto López"), [Manabí Province](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manab%C3%AD_Province "Manabí Province"), [Ecuador](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ecuador "Ecuador").
