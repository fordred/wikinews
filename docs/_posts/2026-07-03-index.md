[Saky air base](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saky_%28air_base%29 "Saky (air base)") and [Gvardeyskoye air base](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gvardeyskoye_%28air_base%29 "Gvardeyskoye (air base)") are also targeted with seven jets destroyed or damaged.
