  * The [Nigerian Army](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nigerian_Army "Nigerian Army") kills nineteen armed bandits in a [shootout](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shootout "Shootout") in [Shanono](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shanono "Shanono"), [Kano State](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kano_State "Kano State"), [Nigeria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nigeria "Nigeria"). Two Nigerian soldiers and a local vigilante fighting alongside the military are also killed during the clashes. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/africa/nigerian-army-says-19-bandits-killed-kano-clash-troops-among-dead-2025-11-04/)

#### Disasters and accidents

* [2025 Atlantic hurricane season](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Atlantic_hurricane_season "2025 Atlantic hurricane season")
  * The casualty toll from [Hurricane Melissa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hurricane_Melissa "Hurricane Melissa") in [Haiti](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Haiti "Haiti") increases to 43 deaths, with 13 others still missing. Nearly 12,000 homes are flooded and at least 200 have been destroyed. [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/melissa-haiti-jamaica-hurricane-storm-caribbean-bbc009020e6a2a8150ccecc5b6370833)
* [2025 Pacific typhoon season](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Pacific_typhoon_season "2025 Pacific typhoon season")
  * The death toll from [Typhoon Kalmaegi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Typhoon_Kalmaegi_%282025%29 "Typhoon Kalmaegi (2025)")'s impact on [Visayas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Visayas "Visayas") and [Mindanao](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mindanao "Mindanao"), [Philippines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippines "Philippines"), rises to 40, with [search and rescue](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Search_and_rescue "Search and rescue") operations ongoing. [(AP)](https://www.dw.com/en/typhoon-kalmaegi-kills-scores-in-central-philippines/a-74605322)
  * Six people are killed when a [Philippine Air Force](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippine_Air_Force "Philippine Air Force") [Bell Huey](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bell_Huey_family "Bell Huey family") helicopter crashes while conducting damage assessment for Typhoon Kalmaegi in [Agusan del Sur](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agusan_del_Sur "Agusan del Sur"), [Caraga](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caraga "Caraga"), Philippines. [(*Philippine Daily Inquirer*)](https://newsinfo.inquirer.net/2134041/paf-chopper-with-6-on-board-crashes-in-agusan-del-sur-source)
* [2025 Chhattisgarh train collision](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Chhattisgarh_train_collision "2025 Chhattisgarh train collision")
  * At least eight people are killed and about a dozen others are injured when a [passenger train](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Passenger_train "Passenger train") [crashes into](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Railway_accident "Railway accident") a [cargo train](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cargo_train "Cargo train") near [Bilaspur](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bilaspur%2C_Chhattisgarh "Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh"), [Chhattisgarh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chhattisgarh "Chhattisgarh"), [India](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/India "India"). [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/india-train-crash-bilaspur-fatalities-14dea2aaf55b2fee62d9839569a6ec84)
