#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [2025 Beninese coup attempt](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Beninese_coup_attempt "2025 Beninese coup attempt")
  * Elements of the [Benin Armed Forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Benin_Armed_Forces "Benin Armed Forces") announce a [coup d'état](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coup_d%27%C3%A9tat "Coup d'état") against [president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Benin "President of Benin") [Patrice Talon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Patrice_Talon "Patrice Talon") and the creation of a [military junta](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Military_junta "Military junta"), the Military Committee for Refoundation, after seizing [state television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/B%C3%A9nin_TV "Bénin TV"). The coup attempt is ultimately unsuccessful. [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2025/12/7/soldiers-appear-on-benin-state-television-announcing-apparent-coup)
  * The [Nigerian Air Force](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nigerian_Air_Force "Nigerian Air Force") launches precision [airstrikes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airstrike "Airstrike") against pro-coup forces in [Cotonou](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cotonou "Cotonou") in support of the [Beninese government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Politics_of_Benin "Politics of Benin"). [(Sahara Reporters)](https://saharareporters.com/2025/12/07/nigerian-air-force-launches-precision-airstrikes-fleeing-benin-republic-coup-plotters)

#### Sports

* [2025 FIFA Futsal Women's World Cup](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_FIFA_Futsal_Women%27s_World_Cup "2025 FIFA Futsal Women's World Cup")
  * In [futsal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Futsal "Futsal"), [Brazil](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brazil_women%27s_national_futsal_team "Brazil women's national futsal team") wins the inaugural [FIFA Futsal Women's World Cup](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/FIFA_Futsal_Women%27s_World_Cup "FIFA Futsal Women's World Cup") after defeating [Portugal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portugal_women%27s_national_futsal_team "Portugal women's national futsal team") 3–0 in the final held in [Metro Manila](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Metro_Manila "Metro Manila"), [Philippines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippines "Philippines"). [Emilly](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emilly "Emilly") is awarded the tournament's [Golden Ball](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Golden_Ball_%28FIFA_World_Cup%29 "Golden Ball (FIFA World Cup)"). [(GMA News)](https://www.gmanetwork.com/news/sports/football/968768/brazil-beats-portugal-to-take-home-first-ever-fifa-futsal-women-s-world-cup-title/story/)
* [2025 Formula One World Championship](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Formula_One_World_Championship "2025 Formula One World Championship")
  * In [auto racing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Auto_racing "Auto racing"), [McLaren](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/McLaren "McLaren")'s [Lando Norris](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lando_Norris "Lando Norris") wins his first [World Drivers' Championship](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions "List of Formula One World Drivers' Champions") after outscoring the [defending champion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2024_Formula_One_World_Championship "2024 Formula One World Championship"), [Red Bull](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing "Red Bull Racing")'s [Max Verstappen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Max_Verstappen "Max Verstappen"), by two points at the end of the season's [final race](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix "2025 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix") in [Abu Dhabi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abu_Dhabi "Abu Dhabi"), [United Arab Emirates](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Arab_Emirates "United Arab Emirates").
