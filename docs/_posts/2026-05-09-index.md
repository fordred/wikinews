#### Politics and elections

* [2026 Farrer by-election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Farrer_by-election "2026 Farrer by-election")
  * [One Nation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/One_Nation "One Nation") candidate [David Farley](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Farley_%28politician%29 "David Farley (politician)") is elected in [Farrer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Division_of_Farrer "Division of Farrer"), marking the first time a candidate of One Nation was elected to the [Australian House of Representatives](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australian_House_of_Representatives "Australian House of Representatives"). [(ABC News Australia)](https://www.abc.net.au/news/2026-05-09/farrer-sat-night-results/106656856)
* [2026 Hungarian parliamentary election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Hungarian_parliamentary_election "2026 Hungarian parliamentary election")
  * The [National Assembly](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Assembly_%28Hungary%29 "National Assembly (Hungary)") of [Hungary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungary "Hungary") reconvenes for the first time since the election as elected [members](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_members_of_the_National_Assembly_of_Hungary_%282026%E2%80%932030%29 "List of members of the National Assembly of Hungary (2026–2030)") are sworn in. [Ágnes Forsthoffer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C3%81gnes_Forsthoffer "Ágnes Forsthoffer") is elected as [speaker](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_speakers_of_the_National_Assembly_%28Hungary%29 "List of speakers of the National Assembly (Hungary)"), while [Péter Magyar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/P%C3%A9ter_Magyar "Péter Magyar") is sworn in as [prime minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_Hungary "Prime Minister of Hungary"). [(AFP via France24)](https://www.france24.com/en/europe/20260509-magyar-set-to-become-hungary-s-new-prime-minister-in-historic-transition) [(*Budapest Business Journal*)](https://bbj.hu/politics/domestic/parliament/agnes-forsthoffer-elected-speaker-only-2nd-woman-to-hold-role/)
