#### Politics and elections

* [2026 Hungarian parliamentary election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Hungarian_parliamentary_election "2026 Hungarian parliamentary election")
  * The [National Assembly](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Assembly_%28Hungary%29 "National Assembly (Hungary)") of [Hungary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungary "Hungary") reconvenes for the first time since the election as elected [members](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_members_of_the_National_Assembly_of_Hungary_%282026%E2%80%932030%29 "List of members of the National Assembly of Hungary (2026–2030)") are sworn in. [Ágnes Forsthoffer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C3%81gnes_Forsthoffer "Ágnes Forsthoffer") is elected as [speaker](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_speakers_of_the_National_Assembly_%28Hungary%29 "List of speakers of the National Assembly (Hungary)"), while [Péter Magyar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/P%C3%A9ter_Magyar "Péter Magyar") is sworn in as [prime minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_Hungary "Prime Minister of Hungary").
