  * [Canada](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canada "Canada") and [China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China "China") reach a [trade agreement](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trade_agreement "Trade agreement") that reduces Canadian [tariffs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tariffs "Tariffs") on Chinese [electric vehicles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electric_vehicle "Electric vehicle") and Chinese tariffs on Canadian [canola](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canola "Canola") seeds, following a high-level [summit](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Summit_%28meeting%29 "Summit (meeting)") in [Beijing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beijing "Beijing"). * [Arrest of Yoon Suk Yeol](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arrest_of_Yoon_Suk_Yeol "Arrest of Yoon Suk Yeol")
  * A [high court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/High_courts_of_South_Korea "High courts of South Korea") in [Seoul](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seoul "Seoul"), [South Korea](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Korea "South Korea"), sentences former [president](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_South_Korea "President of South Korea") [Yoon Suk Yeol](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yoon_Suk_Yeol "Yoon Suk Yeol") to five years in [prison](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Korea_Correctional_Service "Korea Correctional Service") for [obstructing justice](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Perverting_the_course_of_justice "Perverting the course of justice") and excluding [cabinet members](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cabinet_of_Yoon_Suk_Yeol "Cabinet of Yoon Suk Yeol") during [his 2024 martial law declaration](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2024_South_Korean_martial_law_crisis "2024 South Korean martial law crisis"), while [acquitting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Acquittal "Acquittal") him of document [forgery](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Forgery "Forgery"). [(AFP via TRT World)](https://www.trtworld.com/article/9064201d57ea?utm_source=e059f50d6d80) [(AP)](https://apnews.com/article/south-korea-yoon-martial-law-arrest-893ccfbd6432af36028732563769393b)
