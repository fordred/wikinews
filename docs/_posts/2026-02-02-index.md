#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [Gaza War](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_War "Gaza War")
  * The Gaza Health Ministry said at least five people were killed in Israeli attacks throughout Gaza in the past 24 hours, increasing its count of the Palestinian death toll in Gaza to 71,800. Per Palestinian reports, two people were killed in a strike in Nuseirat and the remaining in Mawasi, Khan Yunis and Jabalia. Among those killed reportedly include a 3-year-old boy. The IDF said it targeted militants operating near the Yellow Line who approached its forces and "posed an imminent threat to them."
