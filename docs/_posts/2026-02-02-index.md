    * Clashes erupt between the [Rapid Support Forces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rapid_Support_Forces "Rapid Support Forces") (RSF) and a tribe formerly allied to it in [East Darfur](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/East_Darfur "East Darfur").
