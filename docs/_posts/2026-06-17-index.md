#### Politics and elections

* [Alan Peter Cayetano](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alan_Peter_Cayetano "Alan Peter Cayetano") concedes his [Senate presidency](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_the_Senate_of_the_Philippines "President of the Senate of the Philippines") an hour prior to a scheduled special session in the [Philippine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippines "Philippines") [Senate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Senate_of_the_Philippines "Senate of the Philippines") where [president pro tempore](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_pro_tempore_of_the_Senate_of_the_Philippines "President pro tempore of the Senate of the Philippines") [Sherwin Gatchalian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sherwin_Gatchalian "Sherwin Gatchalian") is elected for the same position in a 13–0 vote, ending [a leadership dispute](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/May_2026_President_of_the_Senate_of_the_Philippines_election#June_2026_leadership_crisis "May 2026 President of the Senate of the Philippines election") that began on June 3.
