#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [2026 Iran war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Iran_war "2026 Iran war")
  * [2025–2026 Iran–United States negotiations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025%E2%80%932026_Iran%E2%80%93United_States_negotiations "2025–2026 Iran–United States negotiations")
    * [Islamabad Accord](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamabad_Accord_%282026%29 "Islamabad Accord (2026)")
      * [Iranian foreign minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ministry_of_Foreign_Affairs_%28Iran%29 "Ministry of Foreign Affairs (Iran)") [Abbas Araghchi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abbas_Araghchi "Abbas Araghchi") confirms the two-week [ceasefire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ceasefire "Ceasefire") between the [United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States") and [Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran "Iran"), which includes reopening the [Strait of Hormuz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Strait_of_Hormuz_crisis "2026 Strait of Hormuz crisis"). The ceasefire is ratified by the [Supreme National Security Council](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_National_Security_Council "Supreme National Security Council"). [(Shafaq)](https://shafaq.com/en/Middle-East/Iran-agrees-to-ceasefire-offers-conditional-Hormuz-passage) [(*Khaleej Times*)](https://www.khaleejtimes.com/world/mena/iran-agrees-to-safe-passage-through-hormuz-strait-for-two-weeks-if-attacks-halted-2)
      * The [Supreme National Security Council](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_National_Security_Council "Supreme National Security Council") says that negotiations between the United States and Iran will start on Friday in [Islamabad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamabad "Islamabad"), [Pakistan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pakistan "Pakistan"). [(Middle East Eye)](https://www.middleeasteye.net/live-blog/live-blog-update/iran-says-talks-us-begin-friday-islamabad?topic=War%2520on%2520Iran&id=436636&fid=553792)
      * [Israeli prime minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_Israel "Prime Minister of Israel") [Benjamin Netanyahu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Benjamin_Netanyahu "Benjamin Netanyahu") announces on social media that the ceasefire does not include [Lebanon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lebanon "Lebanon"). [(Al Jazeera)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/4/8/netanyahu-says-us-iran-ceasefire-does-not-include-lebanon)
      * After the ceasefire took hold, [Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran "Iran") launches [ballistic missiles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ballistic_missile "Ballistic missile") at Israel, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, and the United Arab Emirates. [(CNBC News)](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/08/iran-missile-attacks-after-us-ceasefire-gulf-air-defenses.html)
  * [Saudi Arabia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saudi_Arabia "Saudi Arabia")'s East-West oil pipeline is hit in an [Iranian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Irani "Irani") attack. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/business/energy/saudi-arabias-east-west-oil-pipeline-hit-iranian-attack-damage-being-assessed-2026-04-08/)
  * Early in the morning, Iranian agencies report explosions from [Sirri Island](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sirri_Island "Sirri Island") and [Lavan Island](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lavan_Island "Lavan Island") of unknown origin. Iran accuses the [United Arab Emirates](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Arab_Emirates "United Arab Emirates") and [Kuwait](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kuwait "Kuwait") of the attacks and launches retaliatory attacks against those countries in response. [(Ynet)](https://www.ynetnews.com/article/1cje8v7cv) [(Wanaen)](https://wanaen.com/strikes-hit-lavan-refinery-iran-launches-retaliatory-attacks/)
  * [Thailand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thailand "Thailand") confirms the three crewmen on a vessel that was attacked in the [Strait of Hormuz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strait_of_Hormuz "Strait of Hormuz") in March have died. [(Al Jazeera)](https://aje.news/jf7llm?update=4473051)
