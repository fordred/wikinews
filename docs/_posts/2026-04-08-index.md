#### Armed conflicts and attacks

* [2026 Iran war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Iran_war "2026 Iran war")
  * [2025–2026 Iran–United States negotiations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025%E2%80%932026_Iran%E2%80%93United_States_negotiations "2025–2026 Iran–United States negotiations")
    * [Islamabad Accord](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamabad_Accord_%282026%29 "Islamabad Accord (2026)")
      * [Iranian foreign minister](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ministry_of_Foreign_Affairs_%28Iran%29 "Ministry of Foreign Affairs (Iran)") [Abbas Araghchi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abbas_Araghchi "Abbas Araghchi") confirms the two-week [ceasefire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ceasefire "Ceasefire") between the [United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States") and [Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran "Iran") and says that Iran will [allow](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Strait_of_Hormuz_crisis "2026 Strait of Hormuz crisis") a safe passage for ships crossing the [Strait of Hormuz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strait_of_Hormuz "Strait of Hormuz"). Iran also says that it will halt defensive operations if strikes against the country stop. The ceasefire is ratified by the [Supreme National Security Council](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_National_Security_Council "Supreme National Security Council"). [(Shafaq)](https://shafaq.com/en/Middle-East/Iran-agrees-to-ceasefire-offers-conditional-Hormuz-passage) [(*Khaleej Times*)](https://www.khaleejtimes.com/world/mena/iran-agrees-to-safe-passage-through-hormuz-strait-for-two-weeks-if-attacks-halted-2)
      * The [Supreme National Security Council](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_National_Security_Council "Supreme National Security Council") says that negotiations between the United States and Iran will start on Friday in [Islamabad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamabad "Islamabad"), [Pakistan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pakistan "Pakistan"). [(Middle East Eye)](https://www.middleeasteye.net/live-blog/live-blog-update/iran-says-talks-us-begin-friday-islamabad?topic=War%2520on%2520Iran&id=436636&fid=553792)
