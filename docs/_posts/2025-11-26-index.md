#### Disasters and accidents

* [2025 Tai Po apartment fire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Tai_Po_apartment_fire "2025 Tai Po apartment fire")
  * A fire at a housing estate in [Tai Po](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tai_Po "Tai Po"), [Hong Kong](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hong_Kong "Hong Kong"), kills at least 13 people and injures at least 16. [(*SCMP*)](https://www.scmp.com/news/hong-kong/society/article/3334217/major-fire-hong-kongs-tai-po-leaves-2-severely-burned-residents-trapped)
* The death toll from the [floods](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flood "Flood") in [southern](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southern_Thailand "Southern Thailand") [Thailand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thailand "Thailand") increases to 33 people. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/business/environment/thai-floods-death-toll-33-official-says-2025-11-26/)
* Eleven of the 13 people aboard a [UV Express](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/UV_Express "UV Express") van are killed in a collision with a [truck](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Truck "Truck") in [Camalig, Albay](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Camalig%2C_Albay "Camalig, Albay"), [Philippines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippines "Philippines"). [(*Manila Bulletin*)](https://mb.com.ph/2025/11/26/11-dead-in-uv-express-truck-collision-in-albay)

* A [LOT Polish Airlines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LOT_Polish_Airlines "LOT Polish Airlines") passenger aircraft veered off a taxiway during landing at [Vilnius Airport](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vilnius_Airport "Vilnius Airport") in [Lithuania](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lithuania "Lithuania"). No injuries were reported. The runway was closed until 5 p.m. local time. [(Reuters)](https://www.reuters.com/world/europe/polish-aircraft-veers-off-taxiway-vilnius-airport-2025-11-26/)

#### Law and crime

* [Murders of Yuna and Minu Jo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Murders_of_Yuna_and_Minu_Jo "Murders of Yuna and Minu Jo")
  * Justice [Geoffrey Venning](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geoffrey_Venning "Geoffrey Venning") of [New Zealand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Zealand "New Zealand")'s [High Court](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/High_Court_of_New_Zealand "High Court of New Zealand") sentences 45-year-old mother Hakyung Lee to [life imprisonment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Life_imprisonment_in_New_Zealand "Life imprisonment in New Zealand") with a minimum non-[parole](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Parole "Parole") period of 17 years after convicting Lee of [murdering her two children](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Child_murder "Child murder") in 2018. [(DW)](https://www.dw.com/en/new-zealand-suitcase-murders-mother-sentenced-to-prison-for-life/a-74899111)
